# Mutton Chops – Inženjerski profil i karakteristike

Mutton Chops su klasični, dramatic sideburn stil koji se proteže od linije kose niz obraze prema vilici, ali NE povezuje se preko brade. Ime dolazi od sličnosti sa oblikom ovčjeg kotleta (mutton chop). Ovaj viktorijanski stil je statement look koji zahtijeva značajnu facial hair gustinu na obrazima i spremnost da se ističe.

---

## Ključne dimenzije i proporcije

* **Vertikalna dužina**: Od linije kose do jawline
  → Pokriva cijeli obraz vertikalno
  → Tipično: 10–18 cm od vrha do dna
  → Prati prirodnu liniju rasta

* **Horizontalna širina** (na najširoj tački): 5–12 cm
  → Narrow chops: 5–7 cm
  → Standard chops: 7–10 cm
  → Wide chops: 10–12 cm
  → Širi prema dolje (flare out)

* **Donja granica**: Jawline ili blago ispod
  → Prati jawline curve
  → Može se spustiti 1–2 cm ispod jawline
  → Ends može biti square ili tapered

* **Gap na bradi**: OBAVEZAN
  → Čista brada (chin)
  → Razmak između lijevog i desnog chopa
  → Gap width: 6–12 cm (cijela brada)

* **Mustache**: NEMA ili odvojen
  → Tradicionalno: Clean upper lip
  → Varijanta: Separate mustache (ne connected)
  → Connected = Friendly Mutton Chops (drugi stil)

* **Oblik**: Flared/widening toward bottom
  → Uži kod uha
  → Širi kod jaw/chin area
  → "Lamb chop" silhouette

* **Gustina**: Kritična
  → Fill factor: >85% required
  → Patchy cheeks = problematično za ovaj stil

## Održavanje – Kontrolne tačke

* **Trim interval**:
  * Outer edge definition: Svakih 5–7 dana
  * Jawline edge: Svakih 5–7 dana
  * Cheek line (ako defined): Svakih 5–7 dana
  * Chin shave: Svakih 2–3 dana
  * Upper lip shave: Svakih 2–3 dana
  * Length trim: Svakih 10–14 dana
  * **Ukupno**: Srednje-visoko održavanje (3–4 sessiona sedmično)

* **Alati**:
  * Trimmer sa guards (10–20 mm) – za length
  * Precision trimmer (0 mm) – za edges
  * Razor – za chin i upper lip
  * Beard scissors – za shaping
  * Wide-tooth comb – za styling

* **Proizvodi**:
  * Beard oil: 4–6 kapi
  * Beard balm: Za shaping i hold
  * Aftershave: Za shaved areas
  * Styling balm: Optional za control

* **Vrijeme po sessiji**: 12–18 minuta
* **Sedmični time investment**: 45–65 minuta

## Razlike vs slični stilovi

| Karakteristika | Mutton Chops | Friendly Mutton Chops | Chin Curtain | Full Beard | Sideburns |
|----------------|--------------|----------------------|--------------|------------|-----------|
| **Cheek coverage** | Full | Full | Jawline only | Full | Partial |
| **Chin** | Clean | Bearded | Bearded | Bearded | Clean |
| **Mustache** | No/Separate | Connected | Optional | Integrated | No |
| **Connection** | No | Yes via mustache | Via chin | Full | No |
| **Width** | Wide (flared) | Wide | Thin | Variable | Thin-Medium |
| **Era** | Victorian | Civil War | Amish/Lincoln | Timeless | Modern |

### Ključne distinkcije:
* **Mutton Chops vs Friendly Mutton Chops**: Mutton Chops imaju clean chin i upper lip; Friendly ima connected mustache.
* **Mutton Chops vs Chin Curtain**: Mutton Chops su wide na cheeks; Chin Curtain je thin line along jawline.
* **Mutton Chops vs Sideburns**: Mutton Chops su much wider i extend lower.

---

## Dizajn pravila za autentičan Mutton Chops

1. **Chin MORA biti čist** – Connected chin = Friendly Mutton Chops ili beard. Gap is essential.

2. **Upper lip MORA biti čist** – Čak i separate mustache changes the look significantly.

3. **Flare je characteristic** – Mutton Chops se šire prema dolje. Uniform width nije authentic.

4. **Symmetry critical** – Both chops must be identical in size, shape, and position.

5. **Full cheek coverage** – Mutton Chops pokrivaju većinu obraza. Partial = just sideburns.

6. **Width matters** – Thin chops aren't really "mutton" – need substantial width.

7. **Clean edges** – Defined edges make the style intentional, not neglected.

8. **Era-appropriate boldness** – Ovo je dramatic, Victorian look. Own it.

**Best match**: Square, Oblong (90/100)
**Good**: Oval, Triangle (85/100)
**Moderate**: Diamond (80/100)

## Growth Timeline

**Od clean shaven do Mutton Chops:**

### Phase 1: Full Growth (Sedmica 1–6)
* **Sedmica 1–3**:
  → Expected growth: ~3–4 cm
  → Let full face grow
  → No shaping yet
* **Sedmica 4–6**:
  → Expected growth: +3–4 cm (total: ~7–8 cm)
  → Substantial growth
  → Ready for initial shaping

### Phase 2: Shaping (Sedmica 7–8)
* **Sedmica 7**:
  → INITIAL MUTTON CHOPS SHAPING:
  → Shave chin clean
  → Shave upper lip
  → Define outer edges
  → Create flared shape
* **Sedmica 8**:
  → Refine edges
  → Check symmetry
  → Establish shape

### Phase 3: Development & Maintenance (Sedmica 9+)
* **Sedmica 9–12**:
  → Continued growth za fuller chops
  → Maintenance routine established
* **Ongoing**:
  → Regular edge maintenance
  → Chin/lip shaving

**Total time to achieve**: 7–10 sedmica
**For full dramatic chops**: 12–16 sedmica

## Transition Paths

### 1. Clean Shaven → Mutton Chops
* **Duration**: 7–10 sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Medium
* **Key steps**:
  1. Sedmica 1–6: Grow full face
  2. Sedmica 7: Shave chin/lip, shape chops
  3. Sedmica 8+: Refine and maintain

### 2. Full Beard → Mutton Chops
* **Duration**: 1–2 sessiona
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Shave chin clean
  2. Shave upper lip
  3. Define chop edges
  4. Create flared shape
  5. Immediate maintenance

### 3. Sideburns → Mutton Chops
* **Duration**: 8–12 sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Medium
* **Steps**:
  1. Let sideburns grow wider
  2. Extend down to jawline
  3. Allow flare development
  4. Define edges when full

### 4. Mutton Chops → Friendly Mutton Chops
* **Duration**: 4–6 sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Grow mustache
  2. Connect mustache to chops
  3. Lower maintenance once connected

### 5. Mutton Chops → Full Beard
* **Duration**: 6–10 sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Stop shaving chin
  2. Stop shaving upper lip
  3. Let connect
  4. Full beard achieved
