# Friendly Mutton Chops – Inženjerski profil i karakteristike

Friendly Mutton Chops su varijanta klasičnih Mutton Chops-a koja uključuje mustache koji povezuje oba chopa preko gornje usne. "Friendly" dolazi od toga što povezani brkovi čine lice "prijateljskijim" nego strogi disconnected Mutton Chops. Ovaj stil je popularan u Civil War era Americi i ostao je iconic za taj period.

---

## Ključne dimenzije i proporcije

* **Vertikalna dužina chops-a**: Od linije kose do jawline
  → Pokriva cijeli obraz vertikalno
  → Tipično: 10–18 cm
  → Prati prirodnu liniju rasta

* **Horizontalna širina chops-a**: 5–12 cm
  → Sličan spread kao Mutton Chops
  → Flare prema dolje

* **Mustache**: OBAVEZAN, CONNECTED
  → Povezuje lijevi i desni chop
  → Full ili Chevron style
  → Integrisani sa chops – nema gap

* **Donja granica chops-a**: Jawline
  → Prati jawline curve
  → Može biti square ili rounded

* **Chin**: CLEAN
  → Gap na bradi (chin area)
  → Soul patch opcionalan
  → Razmak: 4–8 cm (ispod donje usne)

* **Oblik ukupni**: Connected U-shape
  → Chops + mustache formiraju kontinuiranu liniju
  → Otvoren na dnu (chin gap)

* **Gustina**: Kritična za chops i mustache
  → Fill factor: >85%
  → Thick mustache za solid connection

## Održavanje – Kontrolne tačke

* **Trim interval**:
  * Outer chop edges: Svakih 5–7 dana
  * Jawline edge: Svakih 5–7 dana
  * Mustache lip line: Svakih 3–5 dana
  * Chin shave: Svakih 2–3 dana
  * Length trim: Svakih 10–14 dana
  * **Ukupno**: Srednje održavanje (3 sessiona sedmično)

* **Alati**:
  * Trimmer sa guards (10–20 mm)
  * Precision trimmer (0 mm)
  * Razor – za chin
  * Beard scissors
  * Wide-tooth comb

* **Proizvodi**:
  * Beard oil: 5–8 kapi (larger coverage)
  * Beard balm: Za shaping
  * Aftershave: Za chin area

* **Vrijeme po sessiji**: 10–15 minuta
* **Sedmični time investment**: 35–50 minuta

## Razlike vs slični stilovi

| Karakteristika | Friendly Mutton Chops | Mutton Chops | Chin Curtain | Full Beard |
|----------------|----------------------|--------------|--------------|------------|
| **Mustache** | Connected | None/Separate | Optional | Integrated |
| **Chin** | Clean | Clean | Bearded | Bearded |
| **Chops** | Wide, flared | Wide, flared | Narrow line | Integrated |
| **Connection** | Via mustache | None | Via chin | Full |
| **Vibe** | Civil War | Victorian | Amish/Lincoln | Classic |

### Ključne distinkcije:
* **Friendly vs Regular Mutton Chops**: Friendly ima connected mustache; Regular ima clean upper lip.
* **Friendly Mutton Chops vs Full Beard**: Friendly ima clean chin; Full Beard connects everywhere.
* **Friendly vs Chin Curtain**: Friendly ima wide chops; Chin Curtain je thin line.

---

## Dizajn pravila za autentičan Friendly Mutton Chops

1. **Mustache MORA biti connected** – Ovo je "friendly" element. Disconnected = Regular Mutton Chops.

2. **Chin MORA biti čist** – Connected chin = Full Beard territory.

3. **Chops moraju biti substantial** – Thin chops + mustache = Horseshoe area, ne Friendly Mutton Chops.

4. **Flare characteristic preserved** – Chops still widen toward bottom.

5. **Seamless connection** – Mustache should blend seamlessly into chops.

6. **Symmetry essential** – Both chops identical.

7. **Mustache style integrated** – Mustache should match chop density/style.

8. **Chin gap defined** – Clear, clean chin area below lower lip.

## Growth Timeline

**Od clean shaven do Friendly Mutton Chops:**

### Phase 1: Full Growth (Sedmica 1–6)
* **Sedmica 1–3**:
  → Expected growth: ~3–4 cm
  → Grow full face including mustache
* **Sedmica 4–6**:
  → Expected growth: +3–4 cm (total: ~7–8 cm)
  → Substantial chops i mustache

### Phase 2: Shaping (Sedmica 7–8)
* **Sedmica 7**:
  → INITIAL SHAPING:
  → Shave chin clean
  → Keep mustache connected to chops
  → Define outer edges
  → Create flared shape on chops
* **Sedmica 8**:
  → Refine connection zone
  → Perfect symmetry
  → Establish chin gap

### Phase 3: Maintenance (Sedmica 9+)
* Regular edge maintenance
* Chin shaving ongoing
* Growth za fuller look

**Total time to achieve**: 7–10 sedmica
**For dramatic Friendly Mutton Chops**: 12–16 sedmica

## Transition Paths

### 1. Clean Shaven → Friendly Mutton Chops
* **Duration**: 7–10 sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Medium
* **Key steps**:
  1. Sedmica 1–6: Grow full face
  2. Sedmica 7: Shave chin only, keep mustache connected
  3. Sedmica 8+: Refine and maintain

### 2. Mutton Chops → Friendly Mutton Chops
* **Duration**: 4–6 sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Grow mustache
  2. Connect to chops
  3. Same chop maintenance
  4. Slightly lower overall maintenance

### 3. Full Beard → Friendly Mutton Chops
* **Duration**: 1 session
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Shave chin only
  2. Keep mustache and cheek areas
  3. Define chop edges
  4. Create flared shape

### 4. Friendly Mutton Chops → Full Beard
* **Duration**: 6–8 sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Stop shaving chin
  2. Let connect
  3. Full beard develops

### 5. Friendly Mutton Chops → Mutton Chops
* **Duration**: 1 session
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Shave mustache
  2. Keep chops
  3. Regular Mutton Chops achieved
