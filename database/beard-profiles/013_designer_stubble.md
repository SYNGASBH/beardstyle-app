# Designer Stubble – Inženjerski profil i karakteristike

Designer Stubble (poznata i kao "3-day stubble" ili "groomed stubble") je pažljivo održavana facial hair opcija koja balansira između clean-shaven i full beard. Ovo je "controlled chaos" pristup – izgleda kao da se niste obrijali 2-3 dana, ali je zapravo pažljivo održavana dužina. Designer Stubble je jedan od najpopularnijih facial hair stilova jer pruža muževan izgled bez commitment-a brade.

---

## Ključne dimenzije i proporcije

* **Dužina stubble-a**: 2–4 mm
  → Optimalno: 2.5–3.5 mm (classic "3-day look")
  → <2 mm: 5 o'clock shadow territory
  → >4 mm: Heavy stubble territory

* **Coverage**: Full face ili selective
  → Full face: Jednaka dužina svuda
  → Selective: Defined cheek line, neckline, ali stubble tekstura

* **Uniformnost**: Kritična za "designer" quality
  → Varijacija: ±0.5 mm maksimum
  → Patchy areas: Manje vidljive nego kod full beard
  → Consistent density izgleda intentional

* **Cheek line**: Može biti defined ili natural
  → Option A: Natural growth, nema linije (casual look)
  → Option B: Soft defined line (groomed look)
  → Option C: Hard defined line (precision look)

* **Neckline**: Obično defined
  → Pozicija: Na ili 1 prst iznad Adam's apple
  → Clean shaved ispod za neat appearance
  → Može biti natural za very casual look

* **Mustache area**: Included, ista dužina
  → Lip clearance: Stubble ne dodiruje usnu
  → Ista tekstura kao ostatak lica

* **Projekcija volumena**: +2–5% (minimal)
  → Shadow effect više nego volume
  → Adds texture bez significant projection

* **Tekstura**:
  → Fill factor: 75%+ acceptable (patchy OK at this length)
  → Stray hairs: Less visible at stubble length
  → Any texture works – stubble equalizes differences

---

## Održavanje – Kontrolne tačke

* **Trim interval**:
  * Full stubble trim: Svakih 2–3 dana (maintain target length)
  * Neckline: Svakih 2–3 dana
  * Cheek line (ako defined): Svakih 2–3 dana
  * **Ukupno**: Srednje održavanje (3–4 sessiona sedmično)

* **Alati**:
  * Stubble trimmer sa preciznim settings (0.5 mm increments)
  * Guard 2–3 mm – primary setting
  * Precision trimmer (0 mm) – za linije
  * Razor – za neckline cleanup

* **Proizvodi**:
  * Stubble oil/moisturizer: Za softness (optional)
  * Aftershave balm: Za neckline area
  * Exfoliating scrub: 2× sedmično za ingrown prevention

* **Vrijeme po sessiji**: 5–8 minuta
* **Sedmični time investment**: 18–25 minuta

## Razlike vs slični stilovi

| Karakteristika | Designer Stubble | 5 O'Clock Shadow | Heavy Stubble | Short Beard | Clean Shaven |
|----------------|------------------|------------------|---------------|-------------|--------------|
| **Dužina** | 2–4 mm | 0.5–2 mm | 4–6 mm | 6–10 mm | 0 mm |
| **Trim frequency** | 2–3 dana | 1–2 dana | 3–4 dana | 5–7 dana | Daily |
| **Visibility** | Obvious stubble | Shadow hint | Near-beard | Short beard | None |
| **Lines defined** | Optional | No | Often | Usually | N/A |
| **Maintenance** | Medium | Medium-High | Medium | Medium | High |
| **Overall vibe** | Rugged groomed | Hint of stubble | Almost beard | Short beard | Smooth |

### Ključne distinkcije:
* **Designer Stubble vs 5 O'Clock Shadow**: Designer Stubble je duži (2–4 mm vs 0.5–2 mm), more visible, more intentional.
* **Designer Stubble vs Heavy Stubble**: Designer Stubble je kraći, čistiji, requires more frequent maintenance.
* **Designer Stubble vs Short Beard**: Short beard ima više volume (6+ mm), Designer Stubble ostaje u stubble kategoriji.

## Dizajn pravila za autentičan Designer Stubble

1. **Uniformna dužina je ključ** – "Designer" u imenu znači intentional. Random dužine = messy, ne designer.

2. **Trim regularly, not occasionally** – Puštanje stubble-a da preraste u beard, pa trimanje nazad nije designer stubble – to je neglect.

3. **Neckline čini razliku** – Defined neckline pretvara "didn't shave" u "chose stubble". Critical distinction.

4. **2–4 mm sweet spot** – Ispod 2 mm je shadow; iznad 4 mm je heavy stubble. Stay in range.

5. **Patchy is OK at this length** – Stubble length masks patchiness bolje nego beard. Don't over-stress.

6. **Cheek line optional ali helpful** – Defined cheek line adds polish. Natural works za casual.

7. **Consistent texture** – No random longer hairs. Trim everything to same length.

8. **Skin care matters** – Stubble exposes skin. Moisturize, exfoliate, prevent ingrowns.

## Growth Timeline

**Od clean shaven do Designer Stubble target (3 mm):**

### Phase 1: Initial Growth (Dan 1–3)
* **Dan 1**:
  → Expected growth: 0.3–0.5 mm
  → 5 o'clock shadow beginning
  → Just let it grow
* **Dan 2**:
  → Expected growth: +0.3–0.5 mm (total: ~1 mm)
  → Visible shadow
  → Still growing
* **Dan 3**:
  → Expected growth: +0.5 mm (total: ~1.5–2 mm)
  → Approaching target
  → Can start defining neckline

### Phase 2: Target Achievement (Dan 4–5)
* **Dan 4–5**:
  → Total growth: 2–3 mm
  → Target length achieved
  → Define neckline
  → Optional: Define cheek line
  → First maintenance trim

### Phase 3: Maintenance (Dan 6+)
* Steady state
* Trim to target length svakih 2–3 dana
* Maintain lines svakih 2–3 dana

**Total time to achieve**: 3–5 dana
**Minimum growth required**: 2 mm

## Transition Paths

### 1. Clean Shaven → Designer Stubble
* **Duration**: 3–5 dana
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Key steps**:
  1. Dan 1–3: Let it grow
  2. Dan 4–5: First trim to target length, define lines
  3. Ongoing: Maintain every 2–3 days

### 2. Heavy Stubble → Designer Stubble
* **Duration**: 1 session
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Trim from 4–6 mm to 2–4 mm
  2. Refine lines if needed
  3. Same maintenance schedule

### 3. Short Beard → Designer Stubble
* **Duration**: 1 session
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Trim from 6+ mm to 2–4 mm
  2. Significant length reduction
  3. May need line refinement
  4. Different (more frequent) maintenance

### 4. Designer Stubble → Heavy Stubble
* **Duration**: 2–4 dana
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Let grow from 3 mm to 5 mm
  2. Maintain lines
  3. Less frequent trimming after

### 5. Designer Stubble → Short Beard
* **Duration**: 2–3 sedmice
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Let grow to 6+ mm
  2. Maintain lines during growth
  3. Shape when target reached
  4. Transition to beard maintenance

### 6. Designer Stubble → Clean Shaven
* **Duration**: 1 session
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Shave completely
  2. Return to daily shaving routine
