# Walrus Mustache – Inženjerski profil i karakteristike

Walrus Mustache je massive, bushy mustache stil koji potpuno prekriva gornju usnu i često se proteže ispod nje. Nazvan po sličnosti sa moržem (walrus) koji ima slične whiskers, ovaj stil je epitome mustache maximalism – veći, gušći, i više neglected-looking nego bilo koji drugi mustache stil. Walrus zahtijeva minimalno styling ali maksimalnu gustinu i strpljenje za rast.

---

## Ključne dimenzije i proporcije

* **Horizontalna širina**: Prelazi uglove usana
  → Tipično: 10–15 cm
  → Može se proširiti do obraza
  → Natural flare, ne styled

* **Vertikalna dužina**: 15–30+ mm
  → Od nosne baze do ispod gornje usne
  → Prekriva usnu potpuno
  → Može "drape" preko usne 5–15+ mm

* **Lip coverage**: POTPUNO
  → Gornja usna nije vidljiva
  → Mustache visi preko usne
  → Jelo i piće zahtijevaju adjustment

* **Oblik**: Natural, bushy, drooping
  → Nema defined shape
  → Gravity dictates direction
  → Wild, untamed aesthetic

* **Krajevi**: Natural, not styled
  → Može biti blago trimmed za neatness
  → No wax styling
  → Natural droop ili slight outward

* **Gustina**: MAXIMUM
  → Fill factor: >95% required
  → Thick, dense growth essential
  → Sparse growth doesn't work za walrus

* **Povezanost sa bradom**: Traditionally none
  → Clean face highlights walrus impact
  → Some combine with beard (less traditional)

## Održavanje – Kontrolne tačke

* **Trim interval**:
  * Shape trim: Svakih 2–4 sedmice (minimal)
  * Nostril clearance: Svakih 1–2 sedmice
  * Face shave: Svakih 1–3 dana
  * **Ukupno**: Nisko održavanje (1–2 sessiona sedmično)

* **Alati**:
  * Beard scissors – primary za occasional trim
  * Wide-tooth comb – za detangling
  * Trimmer – za nostril clearance
  * Razor – za face

* **Proizvodi**:
  * Beard/mustache oil: 4–6 kapi (heavy coverage needs more)
  * Beard balm: Za conditioning
  * No styling products needed

* **Vrijeme po sessiji**: 5–10 minuta
* **Sedmični time investment**: 15–25 minuta

## Razlike vs slični stilovi

| Karakteristika | Walrus | Chevron | Handlebar | Horseshoe | Hungarian |
|----------------|--------|---------|-----------|-----------|-----------|
| **Size** | Massive | Thick | Medium | Large | Massive |
| **Lip coverage** | Total | Partial/Full | Minimal | Partial | Heavy |
| **Styling** | None | None | Wax | Minimal | None |
| **Direction** | Down/drape | Down | Out | Down | Out/Down |
| **Extensions** | No | No | Out | Vertical | Out |
| **Trimming** | Minimal | Regular | Regular | Regular | Minimal |
| **Vibe** | Old frontier | Classic | Victorian | Biker | Eastern Euro |

### Ključne distinkcije:
* **Walrus vs Chevron**: Walrus je larger, covers lip completely, less trimmed.
* **Walrus vs Handlebar**: Walrus nema styled ends, just drapes naturally.
* **Walrus vs Hungarian**: Similar size, ali Hungarian ima more outward direction.
* **Walrus vs Horseshoe**: Walrus nema vertical extensions.

---

## Dizajn pravila za autentičan Walrus

1. **Size is non-negotiable** – If it doesn't completely cover the lip, it's not Walrus.

2. **No styling** – Walrus je natural, bushy. Any wax styling = different style.

3. **Drape, don't shape** – Let gravity do the work. Natural hang is the aesthetic.

4. **Minimal trimming** – Only trim za functional reasons (nostrils, eating). Not za shaping.

5. **Density required** – Sparse mustache can't be Walrus. Need thick, full growth.

6. **Face clean** – Traditional Walrus is standalone. Clean face highlights impact.

7. **Patience essential** – Takes months to develop true Walrus size.

8. **Eating adaptation required** – Must learn to eat/drink with covered lip.

## Growth Timeline

**Od clean shaven do Walrus:**

### Phase 1: Foundation (Mjesec 1–3)
* **Mjesec 1**:
  → Expected growth: ~1.2 cm
  → Basic thick mustache
  → Too short za Walrus
* **Mjesec 2**:
  → Expected growth: +1.2 cm (total: ~2.4 cm)
  → Starting to cover lip
  → Getting substantial
* **Mjesec 3**:
  → Expected growth: +1.2 cm (total: ~3.6 cm)
  → Significant lip coverage
  → Approaching Walrus territory

### Phase 2: Walrus Development (Mjesec 4–6)
* **Mjesec 4**:
  → Total: ~4.5–5 cm
  → True lip coverage achieved
  → Basic Walrus possible
* **Mjesec 5–6**:
  → Total: ~6–7 cm
  → Full Walrus development
  → Significant drape

### Phase 3: Maximum Walrus (Mjesec 7+)
* **Mjesec 7–12**:
  → Maximum natural length
  → Epic Walrus achieved
  → Terminal length approaches
* **Ongoing**:
  → Maintenance mode
  → Minimal trimming

**Total time to achieve**: 4–6 mjeseci
**Total time for maximum Walrus**: 8–12 mjeseci

## Transition Paths

### 1. Clean Shaven → Walrus
* **Duration**: 4–6 mjeseci
* **Difficulty**: Easy (just patience)
* **Key steps**:
  1. Mjesec 1–3: Grow, don't trim
  2. Mjesec 4–6: Continue, minimal intervention
  3. Ongoing: Maintenance only

### 2. Chevron → Walrus
* **Duration**: 2–4 mjeseci
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Stop trimming lip line
  2. Let drape over lip
  3. Allow natural bushy growth
  4. Minimal maintenance

### 3. Full Beard → Walrus
* **Duration**: 0–3 mjeseci
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Shave beard, keep mustache
  2. If mustache already long: Walrus ready
  3. If not: let grow without trimming
  4. Stop all lip line trimming

### 4. Walrus → Chevron
* **Duration**: 1 session
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Trim lip line clear
  2. Shape to Chevron dimensions
  3. Regular maintenance begins
  4. Significant reduction

### 5. Walrus → Handlebar
* **Duration**: Immediate ili weeks
* **Difficulty**: Medium
* **Steps**:
  1. Trim center if needed
  2. Train ends outward
  3. Begin wax styling
  4. Fundamentally different approach

### 6. Walrus → Full Beard with Walrus
* **Duration**: 8–12 sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Stop shaving face
  2. Grow beard
  3. Maintain Walrus mustache
  4. Combined look achieved

## Practical Considerations

### Eating & Drinking:
* Straws become essential za drinks
* Napkins constantly needed
* Soup is challenging
* Must learn to "part" mustache za eating
* Food particles common – regular checks

### Social Awareness:
* Mustache in food is visible to others
* Requires adjustment to eating style
* Kissing logistics change
* Partner accommodation needed

### Maintenance Reality:
* Washing: Must clean food/drink residue
* Drying: Mustache stays wet after drinking
* Sleeping: May get compressed
