# Beardstache – Inženjerski profil i karakteristike

Beardstache je hybrid stil koji kombinuje prominent mustache sa light stubble na ostatku lica. Ime dolazi od spoja "beard" i "mustache" – ali fokus je jasno na brkovima dok stubble služi kao subtle accent. Ovaj stil je gained popularity u posljednjoj deceniji kao alternativa kako full beards tako i standalone mustache stilovima, nudeći unique visual contrast između dominant brkova i minimalne brade.

---

## Ključne dimenzije i proporcije

* **Mustache dužina**: 8–20+ mm
  → Short beardstache: 8–12 mm mustache
  → Medium beardstache: 12–18 mm mustache
  → Full beardstache: 18+ mm mustache
  → Optimalno: 12–15 mm za balanced look

* **Stubble dužina** (face minus mustache): 2–5 mm
  → Optimalno: 3–4 mm
  → <2 mm: Mustache looks isolated
  → >5 mm: Begins to compete with mustache

* **Contrast ratio** (mustache : stubble): 3:1 ili više
  → Key characteristic: Mustache mora biti significantly longer
  → Example: 12 mm mustache with 4 mm stubble = 3:1
  → Higher contrast = more dramatic beardstache

* **Mustache stil**: Various options
  → Natural full: Over lip, sides trimmed
  → Chevron: Thick, follows lip line
  → Horseshoe: Extends past corners
  → Handlebar: Styled outward
  → All work za beardstache

* **Cheek line**: Usually natural na stubble
  → Stubble portion: Natural ili soft defined
  → Hard cheek line on stubble section less common

* **Neckline**: Defined na stubble
  → Standard neckline definition
  → 1–2 prsta iznad Adam's apple

* **Mustache/lip relationship**:
  → Lip coverage: Variable by mustache style
  → From just touching to significant coverage
  → Personal preference

* **Projekcija volumena**:
  → Stubble: +3–5% (minimal)
  → Mustache: +15–30% (significant)
  → Contrast creates visual interest

## Održavanje – Kontrolne tačke

* **Trim interval**:
  * Stubble: Svakih 3–4 dana (maintain short)
  * Mustache length: Svakih 7–14 dana
  * Mustache shape: Svakih 3–5 dana
  * Neckline: Svakih 3–4 dana
  * **Ukupno**: Srednje-visoko održavanje (3–4 sessiona sedmično)

* **Alati**:
  * Stubble trimmer sa 3–4 mm guard – za face
  * Precision trimmer – za mustache edges
  * Mustache scissors – za shaping
  * Mustache comb – za styling
  * Razor – za neckline

* **Proizvodi**:
  * Mustache wax: Za styling i hold
  * Light beard oil: 2–3 kapi za both areas
  * Stubble softener: Optional
  * Aftershave: Za neckline area

* **Vrijeme po sessiji**: 10–15 minuta
* **Sedmični time investment**: 35–55 minuta

## Razlike vs slični stilovi

| Karakteristika | Beardstache | Chevron Mustache | Full Beard | Designer Stubble | Handlebar |
|----------------|-------------|------------------|------------|------------------|-----------|
| **Mustache** | Prominent | Prominent | Integrated | Same as face | Styled |
| **Face hair** | Light stubble | None | Full | Uniform stubble | None |
| **Contrast** | High | Maximum | Low/None | None | Maximum |
| **Focus** | Mustache + accent | Mustache only | Whole beard | Even coverage | Mustache only |
| **Maintenance** | Medium-High | Medium | Variable | Medium | High |
| **Overall vibe** | Hybrid statement | Classic stache | Full coverage | Rugged uniform | Artistic stache |

### Ključne distinkcije:
* **Beardstache vs Full Mustache**: Beardstache ima stubble accent; pure mustache ima clean face.
* **Beardstache vs Full Beard**: Beardstache ima dominant mustache; full beard ima integrated mustache.
* **Beardstache vs Designer Stubble**: Beardstache ima prominent mustache; stubble ima uniform length everywhere.

---

## Dizajn pravila za autentičan Beardstache

1. **Mustache must dominate** – If stubble and mustache are similar length, it's not beardstache. 3:1 ratio minimum.

2. **Stubble is accent, not feature** – Stubble exists to frame mustache, not compete. Keep it short.

3. **Maintain contrast** – Regular stubble trimming essential to maintain contrast ratio.

4. **Mustache styling matters** – Beardstache mustache benefits from shaping, wax, styling.

5. **Transition zone clean** – Area between mustache and stubble should be clean, not graduated.

6. **Neckline on stubble portion** – Define neckline normally despite short length.

7. **Mustache lip line is visible** – Unlike full beard, mustache lip line shows. Keep groomed.

8. **Embrace the contrast** – Don't try to blend. Sharp contrast is the aesthetic.

## Growth Timeline

**Od clean shaven do Beardstache target (12 mm mustache / 4 mm stubble):**

### Phase 1: Full Growth (Sedmica 1–3)
* **Sedmica 1**:
  → Expected growth: ~3 mm everywhere
  → Let everything grow
  → Stubble developing
* **Sedmica 2**:
  → Expected growth: +3 mm (total: ~6 mm)
  → Still growing uniformly
  → Approaching target mustache length
* **Sedmica 3**:
  → Total growth: ~9–10 mm
  → Mustache approaching target

### Phase 2: Differentiation (Sedmica 4)
* **Sedmica 4**:
  → Mustache: ~12 mm
  → FIRST BEARDSTACHE SHAPING:
  → Trim face back to 4 mm
  → Keep mustache long
  → Create contrast
  → Define neckline

### Phase 3: Maintenance (Sedmica 5+)
* Steady state
* Stubble trim svakih 3–4 dana to maintain 4 mm
* Mustache trim svakih 1–2 sedmice
* Mustache shaping svakih 3–5 dana

**Total time to achieve**: 4 sedmice (1 mjesec)
**Minimum mustache growth required**: 10 mm

## Transition Paths

### 1. Clean Shaven → Beardstache
* **Duration**: 4 sedmice
* **Difficulty**: Medium
* **Key steps**:
  1. Sedmica 1–3: Grow full face
  2. Sedmica 4: Trim face to stubble, keep mustache
  3. Ongoing: Maintain contrast ratio

### 2. Full Beard → Beardstache
* **Duration**: 1 session
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Trim beard down to 4 mm
  2. Leave mustache at current length
  3. Instant beardstache if mustache was 10+ mm

### 3. Designer Stubble → Beardstache
* **Duration**: 3–4 sedmice
* **Difficulty**: Medium
* **Steps**:
  1. Maintain face at stubble length
  2. Let only mustache grow
  3. At 10+ mm mustache: beardstache achieved

### 4. Full Mustache → Beardstache
* **Duration**: 1 sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Let face grow stubble (5–7 dana)
  2. Maintain at 3–4 mm
  3. Keep mustache as-is
  4. Beardstache achieved

### 5. Beardstache → Full Beard
* **Duration**: 6–10 sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Stop trimming stubble
  2. Let face catch up to mustache
  3. At equal lengths: full beard
  4. Shape as desired

### 6. Beardstache → Full Mustache Only
* **Duration**: 1 session
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Shave stubble clean
  2. Keep mustache
  3. Classic mustache achieved

## Mustache Styles for Beardstache

### 1. Natural Full
* Over lip coverage
* Minimal styling
* Most casual beardstache

### 2. Chevron
* Thick, follows lip line
* Classic look
* Medium maintenance

### 3. Horseshoe
* Extends down past mouth corners
* Bold statement
* Higher maintenance

### 4. Handlebar
* Styled outward
* Wax required
* Maximum statement

### 5. English
* Narrow, styled outward
* Very refined
* High maintenance
