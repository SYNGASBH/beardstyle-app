# Anchor Beard – Inženjerski profil i karakteristike

Anchor Beard je precision-styled beard koji kombinuje pointed chin beard sa thin mustache linijom, formirajući oblik koji podsjeća na brodsko sidro (anchor). Karakterističan je po svojim oštrim, definisanim linijama i thin connecting extensions koje se protežu od goatee-a duž jawline-a prema ušima. Ovaj stil zahtijeva izuzetnu preciznost i redovno održavanje – jedan od najzahtjevnijih stilova za postići i održavati.

---

## Ključne dimenzije i proporcije

* **Dužina chin bearda** (od donje usne do najniže tačke): 3–7 cm
  → Optimalno: 4–5 cm za proporcionalan anchor look
  → <3 cm: Nedovoljno za defined point
  → >7 cm: Počinje gubiti anchor proporcije

* **Chin beard shape**: Pointed, inverted triangle
  → Point angle: 30–45°
  → Mora biti oštar, defined point
  → Centered na midline lica

* **Jawline extensions**: Thin lines duž jawline
  → Širina extensions: 0.5–1.5 cm (THIN!)
  → Dužina: Od chin beard do mid-cheek ili dalje
  → Moraju biti IDENTIČNE na obje strane
  → Prate natural jawline curve

* **Cheek area**: POTPUNO ČISTA iznad extensions
  → **Nema dlaka iznad jawline extension**
  → Extensions su jedini element na jaw area
  → Clean shave above extensions critical

* **Soul patch area**: Connected sa chin beard
  → Širina: 1–2 cm
  → Forms "stem" of anchor shape
  → Connected vertically to chin point

* **Brkovi (Mustache)**: Thin line, odvojeni
  → Širina: 0.3–1 cm (THIN pencil mustache)
  → **Gap od chin beard**: 0.5–1.5 cm
  → Style: Pencil ili thin natural
  → Nema full mustache – to bi narušilo anchor shape

* **Neckline**: Clean, defined
  → Shape: Prati natural jawline
  → Clean shaved ispod

* **Projekcija volumena**: +10–20% donjeg lica
  → Moderate, focused enhancement
  → Depth: 2–4 cm na chin point

* **Tekstura i gustina**:
  → Fill factor: >95% required za clean lines
  → Stray hairs: ZERO tolerance – narušavaju precision
  → Preferred texture: Straight (curly je problematičan za thin lines)

## Održavanje – Kontrolne tačke

* **Trim interval**:
  * Chin point: Svakih 5–7 dana
  * Jawline extensions: Svakih 3–5 dana (CRITICAL)
  * Pencil mustache: Svakih 2–3 dana
  * Cheek shave: Svakih 1–2 dana (CRITICAL)
  * Neckline: Svakih 3–5 dana
  * Mustache-chin gap: Svakih 2–3 dana
  * **Ukupno**: Vrlo visoko održavanje (5–6 sessiona sedmično)

* **Alati**:
  * Precision trimmer (0 mm blade) – ESSENTIAL za thin lines
  * Detail trimmer sa ultra-thin head
  * Straight razor ili safety razor – za cheek precision
  * Fine scissors – za point definition
  * Magnifying mirror – za symmetry check
  * Stencil/template (optional) – za consistent extensions

* **Proizvodi**:
  * Beard oil: 1–2 kapi dnevno (minimal)
  * Shaving cream/gel: Za frequent cheek shaving
  * Aftershave balm: Za soothed skin
  * Precision edge gel: Za defined lines
  * Mustache wax (minimal): Za thin mustache control

* **Vrijeme po sessiji**: 15–25 minuta
* **Sedmični time investment**: 70–100 minuta

## Razlike vs slični stilovi

| Karakteristika | Anchor | Van Dyke | Goatee | Chin Strap | Balbo |
|----------------|--------|----------|--------|------------|-------|
| **Chin beard** | Pointed | Pointed | Variable | No | Wide rounded |
| **Jawline extensions** | Yes (thin) | No | No | Full chin strap | No |
| **Mustache** | Thin pencil | Styled separated | Optional | No | Separated full |
| **Gap mustache-chin** | Yes | Yes | Variable | N/A | Yes |
| **Cheeks** | Clean | Clean | Clean | Connected | Clean |
| **Precision required** | Very High | High | Medium | High | High |
| **Maintenance** | Very High | High | Medium | High | High |
| **Overall vibe** | Nautical precise | Artistic | Minimalist | Defined jaw | Distinguished |

### Ključne distinkcije:
* **Anchor vs Van Dyke**: Anchor ima thin jawline extensions; Van Dyke nema extensions.
* **Anchor vs Chin Strap**: Anchor ima pointed chin beard + thin extensions; Chin Strap je thin line bez chin volume.
* **Anchor vs Goatee**: Anchor ima extensions i thin mustache; Goatee je simpler bez extensions.
* **Anchor vs Balbo**: Anchor ima thin extensions i pointed chin; Balbo ima wide coverage bez extensions.

## Dizajn pravila za autentičan Anchor

1. **Extensions moraju biti THIN** – Širina 0.5–1.5 cm maksimum. Šire od toga = nije anchor, to je extended beard.

2. **Point mora biti oštro defined** – Blunt ili rounded point nije anchor. Sharp, centered point je obavezan.

3. **Extensions moraju biti IDENTIČNE** – Asimetrične extensions su najčešća greška. Koristi mjerne alate.

4. **Mustache mora biti thin pencil style** – Full mustache narušava anchor estetiku. Thin line only.

5. **Gap između mustache i chin je obavezan** – Connected = nije anchor, to je circle beard varijanta.

6. **Cheeks iznad extensions moraju biti ČISTI** – Jedan stray hair uništava precision look.

7. **Extensions prate jawline** – Ne custom shapes – moraju pratiti natural jaw curve.

8. **Daily maintenance je non-negotiable** – Anchor gubi definition nakon 2 dana bez održavanja.

9. **Simetrija je KRITIČNA** – Ovo je precision stil – asimetrija je neprihvatljiva.

## Face Shape Compatibility

| Oblik lica | Score | Obrazloženje |
|------------|-------|--------------|
| **Oval** | 85/100 | Dobro. Extensions frame balanced face nicely. |
| **Square** | 95/100 | Idealno. Extensions emphasize strong jawline. Best match. |
| **Round** | 80/100 | Radi. Extensions add definition, point elongates. |
| **Triangle** | 70/100 | Oprez. Extensions emphasize already wide jaw. Consider alternatives. |
| **Diamond** | 90/100 | Odličan. Extensions balance cheekbones, point adds chin. |
| **Oblong** | 65/100 | Risky. Pointed chin adds more length. Extensions OK but point problematic. |

**Best match**: Square (95/100)
**Excellent**: Diamond (90/100)
**Good**: Oval (85/100)
**Moderate**: Round (80/100)
**Careful**: Triangle, Oblong (65–70/100)

## Growth Timeline

**Od clean shaven do Anchor target (5 cm chin + extensions):**

### Phase 1: Full Growth (Sedmica 1–4)
* **Sedmica 1–2**:
  → Expected growth: ~2 cm
  → Grow entire face – NO shaping yet
  → Planning phase – study your jawline
* **Sedmica 3–4**:
  → Expected growth: +2 cm (total: ~4 cm)
  → Continue full growth
  → Approaching minimum length

### Phase 2: Initial Anchor Shaping (Sedmica 5–6)
* **Sedmica 5**:
  → Expected growth: ~5 cm on chin
  → MAJOR SHAPING SESSION:
  → Define pointed chin beard
  → Shave cheeks except thin extensions
  → Create thin pencil mustache
  → Create gap between mustache and chin
* **Sedmica 6**:
  → Refine extensions (very thin!)
  → Perfect point
  → Check symmetry obsessively
  → Establish pencil mustache width

### Phase 3: Precision Maintenance (Sedmica 7+)
* Daily touch-ups become routine
* Extensions require constant attention
* Point maintenance svakih 5–7 dana
* Cheek shaving almost daily
* Mustache-chin gap maintenance

**Total time to achieve**: 5–6 sedmica (1.25–1.5 mjeseca)
**Minimum growth required**: 4 cm chin (≈ 3–4 sedmice pre-shaping)
**Skill development**: 2–4 additional weeks to master maintenance

## Transition Paths

### 1. Clean Shaven → Anchor
* **Duration**: 5–6 sedmica + 2–4 sedmice skill development
* **Difficulty**: Hard
* **Key steps**:
  1. Sedmica 1–4: Grow full beard, no shaping
  2. Sedmica 5: Major shaping – form anchor shape
  3. Sedmica 6: Refine thin extensions, point
  4. Sedmica 7–8: Learn maintenance precision
  5. Ongoing: High-maintenance lifestyle

### 2. Full Beard → Anchor
* **Duration**: 2–3 sessiona (major reshaping)
* **Difficulty**: Medium-Hard
* **Steps**:
  1. Session 1: Shave cheeks, form basic chin shape
  2. Session 2: Create thin extensions
  3. Session 3: Refine pencil mustache, perfect symmetry
  4. Immediate high-maintenance

### 3. Goatee → Anchor
* **Duration**: 3–4 sedmice
* **Difficulty**: Medium
* **Steps**:
  1. Already have chin foundation
  2. Grow out jawline area
  3. Form thin extensions
  4. Add thin pencil mustache
  5. Create gaps, refine

### 4. Van Dyke → Anchor
* **Duration**: 2–3 sedmice
* **Difficulty**: Medium
* **Steps**:
  1. Thin down mustache to pencil
  2. Grow out jawline for extensions
  3. Form thin extensions
  4. Maintain pointed chin
  5. Higher precision maintenance

### 5. Anchor → Van Dyke
* **Duration**: 1 session
* **Difficulty**: Easy (removing complexity)
* **Steps**:
  1. Shave jawline extensions
  2. Keep pointed chin
  3. Can grow fuller mustache
  4. Lower maintenance

### 6. Anchor → Chin Strap
* **Duration**: 1–2 sessiona
* **Difficulty**: Easy-Medium
* **Steps**:
  1. Shave pointed chin beard
  2. Extend thin line to full chin strap
  3. Remove mustache
  4. Different but still high-precision

## Assumptions

* Prosječna stopa rasta: 1.1–1.3 cm/mjesečno
* Jawline definition: Clear natural jawline required za extensions
* Cheek shaving frequency: Must be comfortable with daily/near-daily shaving
* Precision skill: High hand-eye coordination required
* Time investment: 70–100 minuta sedmično non-negotiable
* Hair texture: Straight preferred; curly makes thin extensions very difficult
* Facial hair density: High density on chin i jawline required
* Patience: Mastering anchor maintenance takes 4–8 weeks of practice
