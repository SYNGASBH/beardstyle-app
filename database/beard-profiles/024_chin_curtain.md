# Chin Curtain – Inženjerski profil i karakteristike

Chin Curtain (poznata i kao "Lincoln Beard", "Amish Beard", ili "Donegal") je distinctive beard stil koji prati liniju vilice od uha do uha, pokrivajući chin ali ostavljajući gornju usnu čistom. Ovaj stil je become iconic zahvaljujući Abrahamu Lincolnu i ostaje popularan u Amish zajednicama. Chin Curtain naglašava jawline i pruža unikatan okvir za lice.

---

## Ključne dimenzije i proporcije

* **Horizontalna coverage**: Od uha do uha
  → Prati jawline kontinuirano
  → Pokriva chin potpuno
  → Connects sideburns via chin

* **Dužina brade** (na bradi): 3–15 cm
  → Short Curtain: 3–5 cm
  → Medium Curtain: 5–10 cm
  → Long Curtain (Amish style): 10–15+ cm

* **Širina trake** (na jaw): 3–8 cm
  → Narrow: 3–4 cm (closer to chin strap)
  → Medium: 4–6 cm
  → Wide: 6–8 cm

* **Mustache**: NEMA
  → Clean upper lip
  → Ovo je defining characteristic
  → Razlikuje od Full Beard

* **Cheek coverage**: Minimal ili none
  → Clean cheeks preferred
  → Može imati thin sideburns leading into curtain
  → No full cheek coverage

* **Sideburns**: Connected ili separate
  → Option A: Thin sideburns merge into curtain
  → Option B: Curtain starts at ear without sideburns
  → Both valid variations

* **Donja kontura**: Natural ili defined
  → Amish: Long, natural, untrimmed
  → Modern: Shaped, maintained length

## Održavanje – Kontrolne tačke

* **Trim interval**:
  * Length (ako maintained): Svakih 7–14 dana
  * Jawline edge: Svakih 5–7 dana
  * Upper lip shave: Svakih 1–2 dana
  * Cheek shave: Svakih 2–3 dana
  * **Ukupno**: Srednje održavanje (3–4 sessiona sedmično)

* **Alati**:
  * Trimmer sa guards (4–15+ mm zavisno od dužine)
  * Precision trimmer (0 mm) – za edges
  * Razor – za mustache i cheeks
  * Beard scissors – za shaping
  * Comb – za styling

* **Proizvodi**:
  * Beard oil: 3–6 kapi
  * Beard balm: Za shaping (if styling)
  * Aftershave: Za shaved areas

* **Vrijeme po sessiji**: 10–15 minuta
* **Sedmični time investment**: 35–55 minuta

## Razlike vs slični stilovi

| Karakteristika | Chin Curtain | Chin Strap | Full Beard | Mutton Chops | Amish Beard |
|----------------|--------------|------------|------------|--------------|-------------|
| **Chin** | Full coverage | Thin line | Full | Clean | Full (long) |
| **Mustache** | No | No | Yes | No | No |
| **Cheeks** | Clean | Clean | Covered | Covered | Clean |
| **Jawline** | Followed | Followed | Covered | Covered | Followed |
| **Width** | Medium | Thin | Full | Wide | Medium-Wide |
| **Length** | Variable | Short | Variable | Variable | Long |

### Ključne distinkcije:
* **Chin Curtain vs Chin Strap**: Chin Curtain je širi (4–8 cm); Chin Strap je thin line (1–2 cm).
* **Chin Curtain vs Full Beard**: Chin Curtain nema mustache; Full Beard ima.
* **Chin Curtain vs Amish Beard**: Amish Beard je Chin Curtain varijanta sa long, untrimmed length.
* **Chin Curtain vs Mutton Chops**: Chin Curtain pokriva chin; Mutton Chops imaju clean chin.

---

## Dizajn pravila za autentičan Chin Curtain

1. **Mustache MORA biti odsutan** – Connected mustache = Full Beard territory.

2. **Jawline to jawline connection** – Must connect from ear to ear via chin. No gaps.

3. **Cheeks MORAJU biti čisti** – Full cheek coverage = different style.

4. **Width substantial** – Too thin = Chin Strap, ne Chin Curtain.

5. **Upper lip shave non-negotiable** – Any mustache growth changes the style.

6. **Symmetry important** – Both sides should be balanced.

7. **Length choice** – Can be short or long, but consistent.

8. **Ear connection** – Must connect at ili near ear level.

## Growth Timeline

**Od clean shaven do Chin Curtain:**

### Phase 1: Full Growth (Sedmica 1–4)
* **Sedmica 1–2**:
  → Expected growth: ~2–3 cm
  → Grow full face (easier to shape from full)
* **Sedmica 3–4**:
  → Expected growth: +2–3 cm (total: ~5 cm)
  → Substantial growth za shaping

### Phase 2: Shaping (Sedmica 5–6)
* **Sedmica 5**:
  → INITIAL SHAPING:
  → Shave mustache completely
  → Shave cheeks
  → Define jawline edges
  → Create curtain shape
* **Sedmica 6**:
  → Refine edges
  → Perfect symmetry
  → Establish width

### Phase 3: Maintenance ili Growth (Sedmica 7+)
* **If short Curtain**: Maintenance mode
* **If Amish-length**: Continue growing, minimal trimming

**Total time to short Chin Curtain**: 5–6 sedmica
**Total time for long Curtain**: 10–20+ sedmica

## Transition Paths

### 1. Clean Shaven → Chin Curtain
* **Duration**: 5–6 sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Medium
* **Key steps**:
  1. Sedmica 1–4: Grow full face
  2. Sedmica 5: Shave mustache i cheeks, define curtain
  3. Sedmica 6+: Refine and maintain ili grow

### 2. Full Beard → Chin Curtain
* **Duration**: 1 session
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Shave mustache
  2. Shave cheeks
  3. Define jawline coverage
  4. Immediate maintenance

### 3. Goatee → Chin Curtain
* **Duration**: 4–6 sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Medium
* **Steps**:
  1. Grow out jawline areas
  2. Shave mustache if present
  3. Connect goatee to jawline
  4. Create curtain shape

### 4. Chin Curtain → Full Beard
* **Duration**: 4–6 sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Grow mustache
  2. Let cheeks fill if desired
  3. Connect mustache
  4. Full beard achieved

### 5. Chin Curtain → Chin Strap
* **Duration**: 1 session
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Trim width to thin line
  2. Same shape, less width
  3. Chin Strap achieved

### 6. Chin Curtain → Amish Long
* **Duration**: 10–20+ sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Easy (patience)
* **Steps**:
  1. Stop trimming length
  2. Maintain mustache clean
  3. Let grow naturally
  4. Long Amish style achieved

## Cultural Context

### Religious Significance:
* Amish: Married men wear beard, shave mustache (military association)
* Mennonite: Similar tradition
* Religious observance, not just style

### Historical:
* Abraham Lincoln – iconic American association
* 19th century popularity
* Civil War era common

### Modern:
* Less common as fashion choice
* Often religious or historical reenactment
* Some hipster adoption
