# Chevron Mustache – Inženjerski profil i karakteristike

Chevron je klasični, thick mustache stil koji prekriva cijelu gornju usnu u obliku obrnutog slova V (chevron shape). Populariziran 1970-ih i 80-ih, ovaj stil je postao sinonim za muževnost i autoritet. Za razliku od handlebar ili pencil stilova, Chevron ne zahtijeva styling ili wax – prirodna debljina i shape su defining karakteristike.

---

## Ključne dimenzije i proporcije

* **Dužina brkova** (horizontalno, od kraja do kraja): Prati širinu usana
  → Tipično: 8–12 cm
  → Ne prelazi uglove usana značajno
  → Krajevi: Blago tapered ili straight cut

* **Vertikalna visina**: 10–20 mm
  → Od nosne baze do gornje usne
  → Thick, full coverage cijelom širinom
  → Fill factor critical – mora biti thick

* **Lip coverage**: Djelomično ili potpuno
  → Classic Chevron: Prekriva gornju usnu
  → Modified Chevron: Just above lip line
  → Debljina je ključna, ne lip coverage

* **Oblik**: Inverted V ili trapezoid
  → Najširi na vrhu (ispod nosa)
  → Blago sužava prema krajevima
  → Chevron angle: 10–25°
  → Natural, unfussy shape

* **Krajevi (ends)**: Natural ili blago trimmed
  → Ne prelaze značajno uglove usana
  → Nema handlebar styling
  → Natural downward growth

* **Povezanost sa bradom**: Nema (standalone)
  → Clean shaven face
  → Soul patch optional ali usually not
  → Pure mustache statement

* **Gustina**: CRITICAL
  → Fill factor: >95% required
  → Sparse Chevron nije Chevron – to je mustache in progress
  → Thick, full, uniform density

---

## Održavanje – Kontrolne tačke

* **Trim interval**:
  * Lip line clearance: Svakih 3–5 dana
  * Width (ends): Svakih 5–7 dana
  * Overall shape: Svakih 7–10 dana
  * Face shave: Svakih 1–3 dana
  * **Ukupno**: Srednje održavanje (3–4 sessiona sedmično)

* **Alati**:
  * Mustache scissors – primary tool
  * Fine-tooth mustache comb
  * Precision trimmer – za edges
  * Razor – za face shave

* **Proizvodi**:
  * Mustache wax: Optional (za extra hold, not styling)
  * Beard/mustache oil: 2–3 kapi za softness
  * Aftershave: Za shaved face area

* **Vrijeme po sessiji**: 8–12 minuta
* **Sedmični time investment**: 30–45 minuta

## Razlike vs slični stilovi

| Karakteristika | Chevron | Handlebar | Pencil | Walrus | Horseshoe |
|----------------|---------|-----------|--------|--------|-----------|
| **Width** | Full lip | Beyond lip | Thin | Beyond lip | Down to chin |
| **Thickness** | Thick | Medium-Thick | Thin | Very thick | Medium |
| **Styling** | None | Wax required | Precise | None | None |
| **Lip coverage** | Partial/Full | Minimal | None | Heavy | Down sides |
| **Maintenance** | Medium | High | High | Low | Medium |
| **Era association** | 70s-80s | Victorian | 30s-40s | Old West | Biker |

### Ključne distinkcije:
* **Chevron vs Handlebar**: Chevron nema styled ends; Handlebar ima curled/waxed ends.
* **Chevron vs Walrus**: Chevron je trimmed da ne prelazi usnu previše; Walrus potpuno prekriva usnu.
* **Chevron vs Pencil**: Chevron je thick i natural; Pencil je thin i precise.
* **Chevron vs Horseshoe**: Chevron ostaje na usni; Horseshoe extends down past mouth.

---

## Dizajn pravila za autentičan Chevron

1. **Thick is non-negotiable** – Sparse, thin mustache nije Chevron. Ako density nije >95%, razmotriti drugi stil.

2. **No wax styling** – Čim počneš stilizirati krajeve sa waxom, to je handlebar, ne Chevron. Natural shape only.

3. **Lip coverage defines it** – Chevron treba prekrivati ili touch gornju usnu. Ako je previše kratko, nije Chevron.

4. **Keep ends neat, not styled** – Ends mogu biti trimmed clean, ali ne styled outward.

5. **Face must be clean** – Chevron je standalone mustache. Beard, even stubble, changes the style.

6. **Width follows lips** – Ne prelazi značajno uglove usana. Stays within lip width + 0.5–1 cm.

7. **Inverted V shape** – Širi na vrhu, uži na krajevima. Ovo je chevron shape.

8. **Regular trimming required** – Overgrown Chevron postaje Walrus. Maintain defined shape.

## Growth Timeline

**Od clean shaven do Chevron target:**

### Phase 1: Initial Growth (Sedmica 1–4)
* **Sedmica 1–2**:
  → Expected growth: ~3–4 mm
  → Mustache becoming visible
  → Let full face grow (easier to shape later)
* **Sedmica 3–4**:
  → Expected growth: +4 mm (total: ~7–8 mm)
  → Mustache gaining substance
  → Can start shaving face, keep mustache

### Phase 2: Shaping (Sedmica 5–8)
* **Sedmica 5–6**:
  → Expected growth: 10–12 mm
  → Chevron shape becoming possible
  → Begin defining ends
  → Lip line management begins
* **Sedmica 7–8**:
  → Expected growth: 14–16 mm
  → Full Chevron achievable
  → Refine shape
  → Establish maintenance routine

### Phase 3: Maintenance (Sedmica 9+)
* Steady state
* Lip clearance trim svakih 3–5 dana
* Shape maintenance svakih 7–10 dana
* Face shave svakih 1–3 dana

**Total time to achieve**: 6–8 sedmica (1.5–2 mjeseca)
**Minimum growth required**: 10 mm vertical height

## Transition Paths

### 1. Clean Shaven → Chevron
* **Duration**: 6–8 sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Easy-Medium (patience required)
* **Key steps**:
  1. Sedmica 1–4: Grow full face, then shave keeping mustache
  2. Sedmica 5–6: Begin shaping Chevron
  3. Sedmica 7–8: Refine, establish maintenance

### 2. Full Beard → Chevron
* **Duration**: 1 session
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Shave beard completely, keep mustache
  2. Shape mustache to Chevron
  3. Immediate maintenance begins

### 3. Beardstache → Chevron
* **Duration**: 1 session
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Shave stubble
  2. Keep and refine mustache
  3. Pure Chevron achieved

### 4. Chevron → Handlebar
* **Duration**: 4–6 sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Medium
* **Steps**:
  1. Let ends grow past lip corners
  2. Begin wax training
  3. Style ends outward
  4. Different maintenance approach

### 5. Chevron → Walrus
* **Duration**: 4–8 sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Stop trimming lip line
  2. Let grow over lip completely
  3. Allow natural drape
  4. Less maintenance

### 6. Chevron → Full Beard with Mustache
* **Duration**: 8–12 sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Stop shaving face
  2. Let beard grow
  3. Chevron integrates with beard
  4. Full beard style achieved
