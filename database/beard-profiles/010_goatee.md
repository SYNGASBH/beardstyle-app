# Goatee – Inženjerski profil i karakteristike

Goatee je minimalistički, chin-focused beard stil koji pokriva samo bradu (chin) bez pokrivanja obraza ili, u čistoj formi, bez brkova. Ime dolazi od sličnosti sa kozjom bradicom. Ovo je jedan od najstarijih i najprepoznatljivijih facial hair stilova – čist, jednostavan, i izuzetno versatilan. Goatee može varirati od small soul patch-a do punog chin coverage-a, ali uvijek ostaje focused na donji dio lica sa čistim obrazima.

---

## Ključne dimenzije i proporcije

* **Dužina goatee-a** (od donje usne do najniže tačke): 1–8 cm
  → Kratki goatee: 1–3 cm (stubble to short)
  → Srednji goatee: 3–5 cm (classic)
  → Dugi goatee: 5–8 cm (extended)
  → Optimalno: 2–4 cm za clean, defined look

* **Širina goatee-a** (na chin): 3–7 cm
  → Narrow: 3–4 cm (chin tip only)
  → Medium: 4–5 cm (standard coverage)
  → Wide: 5–7 cm (full chin coverage)
  → **Ne prelazi na jawline** – granica je mentolabijalni sulkus

* **Donja kontura**: Variable – rounded, pointed, ili natural
  → Rounded goatee: Radius 2–4 cm
  → Pointed goatee: Angle 40–60°
  → Natural goatee: Follows chin shape
  → Shape choice je personalizacija

* **Cheek area**: ČISTA
  → **Nema dlaka na obrazima**
  → Clean shaved ili stubble OK na obrazima
  → Granica je jasna – goatee je isolated

* **Brkovi (Mustache)**: OPCIONALNO
  → Pure goatee: Nema brkova – samo chin hair
  → Goatee + mustache: Connected ili separated
  → Ako connected: Postaje Circle Beard
  → Ako separated: Postaje Van Dyke varijanta

* **Soul patch**: Često prisutan
  → Pozicija: Direktno ispod donje usne
  → Veličina: 0.5–2 cm širina
  → Može biti jedini element (soul patch only) ili dio šireg goatee-a

* **Projekcija volumena**: +5–15% donjeg lica
  → Subtle enhancement
  → Depth: 1–4 cm zavisno od dužine

* **Tekstura i gustina**:
  → Fill factor: >85% za defined look
  → Stray hairs: Minimalni na obrazima
  → Any texture works – straight, wavy, curly

## Održavanje – Kontrolne tačke

* **Trim interval**:
  * Goatee dužina: Svakih 5–10 dana
  * Goatee shape: Svakih 5–7 dana
  * Cheek maintenance: Svakih 2–4 dana
  * Brkovi (ako postoje): Svakih 3–5 dana
  * **Ukupno**: Srednje održavanje (2–3 sessiona sedmično)

* **Alati**:
  * Precision trimmer (0 mm blade) – za edges
  * Trimmer sa guards (2–10 mm) – za length
  * Razor – za cheek shaving (ako clean cheeks)
  * Beard scissors – za shaping
  * Fine-tooth comb

* **Proizvodi**:
  * Beard oil: 1–3 kapi dnevno (minimal application)
  * Beard balm: Opcionalno, za styling
  * Shaving cream: Za cheek area (ako shaving)
  * Aftershave: Za cheek area

* **Vrijeme po sessiji**: 5–10 minuta
* **Sedmični time investment**: 20–35 minuta

## Razlike vs slični stilovi

| Karakteristika | Goatee | Soul Patch | Circle Beard | Van Dyke | Chin Strap |
|----------------|--------|------------|--------------|----------|------------|
| **Coverage** | Chin | Below lip only | Chin + connected mustache | Chin + separated mustache | Jawline |
| **Mustache** | Optional | No | Required, connected | Required, separated | No |
| **Shape** | Variable | Patch | Circular | Pointed | Thin line |
| **Cheeks** | Clean | Clean | Clean | Clean | Connected to chin |
| **Width** | 3–7 cm | 1–2 cm | 4–7 cm | 4–8 cm | 1–2 cm |
| **Maintenance** | Low-Medium | Low | Medium | High | High |
| **Overall vibe** | Minimalist classic | Subtle accent | Casual connected | Artistic classic | Defined jawline |

### Ključne distinkcije:
* **Goatee vs Soul Patch**: Goatee pokriva chin; Soul Patch je samo mala zona ispod usne.
* **Goatee vs Circle Beard**: Circle Beard ima connected mustache; Goatee može biti bez brkova.
* **Goatee vs Van Dyke**: Van Dyke ima pointed shape i separated styled mustache; Goatee je simpler, mustache optional.
* **Goatee vs Chin Strap**: Chin Strap prati jawline; Goatee je centered na chin.

## Dizajn pravila za autentičan Goatee

1. **Fokus na chin, ne jaw** – Goatee pokriva chin area. Čim se proširuje na jawline, ulazi u Balbo ili extended goatee teritoriju.

2. **Obrazi moraju biti čisti** – Bilo kakve dlake na obrazima dilutiraju goatee efekt. Clean shave ili consistent stubble.

3. **Shape consistency** – Odluči za rounded, pointed, ili natural shape i održavaj konzistentno.

4. **Centering je kritičan** – Goatee mora biti centered na bradi. Off-center izgleda kao greška.

5. **Brkovi su opcioni element** – Pure goatee nema brkove. Ako dodaješ brkove, odluči: connected ili separated.

6. **Width proportional to chin** – Previše uski izgleda kao soul patch; previše široki izgleda messy.

7. **Edge definition matters** – Defined edges čine goatee intentional. Blurry edges = unkempt look.

8. **Length appropriate to width** – Dugi narrow goatee izgleda stringy; kratki wide izgleda kao stubble.

## Face Shape Compatibility

| Oblik lica | Score | Obrazloženje |
|------------|-------|--------------|
| **Oval** | 90/100 | Odličan. Goatee adds subtle interest bez disrupting balance. |
| **Square** | 80/100 | Dobro. Softens angular chin subtly. Keep rounded shape. |
| **Round** | 95/100 | Idealno. Goatee elongates round face, adds definition. Best match. |
| **Triangle** | 70/100 | Oprez. Pointed goatee na pointed chin može biti too much. Use rounded. |
| **Diamond** | 85/100 | Radi dobro. Adds chin definition, balances cheekbones. |
| **Oblong** | 75/100 | OK ali pažljivo. Keep short (2–3 cm) da ne elongira dalje. |

**Best match**: Round (95/100)
**Excellent**: Oval (90/100)
**Good**: Diamond (85/100)
**Moderate**: Square (80/100)
**Careful**: Oblong, Triangle (70–75/100)

## Growth Timeline

**Od clean shaven do Goatee target (3 cm):**

### Phase 1: Initial Growth (Sedmica 1–2)
* **Sedmica 1**:
  → Expected growth: ~1 cm
  → Let entire face grow
  → OR immediately define goatee zone (advanced)
* **Sedmica 2**:
  → Expected growth: +1 cm (total: ~2 cm)
  → Define goatee boundaries
  → Shave cheeks

### Phase 2: Shaping (Sedmica 3–4)
* **Sedmica 3**:
  → Expected growth: ~3 cm
  → Target length achieved
  → Refine shape (rounded/pointed)
  → Define edges precisely
* **Sedmica 4**:
  → Perfect shape
  → Establish maintenance routine
  → Consider mustache addition

### Phase 3: Maintenance (Sedmica 5+)
* Steady state
* Cheek maintenance svakih 2–4 dana
* Goatee trim svakih 5–10 dana

**Total time to achieve**: 3–4 sedmice (0.75–1 mjesec)
**Minimum growth required**: 2 cm (≈ 1.5–2 sedmice)

## Transition Paths

### 1. Clean Shaven → Goatee
* **Duration**: 3–4 sedmice
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Key steps**:
  1. Sedmica 1: Grow full face OR define zone immediately
  2. Sedmica 2: Shave cheeks, define boundaries
  3. Sedmica 3: Shape goatee
  4. Sedmica 4+: Maintenance mode

### 2. Full Beard → Goatee
* **Duration**: 1 session
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Shave cheeks completely
  2. Shave jawline
  3. Define chin area
  4. Trim to desired length
  5. Optional: Remove mustache

### 3. Stubble → Goatee
* **Duration**: 2–3 sedmice
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Continue growing chin area
  2. Shave/maintain cheeks as stubble or clean
  3. Shape when 2+ cm achieved
  4. Maintenance mode

### 4. Goatee → Circle Beard
* **Duration**: 2–3 sedmice
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Grow mustache if not present
  2. Connect mustache to goatee at corners
  3. Shape circular connection
  4. Same cheek maintenance

### 5. Goatee → Van Dyke
* **Duration**: 2–3 sedmice
* **Difficulty**: Easy-Medium
* **Steps**:
  1. Grow styled mustache
  2. Keep mustache SEPARATED from goatee
  3. Form pointed shape on goatee
  4. Higher maintenance begins

### 6. Goatee → Full Beard
* **Duration**: 8–12 sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Easy (just growing)
* **Steps**:
  1. Stop shaving cheeks
  2. Let full face grow
  3. Connect mustache when desired
  4. Shape full beard at 5+ cm

## Varijante Goatee-a

### 1. Pure Goatee (Chin Only)
* Nema brkova
* Samo chin coverage
* Most minimalist version

### 2. Goatee + Soul Patch
* Chin hair + emphasized soul patch
* Soul patch slightly longer/wider
* Two-zone effect

### 3. Goatee + Disconnected Mustache
* = Van Dyke territory
* Separate mustache styling
* Higher maintenance

### 4. Goatee + Connected Mustache
* = Circle Beard territory
* Connected at corners
* More coverage

### 5. Extended Goatee
* Slightly wider coverage
* Approaching Balbo width
* 6–8 cm width

## Assumptions

* Prosječna stopa rasta: 1.1–1.3 cm/mjesečno
* Chin hair density: Mora biti dovoljna za goatee; patchy chin = problematično
* Cheek maintenance: Ability to maintain clean or stubble cheeks
* Commitment: Low-to-medium (2–3 sessiona sedmično)
* Styling skill: Low – jedan od easiest stilova za maintain
* Facial hair distribution: Chin growth primary requirement
