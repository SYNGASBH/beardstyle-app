# Imperial Beard – Inženjerski profil i karakteristike

Imperial Beard je distinguished, aristocratic stil koji kombinuje prominent upward-curling mustache sa pointed chin beard (goatee). Ovaj stil je nazvan po europskoj aristokraciji 19. vijeka, posebno carovima poput Napoleona III i Franz Josefa I. Karakterističan je po elaborate mustache styling-u koji zahtijeva wax i precizno oblikovanje – true statement piece za one koji cijene traditional masculine elegance.

---

## Ključne dimenzije i proporcije

* **Mustache – GLAVNA KARAKTERISTIKA**:
  → Stil: Upward-curling ends (waxed)
  → Dužina: 3–8 cm (od centra do kraja curla)
  → Debljina: 1–2 cm na centru
  → Curl angle: 30–90° upward
  → Tip: Može biti pointed ili rounded curl
  → Wax: OBAVEZAN za održavanje oblika

* **Chin Beard (Goatee component)**:
  → Dužina: 2–5 cm
  → Širina: 2–4 cm (narrow, pointed)
  → Shape: Pointed ili slightly rounded
  → Connected to mustache: NE – separated je karakteristika
  → Gap od mustache: 0.5–2 cm

* **Soul Patch area**: Variable
  → Može biti uključen ili ne
  → Ako postoji: 0.5–1.5 cm širine

* **Cheeks**: ČISTE
  → Potpuno obrijane
  → Bez stubble ili coverage
  → Clean contrast je essential

* **Sideburns**: Minimal ili none
  → Ako postoje: kratke, neat
  → Ne smiju odvlačiti pažnju od mustache

* **Neckline**: Clean defined
  → 1–2 prsta iznad Adam's apple
  → Follows natural curve

* **Projekcija volumena**: +15–25% donjeg lica
  → Fokus na horizontal mustache extension
  → Moderate chin projection

* **Tekstura i gustina**:
  → Mustache fill: >90% za full curl support
  → Chin fill: >85%
  → Hair strength: Strong hair needed za curl retention

## Održavanje – Kontrolne tačke

* **Trim interval**:
  * Mustache shaping: Svakih 3–5 dana
  * Curl re-waxing: DNEVNO (1–2x)
  * Chin beard trim: Svakih 5–7 dana
  * Cheek shave: Svakih 1–2 dana
  * Gap maintenance: Svakih 3–5 dana
  * **Ukupno**: Visoko održavanje (daily waxing + 3–4 trim sessiona sedmično)

* **Alati**:
  * Mustache wax (strong hold) – ESSENTIAL
  * Precision trimmer – za shaping
  * Fine scissors – za mustache tips
  * Beard comb (fine-tooth) – za daily styling
  * Hair dryer (low heat) – za wax activation
  * Razor – za cheek maintenance

* **Proizvodi**:
  * Mustache wax: 1–2 applications dnevno (CRITICAL)
  * Beard oil: 2–3 kapi za chin
  * Shaving cream: Za cheeks
  * Aftershave: Za cheek area

* **Vrijeme po sessiji**: 10–15 minuta (daily waxing) + 15–20 minuta (trim sessions)
* **Sedmični time investment**: 90–120 minuta

### Ključne distinkcije:
* **Imperial vs Van Dyke**: Imperial ima elaborate curled mustache; Van Dyke ima simpler styled mustache.
* **Imperial vs Handlebar**: Imperial ima chin beard; Handlebar je mustache-only.
* **Imperial vs Verdi**: Imperial ima pointed chin; Verdi ima full rounded beard.
* **Imperial vs Anchor**: Imperial nema jawline extensions; Anchor ima thin extensions.

## Dizajn pravila za autentičan Imperial

1. **Mustache curls moraju biti UPWARD** – Downward ili straight nije Imperial. Upward curl je defining feature.

2. **Wax je NON-NEGOTIABLE** – Bez wax-a, mustache gubi Imperial character. Daily application required.

3. **Chin beard mora biti SEPARATED** – Connected to mustache = nije Imperial, to je drugačiji stil.

4. **Cheeks moraju biti CLEAN** – Imperial je o contrast i definition, ne coverage.

5. **Symmetry je CRITICAL** – Oba curl-a moraju biti identična u angle i length.

6. **Pointed chin preferred** – Rounded je acceptable ali pointed je more authentic.

7. **Mustache je FOCAL POINT** – Chin beard dopunjuje, ne dominira.

8. **Curl maintenance je daily commitment** – Imperial bez maintained curls izgleda loše.

## Growth Timeline

**Od clean shaven do Imperial:**

### Phase 1: Full Growth (Sedmica 1–4)
* **Sedmica 1–2**:
  → Expected mustache growth: ~2 cm
  → Expected chin growth: ~2 cm
  → Grow full – no shaping yet
  → Practice wax technique on what you have
* **Sedmica 3–4**:
  → Mustache: ~3–4 cm
  → Chin: ~3–4 cm
  → Start light shaping
  → Begin curl training

### Phase 2: Shaping & Training (Sedmica 5–8)
* **Sedmica 5–6**:
  → Mustache approaching minimum curl length
  → Shave cheeks clean
  → Define chin beard shape
  → Create mustache-chin gap
  → Intensive curl training begins
* **Sedmica 7–8**:
  → Mustache: 5–6 cm
  → Curls becoming trainable
  → Refine pointed chin
  → Establish daily wax routine

### Phase 3: Mature Imperial (Sedmica 9–12)
* **Sedmica 9–10**:
  → Mustache: 6–7 cm
  → Curls holding shape better
  → Full Imperial achievable
* **Sedmica 11–12**:
  → Maintenance mode
  → Curls well-trained
  → Styling becomes routine

**Total time to achieve**: 8–12 sedmica (2–3 mjeseca)
**Minimum mustache length for curls**: 4 cm
**Curl training period**: 3–4 sedmice

## Transition Paths

### 1. Clean Shaven → Imperial
* **Duration**: 8–12 sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Hard
* **Key steps**:
  1. Sedmica 1–4: Grow full face
  2. Sedmica 5–6: Shape chin, clean cheeks, start curl training
  3. Sedmica 7–8: Intensive curl development
  4. Sedmica 9–12: Refine and master

### 2. Full Beard → Imperial
* **Duration**: 2–4 sedmice (if mustache length sufficient)
* **Difficulty**: Medium
* **Steps**:
  1. Shave cheeks and jaw clean
  2. Shape pointed chin beard
  3. Begin curl training on mustache
  4. Master waxing technique

### 3. Handlebar → Imperial
* **Duration**: 3–5 sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Easy-Medium
* **Steps**:
  1. Already have curled mustache skill
  2. Grow chin beard
  3. Shape pointed goatee
  4. Maintain existing curl expertise

### 4. Van Dyke → Imperial
* **Duration**: 4–6 sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Medium
* **Steps**:
  1. Grow mustache longer for curls
  2. Learn waxing and curl technique
  3. Maintain pointed chin
  4. Transition to elaborate curl style

### 5. Imperial → Van Dyke
* **Duration**: 1 session
* **Difficulty**: Easy (simplifying)
* **Steps**:
  1. Stop curling mustache
  2. Trim to simpler shape
  3. Reduce wax usage
  4. Lower maintenance

### 6. Imperial → Handlebar
* **Duration**: 1 session
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Shave chin beard
  2. Keep mustache curls
  3. Focus on mustache-only
  4. Similar maintenance
