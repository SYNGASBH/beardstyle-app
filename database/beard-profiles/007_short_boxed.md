# Short Boxed Beard – Inženjerski profil i karakteristike

Short Boxed Beard je kompaktna, strukturirana beard varijanta koja kombinuje prisutnost "prave brade" sa minimalnom dužinom i maksimalnom preciznošću. Predstavlja kraću verziju Boxed Beard-a sa istim naglaskom na geometrijske linije i čiste ivice. Ovaj stil je idealan za one koji žele definisan beard look bez commitment-a dužih stilova – office-friendly, low-volume, high-definition pristup.

---

## Ključne dimenzije i proporcije

* **Dužina brade** (od donje usne do najniže tačke): 3–5 cm
  → Optimalno: 3.5–4.5 cm za balans prisutnosti i neatness
  → <3 cm: Prelazi u heavy stubble teritoriju
  → >5 cm: Ulazi u Boxed Beard kategoriju

* **Širina brade**: Prati natural jawline
  → Target ratio: Width ≈ 1.0× natural jawline width
  → Sides: Vertikalne linije, prate jawline
  → Nema flare – controlled, compact look

* **Donja kontura**: Squared-off, čista linija
  → Shape: Relativno ravna sa blago zaobljenim kutovima
  → Corners: Radius 0.5–1.5 cm (subtilno zaobljenje)
  → Prati natural jaw angle

* **Cheek line**: Definisana, čista
  → Tip: Hard line (jasna granica, minimalan fade)
  → Pozicija: 1–2 cm ispod jagodične kosti
  → Simetrija: Kritična
  → **Nema stray hairs** iznad linije

* **Neckline**: Precizno definisan
  → Pozicija: 1–2 prsta iznad Adam's apple
  → Shape: Prati natural jaw curve
  → Clean shaven ispod – nema gradual fade

* **Brkovi (Mustache)**: Integrisani, neat
  → Stil: Full connected, ista dužina kao brada
  → Lip clearance: 2–3 mm iznad gornje usne
  → Kutovi: Clean connection sa bradom
  → Nema preklapanja preko usne

* **Projekcija volumena**: +15–25% donjeg lica
  → Subtle enhancement, ne dramatic
  → Depth: 1.5–2.5 cm uniformno

* **Tekstura i gustina**:
  → Fill factor: >90% required za clean look
  → Stray hairs: Zero tolerance
  → Preferred texture: Any, ali mora biti groomed flat

## Održavanje – Kontrolne tačke

* **Trim interval**:
  * Dužina (bulk): Svakih 4–6 dana
  * Cheek line: Svakih 2–4 dana
  * Neckline: Svakih 2–4 dana
  * Brkovi: Svakih 2–4 dana
  * Strays: Daily check
  * **Ukupno**: Visoko održavanje (4–5 sessiona sedmično)

* **Alati**:
  * Trimmer sa guards (3–6 mm range) – za bulk
  * Precision trimmer (0 mm blade) – za linije
  * Detail scissors – za strays
  * Fine-tooth comb – za uniformity
  * Magnifying mirror – za precision

* **Proizvodi**:
  * Beard oil: 2–4 kapi dnevno (light application)
  * Beard balm: Opcionalno, za flyaway control
  * Beard wash: 3× sedmično
  * Aftershave balm: Za neckline/cheek area post-shave

* **Vrijeme po sessiji**: 8–12 minuta
* **Sedmični time investment**: 40–60 minuta maintenance

## Razlike vs slični stilovi

| Karakteristika | Short Boxed | Boxed Beard | Corporate Beard | Heavy Stubble | Beardstache |
|----------------|-------------|-------------|-----------------|---------------|-------------|
| **Dužina** | 3–5 cm | 5–10 cm | 2–5 cm | 0.5–1.5 cm | 0.5–2 cm |
| **Donja kontura** | Squared/flat | Squared/flat | Natural defined | Variable | Natural/minimal |
| **Cheek line** | Hard defined | Hard defined | Hard defined | Defined | Variable |
| **Volume** | Low-Medium | Medium | Low | Minimal | Minimal |
| **Maintenance** | High | Medium-High | High | Medium | Medium |
| **Mustache** | Integrated same | Integrated same | Integrated same | Integrated | Prominent separate |
| **Overall vibe** | Compact structured | Full structured | Professional neat | Rugged casual | Statement contrast |

### Ključne distinkcije:
* **Short Boxed vs Boxed Beard**: Isti oblik, različita veličina. Short Boxed je 3–5 cm; Boxed je 5–10 cm. Short Boxed je kompaktniji i zahtijeva češći trim.
* **Short Boxed vs Corporate**: Vrlo slični – oba su office-appropriate sa defined linijama. Corporate može biti blago kraći i ima više naglaska na "professional" nego "bearded".
* **Short Boxed vs Heavy Stubble**: Short Boxed ima definitivnu "bradu" prisutnost (3+ cm); Stubble je facial hair bez beard volumena.

## Dizajn pravila za autentičan Short Boxed

1. **Uniformna dužina je kritična** – Na kratkoj dužini, svaka varijacija je vidljiva. Isti guard setting na cijeloj bradi, ±2 mm maksimum.

2. **Linije moraju biti oštre** – Blurry linije na short beard izgledaju kao "nije se obrijao" umjesto "ima bradu". Daily ili every-other-day line maintenance.

3. **Corners blago zaobljeni** – Oštri 90° kutovi ne rade na kratkoj dužini. Radius 0.5–1.5 cm za natural look.

4. **Cheek line konzistentna i čista** – Na short beard, high cheek hair izgleda neuredna. Održavaj clean, defined cheek line.

5. **Neckline critical** – Short beard bez definisanog neckline izgleda kao "stubble that got away". Clean shave ispod linije.

6. **Brkovi iste dužine kao brada** – Duži brkovi na short beard izgledaju neproporcijalno. Keep mustache length = beard length.

7. **Zero tolerance za strays** – Na kratkoj bradi, jedan stray hair je očigledan. Daily inspection and removal.

8. **Frequent trimming essential** – Short Boxed zahtijeva trim svakih 4–6 dana. Propuštanje = gubi se boxed shape.

## Face Shape Compatibility

| Oblik lica | Score | Obrazloženje |
|------------|-------|--------------|
| **Oval** | 95/100 | Savršen match. Short Boxed adds structure bez disrupting natural balance. |
| **Square** | 95/100 | Odličan. Angular beard complements angular face. Clean, masculine look. |
| **Round** | 85/100 | Dobro. Angular lines add definition to round face. Helps create structure. |
| **Triangle** | 85/100 | Funkcionira dobro. Adds balance without adding too much width to already wide jaw. |
| **Diamond** | 90/100 | Radi odlično. Angular beard works with cheekbones, adds chin definition. |
| **Oblong** | 80/100 | OK. Short length doesn't add too much vertical elongation. Keep at 3–4 cm. |

**Best match**: Oval, Square (95/100)
**Excellent**: Diamond (90/100)
**Very good**: Round, Triangle (85/100)
**Good**: Oblong (80/100)

## Growth Timeline

**Od clean shaven do Short Boxed target (4 cm):**

### Phase 1: Initial Growth (Sedmica 1–3)
* **Sedmica 1**:
  → Expected growth: ~1 cm
  → Stubble phase – let it grow
  → Define neckline at end of week 1
* **Sedmica 2**:
  → Expected growth: +1 cm (total: ~2 cm)
  → Heavy stubble territory
  → Define cheek line
* **Sedmica 3**:
  → Expected growth: +1 cm (total: ~3 cm)
  → Minimum Short Boxed length reached
  → First full shaping possible

### Phase 2: Shaping & Refinement (Sedmica 4–5)
* **Sedmica 4**:
  → Expected growth: reaching 3.5–4 cm
  → Full Short Boxed shaping
  → Establish squared bottom
  → Perfect all lines
* **Sedmica 5**:
  → Optimal length achieved (4 cm)
  → Fine-tune symmetry
  → Begin maintenance routine

### Phase 3: Maintenance (Sedmica 6+)
* Steady state
* Trim length svakih 4–6 dana
* Line maintenance svakih 2–4 dana
* Daily stray checks

**Total time to achieve**: 4–5 sedmica (1–1.25 mjeseca)
**Minimum growth required**: 3 cm (≈ 2.5–3 sedmice)

## Transition Paths

### 1. Clean Shaven → Short Boxed
* **Duration**: 4–5 sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Easy-Medium
* **Key steps**:
  1. Sedmica 1: Grow, define neckline
  2. Sedmica 2: Define cheek line
  3. Sedmica 3: First shaping, establish square bottom
  4. Sedmica 4–5: Refine, start maintenance

### 2. Heavy Stubble → Short Boxed
* **Duration**: 2–3 sedmice
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Continue growth to 3+ cm
  2. Define/refine all lines
  3. Shape squared bottom
  4. Maintenance mode

### 3. Boxed Beard → Short Boxed
* **Duration**: 1 session
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Trim length from 5–10 cm to 3–5 cm
  2. Maintain same angular shape
  3. Keep lines defined
  4. Slightly more frequent maintenance

### 4. Corporate Beard → Short Boxed
* **Duration**: Immediate (may need 1–2 sedmice if Corporate was shorter)
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Adjust length to 3–5 cm range
  2. Maintain defined lines
  3. Same maintenance frequency
  4. Virtually identical styles

### 5. Short Boxed → Boxed Beard
* **Duration**: 4–6 sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Let length grow to 5–10 cm
  2. Maintain lines during growth
  3. Same shape, more volume
  4. Slightly less frequent trimming

### 6. Short Boxed → Ducktail
* **Duration**: 5–8 sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Medium
* **Steps**:
  1. Let chin grow longer than sides
  2. At 6+ cm chin: start tapering sides
  3. Form pointed bottom
  4. Significant shape transformation

## Assumptions

* Prosječna stopa rasta: 1.1–1.3 cm/mjesečno (~1 cm/sedmica)
* Office environment: Profil pretpostavlja corporate/business casual dress code
* Grooming access: Daily access to mirror i basic tools
* Hair density: Medium-to-high preferred; low density može rezultirati patchy look
* Maintenance commitment: Must be willing to invest 8–12 min every other day
* Facial hair pattern: Requires connected beard; gaps visible at this length
