# Circle Beard – Inženjerski profil i karakteristike

Circle Beard je popularan, easy-to-maintain stil koji kombinuje goatee sa connected mustache, formirajući kružni ili ovalni oblik oko usta. Poznat i kao "door knocker" ili "standard goatee with mustache", ovaj stil je jedan od najuniverzalnijih facial hair opcija – dovoljno prisutan da bude statement, ali dovoljno nenametljiv da bude prihvatljiv u gotovo svim kontekstima.

---

## Ključne dimenzije i proporcije

* **Dužina goatee-a** (od donje usne do najniže tačke): 2–6 cm
  → Kratki: 2–3 cm (neat, compact)
  → Srednji: 3–4 cm (classic)
  → Duži: 4–6 cm (more presence)
  → Optimalno: 2.5–4 cm za balanced circular look

* **Širina circle-a** (mustache corner do mustache corner): 5–9 cm
  → Determined by mouth width + margin
  → Margin: 0.5–2 cm šire od uglova usana na svakoj strani
  → Proporcionalno sa face size

* **Donja kontura goatee**: Rounded ili natural
  → Shape: Follows natural chin curve
  → Radius: 3–5 cm (softer curve than pointed styles)
  → Point: Nema – rounded je karakteristika

* **Connection zone** (mustache to goatee): OBAVEZNO connected
  → Connection width: 0.5–2 cm na svakoj strani
  → Connection point: Corners of mouth area
  → **Nema gap** – ovo razlikuje Circle Beard od Van Dyke

* **Cheek area**: ČISTA
  → **Nema dlaka na obrazima**
  → Clean shaved ili uniform stubble dozvoljen
  → Boundary je jasna

* **Brkovi (Mustache)**: Connected, natural-to-styled
  → Stil: Natural full, chevron, ili shaped
  → Dužina: Typically 0.5–1.5 cm preko uglova usana
  → Lip clearance: 0–3 mm (can touch lip ili clear)
  → Integrisani, ne separated

* **Neckline**: Defined ili natural
  → Pozicija: 1–2 prsta iznad Adam's apple
  → Shape: Following jaw curve
  → Can be clean defined ili soft natural

* **Projekcija volumena**: +10–20% donjeg lica
  → Moderate, focused on mouth area
  → Depth: 1.5–3.5 cm

* **Tekstura i gustina**:
  → Fill factor: >85% za clean circular look
  → Stray hairs: Minimal tolerance outside boundary
  → Any texture works

## Održavanje – Kontrolne tačke

* **Trim interval**:
  * Goatee length: Svakih 5–10 dana
  * Circle shape/boundary: Svakih 4–7 dana
  * Mustache: Svakih 3–5 dana
  * Cheek maintenance: Svakih 2–4 dana
  * Neckline: Svakih 5–7 dana
  * **Ukupno**: Srednje održavanje (2–3 sessiona sedmično)

* **Alati**:
  * Trimmer sa guards (3–8 mm) – za length
  * Precision trimmer (0 mm blade) – za boundary definition
  * Razor – za cheek shaving (if clean)
  * Beard scissors – za fine-tuning
  * Comb – za styling

* **Proizvodi**:
  * Beard oil: 2–4 kapi dnevno
  * Beard balm: Opcionalno, za styling
  * Shaving cream: Za cheek area
  * Aftershave: Za cheek area

* **Vrijeme po sessiji**: 8–12 minuta
* **Sedmični time investment**: 25–40 minuta

## Razlike vs slični stilovi

| Karakteristika | Circle Beard | Goatee | Van Dyke | Extended Goatee | Balbo |
|----------------|--------------|--------|----------|-----------------|-------|
| **Mustache** | Connected | Optional | Separated | Connected | Separated |
| **Connection** | At corners | N/A | Gap | At corners | Gap |
| **Chin shape** | Rounded | Variable | Pointed | Rounded/variable | Rounded/wide |
| **Cheeks** | Clean | Clean | Clean | Partially covered | Clean |
| **Width** | Medium (5–9 cm) | Narrow | Narrow | Wider | Wide (8–14 cm) |
| **Maintenance** | Medium | Low-Medium | High | Medium | High |
| **Overall vibe** | Casual classic | Minimalist | Artistic | Fuller casual | Distinguished |

### Ključne distinkcije:
* **Circle Beard vs Goatee**: Circle Beard ima connected mustache; Goatee može biti bez brkova.
* **Circle Beard vs Van Dyke**: Circle Beard ima connected mustache; Van Dyke ima separated, styled mustache.
* **Circle Beard vs Extended Goatee**: Circle Beard ostaje around mouth; Extended Goatee ima više jaw coverage.
* **Circle Beard vs Balbo**: Circle Beard ima connected elements; Balbo ima separated mustache i širu coverage.

---

## Dizajn pravila za autentičan Circle Beard

1. **Mustache MORA biti connected** – Ovo je defining characteristic. Disconnected = Van Dyke, ne Circle Beard.

2. **Shape treba biti circular/oval** – Pointed chin ili squared edges nisu Circle Beard. Smooth, rounded boundaries.

3. **Obrazi moraju biti čisti** – Circle Beard je defined by what's NOT there – clean cheeks highlight the circle.

4. **Proportional width** – Circle treba biti proporcionalan sa face size. Previše široko = Extended Goatee territory.

5. **Connection mora biti solid** – Thin, weak connection izgleda kao "almost disconnected". Full connection.

6. **Symmetry is important** – Circle mora biti balanced. Uneven connections ili shapes su očite greške.

7. **Rounded bottom** – Pointed bottom nije Circle Beard. Keep rounded atau natural chin shape.

8. **Consistent density** – Mustache i goatee trebaju imati sličnu density za unified look.

## Face Shape Compatibility

| Oblik lica | Score | Obrazloženje |
|------------|-------|--------------|
| **Oval** | 95/100 | Idealno. Circle complements balanced proportions perfectly. |
| **Square** | 85/100 | Dobro. Rounded circle softens angular features. |
| **Round** | 80/100 | OK. Rounded circle on round face može biti redundant. Consider with caution. |
| **Triangle** | 85/100 | Dobro. Circle adds balance to chin area bez emphasizing point. |
| **Diamond** | 90/100 | Odličan. Circle adds chin definition, works with cheekbones. |
| **Oblong** | 85/100 | Dobro. Circular shape doesn't add length. Safe choice for long faces. |

**Best match**: Oval (95/100)
**Excellent**: Diamond (90/100)
**Good**: Square, Triangle, Oblong (85/100)
**Moderate**: Round (80/100)

## Growth Timeline

**Od clean shaven do Circle Beard target (3 cm):**

### Phase 1: Initial Growth (Sedmica 1–2)
* **Sedmica 1**:
  → Expected growth: ~1 cm
  → Option A: Grow full face
  → Option B: Define zone early, grow only circle area
* **Sedmica 2**:
  → Expected growth: +1 cm (total: ~2 cm)
  → Define circle boundaries
  → Shave cheeks, establish connection

### Phase 2: Shaping (Sedmica 3–4)
* **Sedmica 3**:
  → Expected growth: ~3 cm
  → Target length achieved
  → Refine circular shape
  → Perfect connection points
* **Sedmica 4**:
  → Fine-tune shape
  → Establish maintenance routine

### Phase 3: Maintenance (Sedmica 5+)
* Steady state
* Cheek maintenance svakih 2–4 dana
* Circle trim svakih 5–10 dana

**Total time to achieve**: 3–4 sedmice (0.75–1 mjesec)
**Minimum growth required**: 2 cm (≈ 1.5–2 sedmice)

## Transition Paths

### 1. Clean Shaven → Circle Beard
* **Duration**: 3–4 sedmice
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Key steps**:
  1. Sedmica 1–2: Grow, define zone
  2. Sedmica 3: Shape circular boundary
  3. Sedmica 4+: Maintenance mode

### 2. Goatee → Circle Beard
* **Duration**: 2–3 sedmice
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Grow mustache if not present
  2. Connect mustache to goatee at corners
  3. Shape rounded boundary
  4. Same cheek maintenance

### 3. Full Beard → Circle Beard
* **Duration**: 1 session
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Shave cheeks completely
  2. Define circular boundary
  3. Maintain connected mustache-goatee
  4. Trim to desired length

### 4. Van Dyke → Circle Beard
* **Duration**: 2–3 sedmice
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Let mustache grow toward goatee
  2. Connect at corners
  3. Round off pointed chin
  4. Lower maintenance

### 5. Circle Beard → Van Dyke
* **Duration**: 1 session + 2–3 sedmice refinement
* **Difficulty**: Medium
* **Steps**:
  1. Create gap between mustache and goatee
  2. Form pointed chin shape
  3. Style mustache separately
  4. Higher maintenance begins

### 6. Circle Beard → Full Beard
* **Duration**: 8–12 sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Stop shaving cheeks
  2. Let full face grow
  3. At 5+ cm: shape full beard
