# 5 O'Clock Shadow – Inženjerski profil i karakteristike

5 O'Clock Shadow je najkraća forma facial hair-a koja se smatra "stilom" – subtilna sjenka koja se pojavljuje nekoliko sati nakon brijanja. Ime dolazi od tipičnog vremena (17:00) kada je vidljiva sjenka nakon juternjeg brijanja. Ovo je "barely there" look koji dodaje hint muževnosti bez commitment-a brade ili čak stubble-a. Najteži je za održavati konzistentno jer zahtijeva precizno timing ili svakodnevno brijanje.

---

## Ključne dimenzije i proporcije

* **Dužina**: 0.5–2 mm
  → Optimalno: 1–1.5 mm (visible shadow)
  → <0.5 mm: Praktično clean-shaven
  → >2 mm: Prelazi u designer stubble

* **Coverage**: Full face, uniforman
  → Light, consistent shadow
  → Individual hairs barely distinguishable
  → More "shadow" than "hair"

* **Uniformnost**: Critical
  → Varijacija: ±0.3 mm maximum
  → At this length, inconsistency very visible
  → Patchy areas create uneven shadow

* **Cheek line**: Natural/undefined
  → At this length, defined lines look unnatural
  → Natural growth pattern preserved
  → Shadow follows natural boundaries

* **Neckline**: Usually natural ili very soft
  → Hard neckline at 1 mm looks odd
  → Natural fade most appropriate
  → Some define very subtly

* **Mustache area**: Included, same shadow
  → Part of overall face shadow
  → No distinct mustache presence

* **Projekcija volumena**: 0–2%
  → Praktično flat
  → Shadow effect, ne volume
  → Texture more than projection

* **Tekstura**:
  → Fill factor: 70%+ works (shadow masks patchiness)
  → Individual hairs nearly invisible
  → Color creates shadow effect

---

## Održavanje – Kontrolne tačke

* **Trim/Shave interval**:
  * Option A (Natural): Shave morning, 5 o'clock shadow by evening
  * Option B (Maintained): Trim to 1 mm svakih 1–2 dana
  * **Ukupno**: Visoko održavanje (daily attention)

* **Alati**:
  * High-precision trimmer sa 0.5–1 mm setting
  * Electric shaver za very short trim
  * Foil shaver za controlled length
  * Razor za clean shave (option A approach)

* **Proizvodi**:
  * Pre-shave oil: Za smooth skin
  * Post-shave balm: Soothing, daily use
  * Moisturizer: Essential za skin health
  * Exfoliating scrub: 3× sedmično

* **Vrijeme po sessiji**: 3–5 minuta (trimming) ili 10–15 minuta (shaving)
* **Sedmični time investment**: 20–35 minuta (trim approach) ili 70+ minuta (shave approach)

## Razlike vs slični stilovi

| Karakteristika | 5 O'Clock Shadow | Designer Stubble | Clean Shaven | Heavy Stubble |
|----------------|------------------|------------------|--------------|---------------|
| **Dužina** | 0.5–2 mm | 2–4 mm | 0 mm | 4–6 mm |
| **Visibility** | Subtle shadow | Clear stubble | None | Strong shadow |
| **Maintenance** | Daily/High | 2–3 dana | Daily | 3–4 dana |
| **Texture feel** | Sandpaper | Prickly | Smooth | Coarse |
| **Overall vibe** | End-of-day rugged | Groomed stubble | Clean/smooth | Almost-beard |

### Ključne distinkcije:
* **5 O'Clock Shadow vs Designer Stubble**: 5 O'Clock je kraći, subtilniji, requires daily maintenance.
* **5 O'Clock Shadow vs Clean Shaven**: 5 O'Clock ima visible shadow; clean shaven je smooth.
* **5 O'Clock Shadow vs Heavy Stubble**: 5 O'Clock je barely there; Heavy Stubble je obvious facial hair.

---

## Dizajn pravila za autentičan 5 O'Clock Shadow

1. **0.5–2 mm range je strict** – Kraće = clean shaven, duže = stubble. Very narrow window.

2. **Uniformity is everything** – At this length, any inconsistency is magnified. Even coverage critical.

3. **Avoid defined lines** – Natural boundaries only. Sculpted lines look artificial at shadow length.

4. **Daily attention required** – Cannot maintain 5 o'clock look without daily management.

5. **Natural timing option** – Shave morning, natural 5 o'clock shadow by evening works for some.

6. **Trimmer precision essential** – Need equipment that can maintain 1 mm precisely.

7. **Skin condition matters** – Shadow at skin level exposes skin. Skincare is non-negotiable.

8. **Patchy is less obvious** – Shadow effect tends to even out patchy growth better than longer stubble.

## Growth Timeline

**Od clean shaven do 5 O'Clock Shadow target (1.5 mm):**

### Approach A: Natural Timing
* **Dan 1 (Morning)**:
  → Clean shave
  → 0 mm
* **Dan 1 (Afternoon/Evening)**:
  → Natural growth: 0.3–0.5 mm
  → 5 o'clock shadow visible
  → Target achieved naturally

### Approach B: Maintained Trim
* **Dan 1**:
  → Expected growth: ~0.4 mm
* **Dan 2–3**:
  → Expected growth: ~1–1.5 mm
  → Target length achieved
  → Trim to maintain
* **Ongoing**:
  → Trim to 1–1.5 mm daily ili svaki drugi dan

**Total time to achieve**: Hours (natural) ili 2–3 dana (from clean shaven to maintained)
**Minimum growth required**: 0.5 mm

## Transition Paths

### 1. Clean Shaven → 5 O'Clock Shadow
* **Duration**: Hours to 1–2 dana
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Key steps**:
  1. Stop shaving ili shave in morning
  2. By evening ili next day: shadow visible
  3. Maintain with daily/every-other-day trim

### 2. Designer Stubble → 5 O'Clock Shadow
* **Duration**: 1 session
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Trim from 3 mm to 1 mm
  2. Increase maintenance frequency
  3. More subtle look achieved

### 3. 5 O'Clock Shadow → Designer Stubble
* **Duration**: 1–2 dana
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Let grow from 1 mm to 3 mm
  2. Decrease maintenance frequency
  3. More visible stubble

### 4. 5 O'Clock Shadow → Clean Shaven
* **Duration**: 1 session
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Shave completely
  2. Return to clean-shaven routine
  3. Daily shaving required

## Maintenance Strategies

### Strategy 1: Morning Shave, Natural Shadow
* Shave every morning
* Natural shadow appears by afternoon/evening
* No trimming needed
* Requires daily shaving discipline

### Strategy 2: Electric Trimmer Maintenance
* Use foil trimmer ili precision stubble trimmer
* Set to 1 mm guard
* Trim svaki 1–2 dana
* More consistent than natural approach

### Strategy 3: Hybrid Approach
* Shave to zero 2× sedmično
* Let grow to 1–2 mm between shaves
* Cycle between clean and shadow

## Assumptions

* Prosječna stopa rasta: 0.3–0.5 mm/dan
* Facial hair color: Darker hair shows shadow better; light/gray hair less visible
* Skin sensitivity: Daily shaving ili trimming may cause irritation
* Commitment: High – requires daily attention
* Equipment: Precision trimmer ili quality razor essential
* Skin care: Non-negotiable at this length
