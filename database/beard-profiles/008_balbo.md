# Balbo – Inženjerski profil i karakteristike

Balbo je distinctive, sculptured beard stil koji kombinuje tri odvojena elementa: brkove, soul patch (mouche), i bradu na bradi (chin beard) – ali bez povezivanja sa sideburns. Nazvan po italijanskom maršalu Italu Balbu, ovaj stil je prepoznatljiv po svom "floating" chin beard efektu i precizno definisanim granicama. Balbo zahtijeva visoko održavanje i precizno oblikovanje, ali proizvodi sophisticated, intentional izgled koji se ističe u moru generic full bearda.

---

## Ključne dimenzije i proporcije

* **Dužina chin bearda** (od donje usne do najniže tačke): 3–8 cm
  → Optimalno: 4–6 cm za balans prisutnosti i neatness
  → <3 cm: Prelazi u "extended goatee" teritoriju
  → >8 cm: Neobično za Balbo – gubi proportioned look

* **Širina chin bearda** (na jaw line): 8–14 cm
  → Pokriva chin i dijelove jawline
  → Extends 1–3 cm iza uglova usana na svakoj strani
  → **Ne doseže do sideburns** – ključna karakteristika

* **Donja kontura**: Definisana, može biti rounded ili slightly pointed
  → Shape: Obično follows natural chin shape
  → Rounded Balbo: Radius 4–6 cm
  → Pointed Balbo: Subtle V-shape, angle 50–70°
  → Nema dramatic point kao Ducktail

* **Cheek area**: POTPUNO ČISTA
  → **Nema dlaka na obrazima** – dealbreaker za Balbo klasifikaciju
  → Clean shaved od sideburns do chin beard edge
  → Ovo je ono što razlikuje Balbo od Full Beard varijanti

* **Gap between beard and sideburns**: OBAVEZAN
  → Minimum gap: 2–4 cm čiste kože
  → Sideburns: Mogu postojati, ali ne smiju se spojiti sa chin beard
  → Ovaj "floating" efekt je ključna estetska karakteristika

* **Brkovi (Mustache)**: Odvojeni, styled
  → **Ne dodiruju bradu** – postoji gap ili samo tanak connection
  → Stil: Natural full ili shaped (Chevron, Hungarian, ili slično)
  → Dužina: Do 1 cm preko uglova usana
  → Može se koristiti wax za styling

* **Soul patch (Mouche)**: Opcionalno, često prisutno
  → Pozicija: Direktno ispod donje usne
  → Veličina: 1–2 cm širina, 1–2 cm dužina
  → Povezuje brkove sa bradom (ili može biti odvojen)

* **Projekcija volumena**: +15–30% donjeg lica
  → Moderate enhancement, focused na chin area
  → Depth: 2–4 cm na bradi

* **Tekstura i gustina**:
  → Fill factor: >90% na chin beard za clean look
  → Stray hairs: Zero tolerance na obrazima
  → Preferred texture: Any – stil funkcionira sa svim teksturama

## Održavanje – Kontrolne tačke

* **Trim interval**:
  * Chin beard dužina: Svakih 7–10 dana
  * Cheek shave (čisti obrazi): Svakih 2–3 dana (KRITIČNO)
  * Brkovi: Svakih 5–7 dana
  * Beard/mustache gap: Svakih 3–5 dana
  * Donja kontura: Svakih 7–10 dana
  * Neckline: Svakih 5–7 dana
  * **Ukupno**: Visoko održavanje (4–5 sessiona sedmično)

* **Alati**:
  * Precision trimmer (0 mm blade) – za gap i edge definition
  * Razor ili safety razor – za cheek shaving
  * Trimmer sa guards (4–10 mm) – za chin beard length
  * Beard scissors – za fine-tuning
  * Fine-tooth comb
  * Mustache wax (opcionalno) – za mustache styling

* **Proizvodi**:
  * Beard oil: 2–4 kapi dnevno (na chin beard)
  * Beard balm: Za hold i definition
  * Shaving cream/gel: Za cheek shaving
  * Aftershave balm: Za cheek area
  * Mustache wax: Opcionalno

* **Vrijeme po sessiji**: 12–18 minuta
* **Sedmični time investment**: 50–80 minuta

## Razlike vs slični stilovi

| Karakteristika | Balbo | Van Dyke | Goatee | Anchor | Circle Beard |
|----------------|-------|----------|--------|--------|--------------|
| **Chin beard** | Wide (jaw coverage) | Narrow (chin only) | Yes | Yes + pointed | Yes |
| **Mustache** | Separated/styled | Separated | Optional | Thin line | Connected |
| **Soul patch** | Often | Often | Often | Part of design | Connected |
| **Cheeks** | Clean shaved | Clean shaved | Clean shaved | Clean shaved | Clean shaved |
| **Sideburn connection** | NO | NO | NO | NO | NO |
| **Width** | Wide (8–14 cm) | Narrow (4–8 cm) | Narrow | Medium | Medium |
| **Maintenance** | High | High | Medium | Very High | Medium |
| **Overall vibe** | Distinguished bold | Artistic classic | Minimalist | Nautical precise | Casual neat |

### Ključne distinkcije:
* **Balbo vs Van Dyke**: Balbo ima širu chin beard coverage (extends onto jawline); Van Dyke je užiji, focused samo na chin point.
* **Balbo vs Goatee**: Balbo je wider i uvijek ima styled mustache; Goatee je minimal chin coverage, mustache optional.
* **Balbo vs Anchor**: Anchor ima distinctive pointed extensions i thin connecting line; Balbo ima fuller, more natural shape.
* **Balbo vs Circle Beard**: Circle Beard ima connected mustache-to-chin; Balbo ima gap ili minimal connection.

## Dizajn pravila za autentičan Balbo

1. **Obrazi moraju biti potpuno čisti** – Bilo kakve dlake na obrazima (osim sideburns) poništavaju Balbo klasifikaciju. Clean shave svakih 2–3 dana.

2. **Gap između braka i brade** – Brkovi i brada ne smiju se potpuno stapati. Mora postojati vidljiv gap ili samo tanka "bridge" preko soul patcha.

3. **Beard ne dodiruje sideburns** – Chin beard mora "lebdjeti" odvojeno od sideburns. Minimum 2 cm gap na svakoj strani.

4. **Širina pokriva jaw, ne samo chin** – Balbo se razlikuje od Goatee širinom. Mora se proširiti 1–3 cm iza uglova usana.

5. **Brkovi su styling element** – Za razliku od integrated beard stilova, Balbo mustache može (i često treba) biti styled zasebno.

6. **Simetrija je apsolutno kritična** – Asimetrični Balbo izgleda kao greška. Chin beard, mustache, i gap moraju biti perfectly balanced.

7. **Donja kontura je defined ali ne extreme** – Balbo nije Ducktail. Blago zaobljen ili subtle point, ne dramatic V.

8. **Soul patch kao bridge** – Ako postoji soul patch, koristi ga kao vizualnu vezu između mustache i chin beard.

## Face Shape Compatibility

| Oblik lica | Score | Obrazloženje |
|------------|-------|--------------|
| **Oval** | 90/100 | Odličan. Balbo's width adds interest bez disrupting balanced proportions. |
| **Square** | 85/100 | Dobro. Wide chin beard complements strong jaw. Gap adds definition. |
| **Round** | 80/100 | Funkcionira. Width on chin can elongate visually. Clean cheeks help define structure. |
| **Triangle** | 75/100 | OK, ali pažljivo. Wide jaw + wide Balbo može biti previše. Consider narrower Balbo variant. |
| **Diamond** | 95/100 | Savršeno. Balbo adds chin width to balance prominent cheekbones. Ideal match. |
| **Oblong** | 70/100 | Risky. Width helps, ali length can elongate further. Keep chin beard short (3–4 cm). |

**Best match**: Diamond (95/100)
**Excellent**: Oval (90/100)
**Good**: Square (85/100)
**Moderate**: Round, Triangle (75–80/100)
**Careful**: Oblong (70/100)

## Growth Timeline

**Od clean shaven do Balbo target (5 cm chin beard):**

### Phase 1: Full Growth (Sedmica 1–4)
* **Sedmica 1–2**:
  → Expected growth: ~2 cm
  → Grow everything – full face
  → Nema shaving još
* **Sedmica 3–4**:
  → Expected growth: +2 cm (total: ~4 cm)
  → Continue full growth
  → Approaching shapeable length

### Phase 2: Initial Shaping (Sedmica 5–6)
* **Sedmica 5**:
  → Expected growth: ~5 cm
  → MAJOR SHAPING SESSION:
  → Shave cheeks completely
  → Create gap between beard and sideburns
  → Define chin beard edges
  → Create mustache/beard separation
* **Sedmica 6**:
  → Refine all edges
  → Perfect symmetry
  → Style mustache separately

### Phase 3: Maintenance (Sedmica 7+)
* Steady state achieved
* Cheek shaving svakih 2–3 dana (critical)
* Chin beard trim svakih 7–10 dana
* Mustache styling ongoing
* Gap maintenance svakih 3–5 dana

**Total time to achieve**: 5–6 sedmica (1.25–1.5 mjeseca)
**Minimum growth required**: 4 cm chin (≈ 3–4 sedmice growth pre-shaping)

## Transition Paths

### 1. Clean Shaven → Balbo
* **Duration**: 5–6 sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Medium-Hard
* **Key steps**:
  1. Sedmica 1–4: Grow full beard, no shaping
  2. Sedmica 5: Major shaping – shave cheeks, create gaps
  3. Sedmica 6: Refine edges and symmetry
  4. Sedmica 7+: High-maintenance routine begins

### 2. Full Beard → Balbo
* **Duration**: 1–2 sessiona
* **Difficulty**: Medium (significant removal)
* **Steps**:
  1. Session 1: Shave cheeks completely
  2. Create gap from sideburns
  3. Separate mustache from chin
  4. Define chin beard edges
  5. Immediate high-maintenance begins

### 3. Goatee → Balbo
* **Duration**: 3–4 sedmice
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Grow out chin beard wider
  2. Extend to cover more of jawline
  3. Already have clean cheeks (advantage)
  4. Style mustache separately

### 4. Van Dyke → Balbo
* **Duration**: 3–4 sedmice
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Grow chin beard wider
  2. Extend onto jawline
  3. Already have correct separation
  4. Minor shape adjustment

### 5. Balbo → Van Dyke
* **Duration**: 1 session
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Narrow chin beard
  2. Remove jawline coverage
  3. Focus on chin point
  4. Maintain separation

### 6. Balbo → Full Beard
* **Duration**: 6–10 sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Easy (just growing)
* **Steps**:
  1. Stop shaving cheeks
  2. Let sideburns connect to chin
  3. Stop separating mustache
  4. At 5+ cm cheeks: full beard territory

## Assumptions

* Prosječna stopa rasta: 1.1–1.3 cm/mjesečno
* Cheek hair: Profil pretpostavlja ability to grow full cheeks (za initial growth phase)
* Maintenance commitment: Mora biti spreman na shaving obraza svakih 2–3 dana
* Styling skill: Moderate skill required za symetry i edge definition
* Facial hair distribution: Chin growth mora biti dovoljna za width; patchy chin = problematično
* Time investment: 50–80 minuta sedmično je non-negotiable za maintained look
