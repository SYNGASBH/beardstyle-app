# Hulihee – Inženjerski profil i karakteristike

Hulihee (izgovara se "hoo-lee-hee") je dramatic, theatrical beard style originalno povezan sa Hawaiianskim kraljevstvom 19. vijeka, posebno kraljem Kalākaua. Ovaj jedinstveni stil karakteriziraju massive sideburns koji se šire prema van u wing-like formacije, dok je chin area clean-shaven. To je essentially "inverse beard" – volumne tamo gdje većina stilova nema, i praznina tamo gdje većina stilova ima dlake.

---

## Ključne dimenzije i proporcije

* **Sideburns – GLAVNA KARAKTERISTIKA**:
  → Širina na vrhu (ear level): 3–5 cm
  → Širina na dnu (jaw level): 8–15 cm (EXPANSION!)
  → Dužina: Od ear do jaw, 8–12 cm vertikalno
  → Shape: Wing-like, fan expansion
  → Volume: MAKSIMALAN – thick, full, bushy
  → Direction: Outward flare, away from face

* **Cheek coverage**: Extensive
  → Full cheek coverage od sideburns
  → Merges with sideburns seamlessly
  → No distinct boundary between sideburns i cheeks

* **Chin area**: CLEAN SHAVEN
  → Ovo je KRITIČNA karakteristika
  → No hair on chin whatsoever
  → Clean contrast to massive sideburns

* **Mustache**: Variable
  → Can be present ili absent
  → If present: Typically connected to sideburns
  → Style: Full, natural
  → Lip clearance: Variable

* **Jawline**: Partially covered
  → Sideburns extend along upper jaw
  → Lower jaw near chin: Clean
  → Transition zone where sideburns end

* **Neckline**: Natural ili defined
  → Can follow sideburn expansion
  → Clean below jaw

* **Projekcija volumena**: +40–60% side profile
  → MASSIVE horizontal expansion
  → Creates wider face appearance
  → Depth: 4–8 cm od lica na widest point

* **Tekstura i gustina**:
  → Fill factor: >90% za impressive wings
  → Volume: CRITICAL – thin hulihee nije pravi hulihee
  → Texture: Any, but wavy/curly adds natural volume

## Održavanje – Kontrolne tačke

* **Trim interval**:
  * Wing shaping: Svakih 7–14 dana
  * Chin shaving: Svakih 1–2 dana (CRITICAL)
  * Mustache (if present): Svakih 5–7 dana
  * Neckline: Svakih 5–7 dana
  * Volume control: Svakih 7–10 dana
  * **Ukupno**: Srednje-visoko održavanje (chin daily + 2–3 sessiona sedmično)

* **Alati**:
  * Trimmer sa guards (6–15 mm) – za wing shaping
  * Razor – za chin area (CRITICAL)
  * Beard brush – za volume i direction training
  * Wide-tooth comb – za detangling
  * Scissors – za stray hairs
  * Blow dryer – za volume (optional)

* **Proizvodi**:
  * Beard oil: 5–8 kapi dnevno (large coverage area)
  * Beard balm: Za shape control
  * Volume spray/powder: Optional za extra fullness
  * Shaving cream: Za chin area
  * Aftershave: Za chin

* **Vrijeme po sessiji**: 15–25 minuta
* **Sedmični time investment**: 60–90 minuta

## Razlike vs slični stilovi

| Karakteristika | Hulihee | Mutton Chops | Friendly Mutton Chops | Sideburns | Full Beard |
|----------------|---------|--------------|----------------------|-----------|------------|
| **Sideburn size** | MASSIVE wings | Large | Large | Small-medium | Part of beard |
| **Expansion** | Dramatic outward | Moderate | Moderate | Minimal | N/A |
| **Chin** | Clean | Clean | Clean | Clean | Covered |
| **Mustache** | Optional | No | Yes (connected) | Optional | Yes |
| **Width** | 8–15 cm per side | 4–8 cm | 4–8 cm | 1–3 cm | N/A |
| **Volume** | Maximum | High | High | Low-medium | Variable |
| **Overall vibe** | Theatrical Hawaiian | Victorian | Victorian friendly | Classic | Traditional |

### Ključne distinkcije:
* **Hulihee vs Mutton Chops**: Hulihee ima EXTREME outward expansion; Mutton Chops su more contained.
* **Hulihee vs Friendly Mutton Chops**: Hulihee ima dramatic wing shape; Friendly je rounder i less theatrical.
* **Hulihee vs Full Beard**: Hulihee ima clean chin; Full Beard ima chin coverage.
* **Hulihee vs Sideburns**: Hulihee ima massive volume; Sideburns su typically narrow.

---

## Dizajn pravila za autentičan Hulihee

1. **Wings moraju EXPAND OUTWARD** – Contained sideburns nisu Hulihee. Dramatic flare je essential.

2. **Chin mora biti COMPLETELY CLEAN** – Any chin hair negates Hulihee character. Daily chin maintenance.

3. **Volume je EVERYTHING** – Thin, wispy sideburns nisu Hulihee. Need thick, full growth.

4. **Symmetry u wing size** – Both sides must expand equally. Asymmetric wings su obvious error.

5. **Width must be DRAMATIC** – Subtle nije Hulihee. If people don't notice, it's not wide enough.

6. **Train hair OUTWARD** – Hair naturally grows down; Hulihee requires training growth outward.

7. **Embrace the theatrical** – Hulihee je statement style. Subtlety defeats the purpose.

8. **Cheek coverage connects to wings** – No gap between cheeks i sideburns.

## Face Shape Compatibility

| Oblik lica | Score | Obrazloženje |
|------------|-------|--------------|
| **Oval** | 75/100 | Oprez. Wings add significant width to balanced face. Consider carefully. |
| **Square** | 70/100 | Risky. Already wide jaw + wings = very wide appearance. |
| **Round** | 65/100 | Problematic. Adds more width to already wide face. Not recommended. |
| **Triangle** | 60/100 | Difficult. Emphasizes already wide jaw significantly. |
| **Diamond** | 85/100 | Dobro. Cheekbones balance wings. Most compatible narrow face. |
| **Oblong** | 95/100 | Idealno! Horizontal expansion balances long face. Best match. |

**Best match**: Oblong (95/100)
**Good**: Diamond (85/100)
**Moderate**: Oval (75/100)
**Careful**: Square (70/100)
**Challenging**: Round, Triangle (60–65/100)

## Growth Timeline

**Od clean shaven do Hulihee:**

### Phase 1: Full Sideburn Growth (Sedmica 1–8)
* **Sedmica 1–2**:
  → Expected growth: ~2 cm
  → Grow full face first
  → No shaping yet
* **Sedmica 3–4**:
  → Growth: ~4 cm
  → Continue full growth
  → Start brushing outward direction
* **Sedmica 5–6**:
  → Growth: ~6 cm
  → Beginning visible volume
  → Train hair direction daily
* **Sedmica 7–8**:
  → Growth: ~8 cm
  → Approaching minimum Hulihee length
  → Can begin preliminary shaping

### Phase 2: Wing Formation (Sedmica 9–12)
* **Sedmica 9–10**:
  → Growth: ~10 cm
  → MAJOR SHAPING SESSION:
  → Shave chin completely
  → Shape wing outlines
  → Define expansion zones
* **Sedmica 11–12**:
  → Refine wing shapes
  → Maximize outward direction
  → Establish chin shaving routine

### Phase 3: Dramatic Expansion (Sedmica 13–16+)
* **Sedmica 13–14**:
  → Growth: ~12–14 cm
  → Wings becoming dramatic
  → Full Hulihee taking shape
* **Sedmica 15–16**:
  → Full theatrical Hulihee
  → Maximum expansion achieved
  → Maintenance mode

**Total time to achieve**: 12–16 sedmica (3–4 mjeseca)
**Minimum sideburn length**: 8 cm za basic wings
**Full theatrical Hulihee**: 12+ cm

## Transition Paths

### 1. Clean Shaven → Hulihee
* **Duration**: 12–16 sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Hard
* **Key steps**:
  1. Sedmica 1–8: Grow full face, train direction
  2. Sedmica 9–10: Major shaping, establish wings
  3. Sedmica 11–12: Refine and expand
  4. Sedmica 13–16: Achieve full theatrical effect

### 2. Full Beard → Hulihee
* **Duration**: 1–2 sessiona (if length sufficient)
* **Difficulty**: Medium
* **Steps**:
  1. Shave chin completely
  2. Shape sideburns into wing forms
  3. Train outward direction
  4. Maintain chin daily

### 3. Mutton Chops → Hulihee
* **Duration**: 4–8 sedmica (growth for expansion)
* **Difficulty**: Easy-Medium
* **Steps**:
  1. Already have sideburn foundation
  2. Grow wider and longer
  3. Train extreme outward expansion
  4. Maximize volume and flare

### 4. Friendly Mutton Chops → Hulihee
* **Duration**: 4–8 sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Easy-Medium
* **Steps**:
  1. Remove mustache (if desired)
  2. Grow sideburns wider
  3. Shape into wing expansion
  4. Maintain chin shaving

### 5. Hulihee → Mutton Chops
* **Duration**: 1 session
* **Difficulty**: Easy (reducing)
* **Steps**:
  1. Trim wing expansion
  2. Create more contained shape
  3. Less dramatic width
  4. Similar maintenance

### 6. Hulihee → Full Beard
* **Duration**: 6–10 sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Stop shaving chin
  2. Let chin grow
  3. Trim sideburns to beard proportion
  4. Full beard develops
