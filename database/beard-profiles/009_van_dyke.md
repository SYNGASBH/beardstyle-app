# Van Dyke – Inženjerski profil i karakteristike

Van Dyke je klasični, umjetnički beard stil nazvan po flamanskom slikaru Anthonyju van Dycku iz 17. vijeka. Karakterizira ga odvojena kombinacija pointed goatee-a i brkova, sa potpuno čistim obrazima. Za razliku od connected stilova poput Circle Beard-a, Van Dyke naglašava razdvojenost elemenata – brkovi i brada su dva zasebna, pažljivo oblikovana elementa koji zajedno stvaraju sophisticated, aristokratski izgled.

---

## Ključne dimenzije i proporcije

* **Dužina goatee-a** (od donje usne do najniže tačke): 4–10 cm
  → Optimalno: 5–7 cm za klasični look
  → <4 cm: Prelazi u stubble goatee teritoriju
  → >10 cm: Extended Van Dyke, manje tradicionalan

* **Širina goatee-a** (na najširoj tački, obično na chin): 4–8 cm
  → Pokriva chin, ne extends značajno na jawline
  → **Uža od Balbo** – fokusiran na chin, ne jaw coverage

* **Donja kontura**: Pointed, definisana
  → Shape: Karakteristični point na bradi
  → Point angle: 30–50° (oštar ali ne extreme)
  → Point mora biti na midline lica, centered
  → Može biti subtle point ili pronounced point

* **Cheek area**: POTPUNO ČISTA
  → **Nema dlaka na obrazima**
  → Clean shaved od sideburns do goatee edge
  → Ovo je esencijalna Van Dyke karakteristika

* **Gap between goatee and sideburns**: OBAVEZAN
  → Minimum gap: 3–5 cm čiste kože
  → Sideburns: Mogu postojati (kratki), ne smiju se spojiti
  → Goatee "lebdi" na bradi

* **Brkovi (Mustache)**: ODVOJENI od brade
  → **Ne dodiruju goatee** – čist gap između
  → Gap širina: 0.5–2 cm
  → Stil: Može biti handlebar, chevron, pencil, ili natural
  → Wax: Često korišten za styling, posebno za handlebar varijante
  → Dužina: Varira – od 0.5 cm preko usne do 3+ cm za handlebar

* **Soul patch**: Opcionalno
  → Može postojati kao vizualni bridge
  → Ili može biti potpuno odsutan (gap)
  → Ako postoji: 1–1.5 cm širina

* **Projekcija volumena**: +10–25% donjeg lica
  → Focused enhancement na chin area
  → Depth: 2–5 cm na goatee point

* **Tekstura i gustina**:
  → Fill factor: >90% na goatee za clean pointed look
  → Stray hairs: Zero tolerance na obrazima
  → Preferred texture: Straight-to-wavy za defined point

## Održavanje – Kontrolne tačke

* **Trim interval**:
  * Goatee shape: Svakih 5–7 dana
  * Point definition: Svakih 5–7 dana
  * Cheek shave: Svakih 2–3 dana (KRITIČNO)
  * Brkovi: Svakih 3–5 dana
  * Mustache/goatee gap: Svakih 3–4 dana
  * **Ukupno**: Visoko održavanje (4–5 sessiona sedmično)

* **Alati**:
  * Precision trimmer (0 mm blade) – za edges i gap
  * Razor/safety razor – za cheek shaving
  * Trimmer sa guards (4–12 mm) – za goatee length
  * Beard scissors – za point shaping
  * Fine-tooth comb – za goatee styling
  * Mustache wax – za mustache styling
  * Mustache comb – za precise styling

* **Proizvodi**:
  * Beard oil: 2–4 kapi dnevno (na goatee)
  * Mustache wax: Za styling (ako handlebar ili shaped)
  * Beard balm: Za goatee hold
  * Shaving cream/gel: Za cheek shaving
  * Aftershave balm: Za cheek area

* **Vrijeme po sessiji**: 12–18 minuta
* **Sedmični time investment**: 55–75 minuta

## Razlike vs slični stilovi

| Karakteristika | Van Dyke | Balbo | Goatee | Anchor | Musketeer |
|----------------|----------|-------|--------|--------|-----------|
| **Goatee width** | Narrow (4–8 cm) | Wide (8–14 cm) | Narrow | Medium | Narrow |
| **Goatee shape** | Pointed | Rounded/any | Variable | Pointed + extensions | Very pointed |
| **Mustache** | Separated, styled | Separated | Optional | Thin line down | Long, upturned |
| **Gap mustache-beard** | Yes (required) | Yes or minimal | Variable | Thin connection | Yes |
| **Cheeks** | Clean shaved | Clean shaved | Clean shaved | Clean shaved | Clean shaved |
| **Jaw coverage** | No | Yes | No | No | No |
| **Maintenance** | High | High | Medium | Very High | High |
| **Overall vibe** | Artistic classic | Distinguished bold | Minimalist | Nautical precise | Theatrical |

### Ključne distinkcije:
* **Van Dyke vs Balbo**: Van Dyke je uži, focused na chin point; Balbo pokriva jawline. Van Dyke uvijek ima separated mustache.
* **Van Dyke vs Goatee**: Van Dyke uvijek ima styled, separated mustache; Goatee može biti bez brkova.
* **Van Dyke vs Anchor**: Anchor ima thin line extensions; Van Dyke je clean pointed goatee bez extensions.
* **Van Dyke vs Musketeer**: Musketeer ima dramatičnije, duže, upturned brkove i thinner goatee.

## Dizajn pravila za autentičan Van Dyke

1. **Brkovi i brada moraju biti ODVOJENI** – Ovo je non-negotiable. Connected mustache-to-goatee = Circle Beard, ne Van Dyke.

2. **Point mora biti centered i defined** – Off-center ili rounded point nije Van Dyke. Koristi midline lica kao guide.

3. **Obrazi moraju biti potpuno čisti** – Bilo kakve dlake na obrazima poništavaju Van Dyke. Clean shave svakih 2–3 dana.

4. **Goatee je narrow, ne wide** – Van Dyke goatee pokriva chin, ne extends na jawline. Širi od 8 cm = Balbo territory.

5. **Brkovi su statement element** – Van Dyke brkovi su often styled (handlebar, chevron). Plain stubble mustache nije authentic Van Dyke.

6. **Simetrija je kritična** – Point, brkovi, i gap moraju biti perfectly balanced.

7. **Gap mora biti vidljiv** – Ako je gap između brkova i goatee-a manje od 5 mm, to je de facto connected.

8. **Maintain artistic intention** – Van Dyke je "painter's beard" – mora izgledati namjerno oblikovan, ne slučajno.

## Face Shape Compatibility

| Oblik lica | Score | Obrazloženje |
|------------|-------|--------------|
| **Oval** | 90/100 | Odličan. Pointed goatee adds interest bez disrupting balanced proportions. |
| **Square** | 85/100 | Dobro. Point softens angular chin. Adds artistic flair to strong features. |
| **Round** | 95/100 | Idealno. Point elongates round face vizualno. Best match za Van Dyke. |
| **Triangle** | 70/100 | Oprez. Već pointed chin + pointed goatee može biti "too much point". |
| **Diamond** | 90/100 | Radi odlično. Point balances narrow chin, complements cheekbones. |
| **Oblong** | 65/100 | Risky. Point može dodatno elongirati već dugo lice. Consider rounded style. |

**Best match**: Round (95/100)
**Excellent**: Oval, Diamond (90/100)
**Good**: Square (85/100)
**Careful**: Triangle (70/100)
**Avoid**: Oblong (65/100)

## Growth Timeline

**Od clean shaven do Van Dyke target (6 cm goatee):**

### Phase 1: Full Face Growth (Sedmica 1–4)
* **Sedmica 1–2**:
  → Expected growth: ~2 cm
  → Grow entire face – NO shaving
  → Resist urge to shape
* **Sedmica 3–4**:
  → Expected growth: +2 cm (total: ~4 cm)
  → Continue full growth
  → Minimum shapeable length approaching

### Phase 2: Initial Shaping (Sedmica 5–6)
* **Sedmica 5**:
  → Expected growth: ~5 cm
  → MAJOR SHAPING SESSION:
  → Shave cheeks completely
  → Define goatee boundaries
  → Create pointed shape
  → Separate mustache
  → Create visible gap
* **Sedmica 6**:
  → Expected growth: ~6 cm
  → Refine point
  → Style mustache
  → Perfect symmetry

### Phase 3: Maintenance (Sedmica 7+)
* Steady state achieved
* Cheek shaving svakih 2–3 dana
* Goatee trim svakih 5–7 dana
* Gap maintenance svakih 3–4 dana
* Mustache styling ongoing

**Total time to achieve**: 5–6 sedmica (1.25–1.5 mjeseca)
**Minimum growth required**: 4 cm goatee (≈ 3–4 sedmice pre-shaping)

## Transition Paths

### 1. Clean Shaven → Van Dyke
* **Duration**: 5–6 sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Medium-Hard
* **Key steps**:
  1. Sedmica 1–4: Grow full beard, no shaping
  2. Sedmica 5: Major shaping – shave cheeks, create point, separate mustache
  3. Sedmica 6: Refine point, style mustache
  4. Sedmica 7+: High-maintenance routine

### 2. Full Beard → Van Dyke
* **Duration**: 1–2 sessiona
* **Difficulty**: Medium
* **Steps**:
  1. Shave cheeks completely
  2. Narrow goatee to chin-width
  3. Form pointed shape
  4. Create mustache-goatee gap
  5. Immediate styling/maintenance

### 3. Goatee → Van Dyke
* **Duration**: 2–4 sedmice (if mustache needs growth)
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Grow out mustache if needed
  2. Already have goatee foundation
  3. Define point more precisely
  4. Style mustache separately
  5. Create visible gap

### 4. Balbo → Van Dyke
* **Duration**: 1 session
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Narrow chin beard
  2. Remove jaw coverage
  3. Form pointed chin
  4. Maintain mustache separation

### 5. Van Dyke → Circle Beard
* **Duration**: 3–4 sedmice
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Let mustache grow toward goatee
  2. Connect at corners
  3. Round off goatee point
  4. Lower maintenance once connected

### 6. Van Dyke → Full Beard
* **Duration**: 6–10 sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Stop shaving cheeks
  2. Let cheeks fill in
  3. Connect mustache to beard
  4. At 5+ cm cheeks: full beard
