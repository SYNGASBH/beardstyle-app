# Boxed Beard – Inženjerski profil i karakteristike

Boxed Beard je strukturirana full beard varijanta sa čistim, geometrijskim linijama koje formiraju "kutijasti" oblik oko donje polovice lica. Za razliku od rounded stilova poput Garibaldija, Boxed Beard naglašava angularne linije, definisane ivice i kontroliranu formu. Ovaj stil kombinuje prisutnost full bearda sa sculptiranom preciznošću – zahtijeva više održavanja od natural stilova, ali proizvodi polished, intentional izgled.

---

## Ključne dimenzije i proporcije

* **Dužina brade** (od donje usne do najniže tačke): 5–10 cm
  → Optimalno: 6–8 cm za balans definicije i volumena
  → <5 cm: Prelazi u Short Boxed teritoriju
  → >10 cm: Ulazi u Full Beard/Garibaldi kategoriju, gubi "boxed" estetiku

* **Širina brade** (na najširoj tački): Jednaka ili blago šira od jawline
  → Target ratio: Width ≈ 1.0–1.15× natural jawline width
  → Sides: Vertikalne ili blago taperirane linije
  → Nema dramatic flare kao kod Garibaldija

* **Donja kontura**: Relativno ravna ili blago zaobljena
  → Shape: Squared-off look, nije pointed kao Ducktail
  → Corners: Blago zaobljeni (radius 1–2 cm), ne oštri kutovi
  → Horizontalna linija dominira nad vertikalnom

* **Cheek line**: Definisana, čista linija
  → Tip: Medium-to-hard line (jasna granica)
  → Pozicija: Na razini sredine uha do 2 cm ispod jagodične kosti
  → Simetrija: Kritična – obje strane moraju biti identične
  → Blend: Minimalan (0–1 cm fade) ili nema fade

* **Brkovi (Mustache)**: Integrisani, groomed
  → Stil: Full, connected sa bradom
  → Dužina: Do uglova usana, ne značajno preko
  → Lip clearance: 1–3 mm iznad gornje usne
  → Konzistentna gustina sa ostatkom brade

* **Projekcija volumena**: +25–40% donjeg lica
  → Moderate enhancement, kontrolirana forma
  → Depth: 2–4 cm uniformno preko cijele brade

* **Tekstura i gustina**:
  → Fill factor: >85% required za clean boxed look
  → Stray hairs: Minimalna tolerancija – narušavaju geometriju
  → Preferred texture: Straight-to-slightly-wavy; curly zahtijeva više styling

## Održavanje – Kontrolne tačke

* **Trim interval**:
  * Dužina (bulk): Svakih 5–7 dana
  * Cheek line: Svakih 3–5 dana
  * Donja kontura: Svakih 5–7 dana
  * Neckline: Svakih 3–5 dana
  * Brkovi: Svakih 3–5 dana
  * **Ukupno**: Srednje-visoko održavanje (3–4 sessiona sedmično)

* **Alati**:
  * Trimmer sa guards (6–12 mm range) – za bulk maintenance
  * Precision trimmer/edger (0 mm blade) – za linije
  * Beard scissors (5 inch) – za fine-tuning
  * Fine-tooth comb – za uniformity check
  * Straight edge/template – opcionalno za perfektne linije

* **Proizvodi**:
  * Beard oil: 4–6 kapi dnevno
  * Beard balm: Za hold i definition, dnevno
  * Beard wash: 2–3× sedmično
  * Aftershave balm: Za shaved areas (neckline, cheeks)

* **Vrijeme po sessiji**: 12–18 minuta
* **Sedmični time investment**: 45–70 minuta maintenance + 5 min daily styling

## Razlike vs slični stilovi

| Karakteristika | Boxed Beard | Short Boxed | Corporate Beard | Full Beard | Garibaldi |
|----------------|-------------|-------------|-----------------|------------|-----------|
| **Dužina** | 5–10 cm | 3–5 cm | 2–5 cm | 5–12 cm | 12–20 cm |
| **Donja kontura** | Squared/flat | Squared/flat | Defined natural | Variable | Wide rounded |
| **Cheek line** | Hard defined | Hard defined | Hard defined | Variable | Natural/soft |
| **Side profile** | Boxy/angular | Boxy/angular | Neat straight | Natural | Wide flare |
| **Maintenance** | Medium-High | Medium-High | High | Medium | Low |
| **Overall vibe** | Structured masculine | Compact structured | Professional neat | Classic | Distinguished wild |

### Ključne distinkcije:
* **Boxed vs Short Boxed**: Boxed je duži (5–10 cm vs 3–5 cm), ima više volumena i prisutnosti. Short Boxed je kompaktnija verzija istog oblika.
* **Boxed vs Corporate**: Corporate je kraći i ima stroži focus na office-appropriate neatness. Boxed dozvoljava više "beardiness".
* **Boxed vs Garibaldi**: Garibaldi ima rounded, wide, natural konturu. Boxed ima angular, defined, structured konturu.
* **Boxed vs Full Beard**: Boxed je specifična varijanta sa definisanim geometrijskim shapom. Generic full beard nema striktnu shape specifikaciju.

## Dizajn pravila za autentičan Boxed Beard

1. **Linije moraju biti čiste i definirane** – Blurry ili uneven linije poništavaju "boxed" estetiku. Cheek line, neckline i donja kontura moraju biti precizne.

2. **Donja kontura je horizontalna, ne pointed** – Ako se formira point na bradi, to je Ducktail. Boxed održava squared-off ili blago curved bottom.

3. **Simetrija je non-negotiable** – Obje strane moraju biti identične. Asimetrija je najčešća greška koja uništava boxed look.

4. **Uniformna dužina preko cijele brade** – Varijacija dužine ±4 mm maksimum. Koristi isti guard setting na cijeloj bradi.

5. **Corners blago zaobljeni** – Oštri 90° kutovi izgledaju neprirodno na licu. Radius 1–2 cm za natural-looking angularnost.

6. **Cheek line konzistentna visina** – Cheek line ne smije "padati" ili "dizati se" kako ide od uha prema nosu. Horizontalna konzistentnost.

7. **Neckline čist i definisan** – Boxed beard zahtijeva clean neckline. 1–2 prsta iznad Adam's apple, clean shave ispod.

8. **Regular maintenance obavezan** – Boxed look se brzo gubi bez održavanja. 5–7 dana bez trim-a = lost definition.

## Face Shape Compatibility

| Oblik lica | Score | Obrazloženje |
|------------|-------|--------------|
| **Oval** | 90/100 | Odličan match. Angular beard adds structure to balanced face without distortion. |
| **Square** | 95/100 | Idealno. Boxed shape komplementira angular jawline. Strong, masculine look. |
| **Round** | 85/100 | Dobro. Angular lines dodaju definition i vizualno izdužuju okruglo lice. |
| **Triangle** | 80/100 | Funkcionira. Brada balansira široku vilicu. Pažnja na width – ne pretjerivati. |
| **Diamond** | 85/100 | Radi dobro. Angular beard complements cheekbones while adding chin definition. |
| **Oblong** | 70/100 | OK, ali oprez. Boxed shape može dodatno elongirati. Keep length on shorter end (5–6 cm). |

**Best match**: Square (95/100)
**Excellent**: Oval, Round, Diamond (85–90/100)
**Good**: Triangle (80/100)
**Careful**: Oblong (<75/100)

## Growth Timeline

**Od clean shaven do Boxed Beard target (7 cm):**

### Phase 1: Foundation Growth (Sedmica 1–4)
* **Sedmica 1–2**:
  → Expected growth: ~2 cm
  → Let it grow, minimal intervention
  → Define neckline early (sedmica 2)
* **Sedmica 3–4**:
  → Expected growth: +2 cm (total: ~4 cm)
  → Start defining cheek line
  → Beginning of shapeable territory

### Phase 2: Initial Shaping (Sedmica 5–7)
* **Sedmica 5**:
  → Expected growth: +1 cm (total: ~5 cm)
  → Minimum boxed length reached
  → First full shaping session
  → Establish squared bottom contour
* **Sedmica 6–7**:
  → Expected growth: +1–2 cm (total: 6–7 cm)
  → Refine all lines
  → Check symmetry
  → Optimal length achieved

### Phase 3: Maintenance (Sedmica 8+)
* Target shape achieved
* Regular maintenance svakih 5–7 dana
* Focus: Keeping lines clean, symmetry perfect
* Product routine established

**Total time to achieve**: 6–8 sedmica (1.5–2 mjeseca)
**Minimum growth required**: 5 cm (≈ 4–5 sedmice)

## Transition Paths

### 1. Clean Shaven → Boxed Beard
* **Duration**: 6–8 sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Medium
* **Key steps**:
  1. Sedmica 1–2: Grow out, define neckline
  2. Sedmica 3–4: Define cheek line
  3. Sedmica 5–6: Shape squared bottom, achieve uniformity
  4. Sedmica 7+: Refine and maintain

### 2. Stubble/Short → Boxed Beard
* **Duration**: 4–6 sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Continue growth to 5+ cm
  2. Maintain existing lines or redefine
  3. Shape squared bottom when length allows
  4. Establish maintenance routine

### 3. Full Beard → Boxed Beard
* **Duration**: 1–2 sessiona (immediate reshaping)
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Trim to uniform 6–8 cm length
  2. Define sharp cheek line
  3. Shape squared/flat bottom contour
  4. Clean up neckline precisely
  5. Immediate maintenance schedule

### 4. Garibaldi → Boxed Beard
* **Duration**: 1–2 sessiona
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Reduce length to 6–8 cm
  2. Change rounded bottom to squared
  3. Define hard cheek line (was soft/natural)
  4. Reduce width to closer to jawline
  5. Higher maintenance schedule begins

### 5. Boxed Beard → Ducktail
* **Duration**: 3–5 sedmica (growing/reshaping)
* **Difficulty**: Medium
* **Steps**:
  1. Let chin grow longer than sides
  2. Gradually form pointed bottom contour
  3. Taper sides inward
  4. May need 8+ cm chin length
  5. Soften cheek line slightly

### 6. Boxed Beard → Short Boxed
* **Duration**: 1 session (immediate)
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Trim length to 3–5 cm
  2. Maintain same angular shape
  3. Keep all lines defined
  4. Lower volume, same structure

## Assumptions

* Prosječna stopa rasta: 1.1–1.3 cm/mjesečno
* Hair density: Medium-to-high preferred; low density rezultira patchy boxed look
* Tekstura: Straight-to-wavy ideal; curly može zahtijevati više proizvoda za definition
* Commitment: Kandidat mora biti spreman na redovno održavanje (3–4× sedmično)
* Facial hair distribution: Uniforman rast na cheeks, chin, i jawline required
* Line precision: Steady hand ili willingness da posjeti brijača za line maintenance
