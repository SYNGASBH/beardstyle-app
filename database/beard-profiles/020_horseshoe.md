# Horseshoe Mustache – Inženjerski profil i karakteristike

Horseshoe Mustache je bold, statement stil koji kombinuje full mustache sa vertikalnim ekstenzijama koje se spuštaju niz strane usta prema bradi, formirajući oblik potkove (horseshoe). Poznat i kao "biker mustache", ovaj stil je postao ikonski u biker i rock kulturi, te je sinonim za tough, no-nonsense maskulinitet.

---

## Ključne dimenzije i proporcije

* **Horizontalni segment** (mustache proper): Full lip width
  → Širina: 8–12 cm
  → Debljina: 8–15 mm
  → Prekriva gornju usnu djelomično ili potpuno

* **Vertikalne ekstenzije**: Niz strane usta
  → Dužina: 3–8 cm ispod uglova usana
  → Širina ekstenzija: 10–20 mm
  → Prate liniju od uglova usana prema bradi
  → Ne spajaju se sa bradom (čist gap na bradi)

* **Gap na bradi**: Obavezan
  → Čista brada ispod donje usne
  → Gap između lijevog i desnog "kraka"
  → Ovo stvara horseshoe oblik

* **Oblik**: Inverted U
  → Mustache + dva vertikalna kraka
  → Krajevi mogu završavati square ili tapered
  → Symmetry critical

* **Krajevi (bottom)**: Various options
  → Square cut: Clean, blunt ends
  → Tapered: Gradually thin to point
  → Natural: Following growth pattern

* **Cheek area**: Clean
  → No connection to sideburns
  → Isolated horseshoe shape
  → Face otherwise clean-shaven

---

## Održavanje – Kontrolne tačke

* **Trim interval**:
  * Vertical extensions: Svakih 5–7 dana
  * Mustache lip line: Svakih 3–5 dana
  * Edge definition: Svakih 3–5 dana
  * Face shave (cheeks): Svakih 1–3 dana
  * **Ukupno**: Srednje-visoko održavanje (3–4 sessiona sedmično)

* **Alati**:
  * Trimmer sa guards (5–10 mm) – za bulk
  * Precision trimmer (0 mm) – za edges
  * Mustache scissors – za detail work
  * Razor – za face i chin area
  * Comb – za styling

* **Proizvodi**:
  * Light mustache/beard oil: 2–4 kapi
  * Styling balm: Optional za hold
  * Aftershave: Za shaved areas

* **Vrijeme po sessiji**: 10–15 minuta
* **Sedmični time investment**: 35–55 minuta

## Razlike vs slični stilovi

| Karakteristika | Horseshoe | Handlebar | Fu Manchu | Chevron | Biker Beard |
|----------------|-----------|-----------|-----------|---------|-------------|
| **Vertical extensions** | Yes | No | Yes | No | + Full beard |
| **Horizontal width** | Full lip | Extended | Full lip | Full lip | Full lip |
| **Chin** | Clean | Clean | Clean | Clean | Bearded |
| **Ends** | Down to chin | Curved out | Long, straight | At lip | Connected |
| **Styling** | Minimal | Wax required | None | None | Variable |
| **Vibe** | Biker/tough | Victorian | Asian martial | Classic | Full biker |

### Ključne distinkcije:
* **Horseshoe vs Fu Manchu**: Horseshoe ima thicker extensions; Fu Manchu ima thin, long strands.
* **Horseshoe vs Handlebar**: Horseshoe ide down; Handlebar ide out.
* **Horseshoe vs Chevron**: Horseshoe ima vertical extensions; Chevron nema.
* **Horseshoe vs Full Beard**: Horseshoe ima clean chin; Full beard connects.

---

## Dizajn pravila za autentičan Horseshoe

1. **Vertical extensions mandatory** – Bez vertikalnih ekstenzija, to je samo mustache. Extensions define horseshoe.

2. **Chin must be clean** – Ekstenzije završavaju PRIJE nego što se spoje. Gap na bradi is essential.

3. **Extensions should be substantial** – Thin, wispy extensions aren't horseshoe. Width 10+ mm.

4. **Symmetry is critical** – Both extensions must be identical in length, width, and position.

5. **Face is clean besides horseshoe** – No sideburns connection, no cheek hair.

6. **Extensions follow jawline** – Natural curve od usta prema bradi.

7. **Square ili tapered ends – consistent** – Choose one end style i maintain.

8. **Minimum extension length 3 cm** – Shorter než to nije full horseshoe.

## Growth Timeline

**Od clean shaven do Horseshoe:**

### Phase 1: Foundation (Sedmica 1–4)
* **Sedmica 1–2**:
  → Expected growth: ~3–4 mm
  → Let full face grow ili target areas
  → Mustache developing
* **Sedmica 3–4**:
  → Expected growth: +4 mm (total: ~7–8 mm)
  → Substantial mustache
  → Extensions beginning

### Phase 2: Extension Development (Sedmica 5–8)
* **Sedmica 5–6**:
  → Expected growth: ~10–12 mm mustache
  → Extensions: ~2–3 cm below lip corners
  → Begin shaping horseshoe
  → Clear chin area
* **Sedmica 7–8**:
  → Extensions: ~3–4 cm
  → Full horseshoe visible
  → Refine shape

### Phase 3: Full Development (Sedmica 9–12)
* **Sedmica 9–12**:
  → Extensions: 4–6+ cm
  → Dramatic horseshoe achieved
  → Maintenance mode

**Total time to basic horseshoe**: 6–8 sedmica
**Total time for dramatic horseshoe**: 10–12 sedmica

## Transition Paths

### 1. Clean Shaven → Horseshoe
* **Duration**: 8–12 sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Medium
* **Key steps**:
  1. Sedmica 1–4: Grow full mustache + corner areas
  2. Sedmica 5–6: Begin horseshoe shaping
  3. Sedmica 7–12: Develop full extensions

### 2. Chevron → Horseshoe
* **Duration**: 4–6 sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Easy-Medium
* **Steps**:
  1. Keep Chevron, stop trimming corners
  2. Let corners grow down
  3. Shape extensions as they develop
  4. Clear chin when extensions reach 3+ cm

### 3. Full Beard → Horseshoe
* **Duration**: 1–2 sessiona
* **Difficulty**: Medium
* **Steps**:
  1. Shave cheeks, sideburns
  2. Shave chin (center)
  3. Keep mustache + vertical strips
  4. Shape horseshoe

### 4. Horseshoe → Chevron
* **Duration**: 1 session
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Shave vertical extensions
  2. Keep mustache only
  3. Lower maintenance

### 5. Horseshoe → Fu Manchu
* **Duration**: 4–8 sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Easy-Medium
* **Steps**:
  1. Thin out extensions dramatically
  2. Let length grow longer
  3. Create thin, drooping strands
  4. Different aesthetic

### 6. Horseshoe → Full Beard
* **Duration**: 8–12 sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Stop shaving chin
  2. Stop shaving cheeks
  3. Let connect
  4. Horseshoe becomes part of full beard
