# Corporate Beard – Inženjerski profil i karakteristike

Corporate Beard je profesionalno orijentisan, medium-length stil dizajniran za poslovne okoline. Karakterišu ga precizne linije, čiste ivice, kontrolirana dužina i groomed appearance koji signalizira profesionalizam bez žrtvovanja muževnog izgleda. Ovaj stil balansira "ima bradu" sa "izgleda uredno" – idealan za office environment gdje su neuredne brade neprihvatljive, ali clean-shaven nije obavezan.

---

## Ključne dimenzije i proporcije

* **Dužina brade** (od donje usne do najniže tačke): 2–5 cm
  → Optimalno: 3–4 cm za balans prisutnosti i neatness
  → <2 cm: Prelazi u heavy stubble teritoriju
  → >5 cm: Počinje izgledati casual/less corporate

* **Uniformnost dužine**: Kritična karakteristika
  → Varijacija dužine: ±3 mm maksimum preko cijele brade
  → Sides = Chin = Cheeks u dužini (za razliku od tapered stilova)
  → Trimmer guard: Jedan setting za cijelu bradu

* **Donja kontura**: Čista, definisana linija
  → Shape: Blago zaobljena prateći natural jawline
  → Neckline: 1–2 prsta iznad Adam's apple, clean shaven ispod
  → **Oštra granica** – nema gradualnog fade na vratu

* **Cheek line**: Visoka, definisana, konzistentna
  → Tip: Hard line ili very tight fade (<5 mm blend)
  → Pozicija: Na ili 1 cm ispod jagodične kosti
  → Simetrija: Kritična – obje strane identične
  → **Nema stray hairs** iznad cheek line

* **Brkovi (Mustache)**: Groomed, povezani sa bradom
  → Stil: Trimani na istu dužinu kao brada
  → Lip clearance: 2–3 mm iznad gornje usne
  → Kutovi: Clean, defined connection sa bradom
  → Nema preklapanja preko usne

* **Projekcija volumena**: +10–20% donjeg lica
  → Subtle enhancement, ne dramatic change
  → Depth: 1.5–3 cm uniformno

* **Tekstura i gustina**:
  → Fill factor: >90% required (patchy areas problematične)
  → Stray hairs: **Zero tolerance** – moraju biti uklonjeni
  → Preferred texture: Any, ali mora biti groomed flat

---

## Održavanje – Kontrolne tačke

* **Trim interval**:
  * Puna brada (length): Svakih 3–5 dana
  * Cheek line: Svakih 2–3 dana (daily za perfectionists)
  * Neckline: Svakih 2–3 dana
  * Brkovi: Svakih 2–3 dana
  * Strays/outliers: Daily check
  * **Ukupno**: Visoko održavanje (4–5 sessiona sedmično)

* **Alati**:
  * Quality trimmer sa preciznim guards (3–12 mm range)
  * Precision trimmer/edger za linije (0 mm blade)
  * Detail scissors za strays
  * Fine-tooth comb za uniformity check
  * Magnifying mirror za precision work

* **Proizvodi**:
  * Beard oil: 3–5 kapi dnevno (light application)
  * Beard balm: Za flyaway control i polished look
  * Beard wash: 3× sedmično
  * Aftershave balm: Za neckline area post-shave

* **Vrijeme po sessiji**: 10–15 minuta
* **Sedmični time investment**: 50–75 minuta
* **Daily touch-up**: 2–3 minute (line checks, strays)

## Razlike vs slični stilovi

| Karakteristika | Corporate Beard | Short Boxed | Full Beard | Stubble |
|----------------|-----------------|-------------|------------|---------|
| **Dužina** | 2–5 cm | 3–5 cm | 5–12 cm | 0.5–1 cm |
| **Line precision** | Very high | High | Variable | Moderate |
| **Cheek line** | Hard/defined | Defined | Natural/soft | Defined |
| **Uniformity** | Critical | Important | Less critical | Important |
| **Maintenance** | High | Medium-High | Medium | Medium |
| **Office suitable** | Primary purpose | Yes | Depends | Yes |
| **Overall vibe** | Professional polished | Structured casual | Classic masculine | Rugged casual |

### Ključne distinkcije:
* **Corporate vs Short Boxed**: Vrlo slični – Corporate naglašava još više precision i office-appropriate appearance. Short Boxed može biti slightly more relaxed.
* **Corporate vs Full Beard**: Corporate je kraći, uniformniji, sa strožim linijama. Full Beard dozvoljava više natural variation.
* **Corporate vs Stubble**: Corporate ima definitivnu "bradu" prisutnost (3+ cm), dok je stubble subtle facial hair.

---

## Dizajn pravila za autentičan Corporate Beard

1. **Uniformna dužina je non-negotiable** – Ako cheeks izgledaju duže od chin, ili obrnuto, nije corporate. Koristi isti guard setting svuda.

2. **Linije moraju biti oštre** – Blurry cheek line ili neckline poništava corporate estetiku. Daily maintenance za perfection.

3. **Neckline mora biti vidljivo definisan** – Corporate beard NE prelazi u vrat. Clean shave ispod neckline svaki put.

4. **Zero tolerance za strays** – Jedan stray hair iznad cheek line ili ispod neckline uništava polished look. Daily inspection.

5. **Brkovi ne prelaze usnu** – Za corporate setting, lip coverage izgleda neuredna. Maintain 2–3 mm clearance.

6. **Simetrija je obavezna** – Cheek lines moraju biti identične s obje strane. Koristi referentne tačke (nos, uši).

7. **Products za control, ne za volume** – Corporate beard ne treba "big" look. Balm za smoothness i control, ne za puffiness.

8. **Brightness/health matters** – Suha, beživotna brada nije corporate. Oil za zdravi sjaj, ali ne greasy.

## Face Shape Compatibility

| Oblik lica | Score | Obrazloženje |
|------------|-------|--------------|
| **Oval** | 95/100 | Savršen match. Corporate beard frame-uje već balansirano lice bez distorzije. |
| **Square** | 90/100 | Odličan. Čiste linije komplementiraju angular features. Strong professional look. |
| **Round** | 80/100 | Dobro funkcionira. Defined lines dodaju strukture okruglom licu. Cheek line helps define. |
| **Triangle** | 85/100 | Dobro. Brada balansira široku vilicu. Keep length moderate (3–4 cm). |
| **Diamond** | 85/100 | Radi dobro. Beard adds definition to narrow chin area while lines work with cheekbones. |
| **Oblong** | 75/100 | OK, ali pažljivo. Vertical length može elongirati. Držati na 2–3 cm, emphasize width. |

**Best match**: Oval, Square (≥90/100)
**Univerzalno prihvatljiv**: Svi oblici mogu nositi corporate beard uz male adjustments

## Growth Timeline

**Od clean shaven do Corporate Beard target (3.5 cm):**

### Phase 1: Initial Growth (Sedmica 1–3)
* **Sedmica 1**:
  → Expected growth: ~1 cm
  → Awkward stubble phase – profesionalno problematično
  → **Timing tip**: Počni growth za vikend ili godišnji odmor
* **Sedmica 2**:
  → Expected growth: +1 cm (total: ~2 cm)
  → Počinje izgledati kao "growing beard" ne "didn't shave"
  → Može se definisati neckline
* **Sedmica 3**:
  → Expected growth: +1 cm (total: ~3 cm)
  → Minimum corporate length dostignut
  → Define cheek lines, full shaping moguć

### Phase 2: Shaping & Refinement (Sedmica 4–5)
* **Sedmica 4**:
  → Expected growth: reaching 3.5–4 cm
  → Full corporate shaping
  → Establish all lines precisely
  → Test uniformity sa trimmer
* **Sedmica 5**:
  → Optimal length achieved
  → Fine-tune simetriju
  → Establish maintenance routine

### Phase 3: Maintenance (Sedmica 6+)
* Steady state
* Trim length svakih 3–5 dana
* Line maintenance svakih 2–3 dana
* Daily stray checks

**Total time to achieve**: 3–5 sedmica
**Minimum growth required**: 3 cm (≈ 2.5–3 sedmice)
**Professional timing note**: Plan awkward phase around low-visibility period

## Transition Paths

### 1. Clean Shaven → Corporate Beard
* **Duration**: 3–5 sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Easy-Medium
* **Key steps**:
  1. Week 1: Let it grow, plan for awkward phase
  2. Week 2: Define neckline early
  3. Week 3: Define cheek lines when length allows
  4. Week 4: Shape to uniform length
  5. Week 5: Perfect symmetry, establish routine

### 2. Heavy Stubble → Corporate Beard
* **Duration**: 2–3 sedmice
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Continue growth to 3+ cm
  2. Define/refine lines
  3. Achieve uniformity
  4. Maintenance mode

### 3. Full Beard → Corporate Beard
* **Duration**: 1 session
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Trim to 3–4 cm uniform length
  2. Define sharp cheek line
  3. Clean up neckline
  4. Remove all strays
  5. Immediate maintenance schedule

### 4. Corporate Beard → Full Beard
* **Duration**: 6–10 sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Stop trimming length (or use longer guard)
  2. Allow cheek line to soften gradually
  3. Let natural shape develop
  4. At 5+ cm: full beard territory

### 5. Corporate Beard → Stubble
* **Duration**: 1 session
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Trim to 3–5 mm
  2. Maintain defined lines
  3. Lower maintenance than corporate

## Assumptions

* Prosječna stopa rasta: 1.1–1.3 cm/mjesečno (~1 cm/sedmica)
* Office environment: Profil pretpostavlja corporate/business casual dress code
* Grooming access: Daily access to mirror i basic tools
* Hair density: Medium-to-high preferred; low density može rezultirati patchy corporate look (consider stubble instead)
* Maintenance commitment: Must be willing to invest 10–15 min daily; inconsistent maintenance defeats purpose
* Facial hair pattern: Requires connected beard; if cheeks don't connect, consider modified corporate (goatee + stubble cheeks)
