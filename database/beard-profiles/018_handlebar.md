# Handlebar Mustache – Inženjerski profil i karakteristike

Handlebar je iconic, styled mustache poznat po svojim upturned ili horizontally-extended krajevima koji podsjećaju na ručke bicikla. Ovo je jedan od najprepoznatljivijih i najartističnijih mustache stilova, zahtijevajući redovno styling sa waxom i značajan commitment. Originira iz viktorijanske ere, Handlebar je doživio revival u craft/hipster kulturi 2010-ih.

---

## Ključne dimenzije i proporcije

* **Ukupna širina** (krajevi extended): 10–20+ cm
  → Short handlebar: 10–12 cm
  → Medium handlebar: 12–16 cm
  → Long handlebar: 16–20+ cm
  → Prelazi uglove usana značajno

* **Centralna visina** (ispod nosa): 8–15 mm
  → Gušći centar, thinner toward ends
  → Fill factor u centru: >90%

* **Krajevi (ends)**: Styled, pointed
  → Length beyond lip corner: 2–5+ cm na svakoj strani
  → Shape: Upturned, horizontal, ili slight downturn
  → Taper to a point

* **End styling options**:
  → Classic Upturned: Ends curve upward
  → Horizontal: Ends extend straight out
  → Hungarian: Extreme length, dramatic curves
  → Imperial: Upturned with curl
  → Petit Handlebar: Shorter, subtle curves

* **Philtrum area**: Usually visible
  → Small gap at center ili thin coverage
  → Diverging growth from center

* **Lip coverage**: Minimal u centru
  → Ends flow away from lip
  → Lip line usually visible

* **Povezanost sa bradom**: Traditionally none
  → Clean shaven face
  → Some combine with goatee (Van Dyke variant)

## Održavanje – Kontrolne tačke

* **Trim interval**:
  * End shaping: Daily styling
  * Center trim: Svakih 5–7 dana
  * Stray hairs: Svakih 2–3 dana
  * Face shave: Svakih 1–3 dana
  * **Ukupno**: Visoko održavanje (daily styling)

* **Alati**:
  * Mustache wax (firm hold) – ESSENTIAL
  * Fine mustache comb
  * Mustache scissors – za precision trimming
  * Blow dryer (optional) – za setting wax
  * Precision trimmer – za edges

* **Proizvodi**:
  * Strong hold mustache wax: Daily, multiple applications
  * Training wax/balm: Za softer hold
  * Mustache oil: Za conditioning
  * Aftershave: Za face

* **Vrijeme po sessiji**: 10–20 minuta (daily styling)
* **Sedmični time investment**: 70–100+ minuta

## Razlike vs slični stilovi

| Karakteristika | Handlebar | English | Imperial | Hungarian | Chevron |
|----------------|-----------|---------|----------|-----------|---------|
| **End styling** | Upturned/Out | Thin, out | Curled up | Extreme | None |
| **Width** | 10–20 cm | 10–15 cm | 12–18 cm | 15–25+ cm | 8–12 cm |
| **Wax required** | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | No |
| **Thickness** | Medium | Thin | Medium | Thick | Thick |
| **Maintenance** | High | High | Very High | High | Medium |
| **Era** | Victorian | British | German | Eastern Euro | 70s-80s |

### Ključne distinkcije:
* **Handlebar vs English**: Handlebar je thicker; English je narrow i pencil-thin.
* **Handlebar vs Imperial**: Imperial ima tight curls; Handlebar ima sweeping curves.
* **Handlebar vs Hungarian**: Hungarian je larger, bushier; Handlebar je more refined.
* **Handlebar vs Chevron**: Handlebar je styled sa waxom; Chevron je natural.

---

## Dizajn pravila za autentičan Handlebar

1. **Wax is mandatory** – Bez waxa, ovo nije handlebar. Daily styling defines the style.

2. **Ends must extend past lip corners** – If ends don't extend, it's not handlebar territory.

3. **Points required** – Ends should taper to points, not blunt cuts.

4. **Direction is a choice** – Upturned, horizontal, ili slight downturn all valid.

5. **Center should be neat** – Messy center dilutes the styled ends' impact.

6. **Face should be clean** – Traditional handlebar is standalone statement.

7. **Training required** – Hair must be trained to grow/lay in correct direction.

8. **Patience essential** – True handlebar takes 3–6 months to develop fully.

## Growth Timeline

**Od clean shaven do Handlebar:**

### Phase 1: Foundation Growth (Mjesec 1–2)
* **Mjesec 1**:
  → Expected growth: ~1.2 cm
  → Basic mustache forming
  → Too short for handlebar
  → Begin face shaving, keep mustache
* **Mjesec 2**:
  → Expected growth: +1.2 cm (total: ~2.4 cm)
  → Ends reaching lip corners
  → Begin wax training

### Phase 2: Extension Growth (Mjesec 3–4)
* **Mjesec 3**:
  → Expected growth: +1.2 cm (total: ~3.6 cm)
  → Ends extending past corners
  → Daily wax training
  → Basic handlebar shape possible
* **Mjesec 4**:
  → Expected growth: +1.2 cm (total: ~4.8 cm)
  → Full handlebar achievable
  → Refined styling possible

### Phase 3: Full Development (Mjesec 5–6+)
* **Mjesec 5–6**:
  → Total growth: 6+ cm
  → Dramatic handlebar possible
  → Hungarian-length approaches
* **Ongoing**:
  → Maintenance mode
  → Daily styling
  → Regular trimming

**Total time to achieve basic handlebar**: 3–4 mjeseca
**Total time for dramatic handlebar**: 5–6+ mjeseci

## Transition Paths

### 1. Clean Shaven → Handlebar
* **Duration**: 3–4 mjeseca minimum
* **Difficulty**: Hard (patience + styling)
* **Key steps**:
  1. Mjesec 1–2: Grow full mustache
  2. Mjesec 2: Begin wax training
  3. Mjesec 3–4: Develop handlebar shape
  4. Ongoing: Daily styling maintenance

### 2. Chevron → Handlebar
* **Duration**: 2–3 mjeseca
* **Difficulty**: Medium
* **Steps**:
  1. Stop trimming ends
  2. Let ends grow past lip corners
  3. Begin wax training
  4. Style ends outward

### 3. Full Beard → Handlebar
* **Duration**: 1 session + 2–3 mjeseca
* **Difficulty**: Medium
* **Steps**:
  1. Shave beard, keep mustache
  2. If mustache already long: begin styling
  3. If mustache short: grow out ends
  4. Wax training begins

### 4. Handlebar → Chevron
* **Duration**: 1 session
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Stop waxing
  2. Trim ends to lip-width
  3. Let natural shape develop
  4. Lower maintenance

### 5. Handlebar → Full Beard with Handlebar
* **Duration**: 8–12 sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Stop shaving face
  2. Maintain handlebar styling
  3. Grow beard
  4. Styled handlebar + beard combo

## Styling Techniques

### Basic Styling Routine:
1. **Prep**: Clean, dry mustache
2. **Warm wax**: Between fingers
3. **Apply center first**: Work outward
4. **Twist ends**: Create points
5. **Shape direction**: Up, out, ili desired angle
6. **Set**: Optional blow dryer (low heat)
7. **Refine**: Adjust throughout day

### Training Tips:
* Brush/comb in desired direction daily
* Use softer balm at night
* Firm wax during day
* Be patient – training takes weeks
