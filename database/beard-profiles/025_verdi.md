# Verdi – Inženjerski profil i karakteristike

Verdi je elegantna, sculptured full beard varijanta nazvana po italijanskom kompozitoru Giuseppeu Verdiju. Karakterizira je rounded, shorter full beard kombinirana sa styled, separated mustache - često curled ili waxed. Za razliku od Garibaldija koji prihvata natural look, Verdi zahtijeva precision styling i redovno održavanje za polished, artistic izgled.

---

## Ključne dimenzije i proporcije

* **Dužina brade** (od donje usne do najniže tačke): 7–12 cm
  → Optimalno: 8–10 cm
  → <7 cm: Prelazi u boxed beard territory
  → >12 cm: Ulazi u Garibaldi kategoriju

* **Širina brade** (na najširoj tački): 12–18 cm
  → Controlled width, ne dramatic flare
  → Proporcionalno sa face size

* **Donja kontura**: Rounded, controlled
  → Shape: Tight rounded arc (ne wide kao Garibaldi)
  → Radius: 6–10 cm
  → More sculpted než natural stilovi

* **Cheek line**: Definisana, groomed
  → Clear fade ili hard line
  → Neat, controlled appearance
  → Higher než natural stilovi

* **Mustache**: SEPARATED i STYLED
  → **Ovo je key Verdi characteristic**
  → Not integrated sa bradom
  → Waxed, curled, ili shaped
  → Handlebar-style ili similar
  → Separate styling routine

* **Mustache-beard gap**: Present
  → Visible separation
  → Clean corners ili thin connection
  → Mustache je separate element

* **Gustina**: High, uniform
  → Fill factor: >90%
  → Polished, not wild

## Održavanje – Kontrolne tačke

* **Trim interval**:
  * Mustache styling: Daily ili every-other-day
  * Mustache trim: Svakih 5–7 dana
  * Beard shape: Svakih 7–10 dana
  * Cheek line: Svakih 5–7 dana
  * Neckline: Svakih 5–7 dana
  * **Ukupno**: Visoko održavanje (4–5 sessiona sedmično)

* **Alati**:
  * Mustache wax (firm hold) – ESSENTIAL
  * Beard trimmer sa guards
  * Precision trimmer
  * Beard scissors
  * Mustache comb
  * Wide-tooth beard comb

* **Proizvodi**:
  * Mustache wax: Daily za styling
  * Beard oil: 4–6 kapi
  * Beard balm: Za shaping
  * Aftershave: Za cheek areas

* **Vrijeme po sessiji**: 15–20 minuta
* **Sedmični time investment**: 65–90 minuta

## Razlike vs slični stilovi

| Karakteristika | Verdi | Garibaldi | Bandholz | Ducktail | Full Beard |
|----------------|-------|-----------|----------|----------|------------|
| **Dužina** | 7–12 cm | 12–20 cm | 20–40 cm | 8–15 cm | 5–12 cm |
| **Mustache** | Separated styled | Integrated | Integrated | Integrated | Integrated |
| **Styling** | High | Low | Minimal | Medium | Variable |
| **Shape** | Tight rounded | Wide rounded | Natural | Pointed | Variable |
| **Maintenance** | High | Low | Very Low | Medium-High | Medium |
| **Vibe** | Polished artistic | Distinguished wild | Epic natural | Refined masculine | Classic |

### Ključne distinkcije:
* **Verdi vs Garibaldi**: Verdi je shorter, sa separated styled mustache; Garibaldi je longer sa integrated mustache.
* **Verdi vs Full Beard**: Verdi ima specific requirements (styled mustache, tight shape); Full Beard je generic category.
* **Verdi vs Ducktail**: Verdi ima rounded bottom; Ducktail ima pointed bottom.

---

## Dizajn pravila za autentičan Verdi

1. **Mustache MORA biti odvojen i styled** – Integrated mustache = nije Verdi. Separate styling essential.

2. **Wax za mustache non-negotiable** – Natural mustache nije Verdi aesthetic.

3. **Beard shorter than Garibaldi** – 7–12 cm range. Longer nije Verdi.

4. **Rounded, controlled shape** – Ne pointed, ne wild. Sculpted roundness.

5. **Higher maintenance accepted** – Verdi zahtijeva commitment.

6. **Polished, not wild** – Stray hairs minimal, groomed appearance.

7. **Clear mustache-beard distinction** – Gap ili thin connection, not full integration.

8. **Artistic intention** – Named after composer, should look refined.

## Growth Timeline

**Od clean shaven do Verdi:**

### Phase 1: Foundation Growth (Sedmica 1–6)
* **Sedmica 1–3**:
  → Expected growth: ~3–4 cm
  → Grow full face including mustache
* **Sedmica 4–6**:
  → Expected growth: +3–4 cm (total: ~7 cm)
  → Minimum Verdi length approaching

### Phase 2: Shaping (Sedmica 7–10)
* **Sedmica 7–8**:
  → Begin Verdi shaping
  → Create mustache separation
  → Start wax training za mustache
  → Shape rounded bottom
* **Sedmica 9–10**:
  → Refine mustache styling
  → Perfect beard shape
  → Establish grooming routine

### Phase 3: Full Verdi (Sedmica 11+)
* Optimal length achieved (8–10 cm)
* Daily mustache styling
* Regular beard maintenance
* Polished look achieved

**Total time to achieve**: 8–12 sedmica (2–3 mjeseca)

## Transition Paths

### 1. Clean Shaven → Verdi
* **Duration**: 8–12 sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Medium-Hard
* **Key steps**:
  1. Sedmica 1–6: Grow full beard + mustache
  2. Sedmica 7–8: Shape beard, separate mustache
  3. Sedmica 9–10: Begin wax styling
  4. Ongoing: High maintenance routine

### 2. Full Beard → Verdi
* **Duration**: 2–4 sedmice
* **Difficulty**: Medium
* **Steps**:
  1. Shape beard to rounded form (7–12 cm)
  2. Create mustache separation
  3. Begin wax styling
  4. Learn mustache maintenance

### 3. Garibaldi → Verdi
* **Duration**: 1–2 sessiona + styling learning
* **Difficulty**: Medium
* **Steps**:
  1. Trim beard to 7–12 cm
  2. Tighten rounded shape
  3. Separate mustache from beard
  4. Begin wax styling
  5. Higher maintenance begins

### 4. Verdi → Garibaldi
* **Duration**: 6–12 sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Stop wax styling
  2. Let mustache integrate
  3. Allow longer growth
  4. Let shape become more natural
  5. Lower maintenance

### 5. Verdi → Bandholz
* **Duration**: 12–24 sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Stop trimming length
  2. Stop wax styling
  3. Let natural shape develop
  4. At 20+ cm: Bandholz territory
