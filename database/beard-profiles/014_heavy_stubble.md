# Heavy Stubble – Inženjerski profil i karakteristike

Heavy Stubble je "almost-beard" facial hair opcija koja predstavlja gornju granicu stubble kategorije. Na 4–6 mm dužine, Heavy Stubble pruža značajniju facial hair prisutnost od designer stubble-a, ali još uvijek ostaje ispod beard teritorije. Ovaj stil je popularan jer pruža "rugged" izgled bez full beard maintenance-a, i često se citira kao jedna od najatrraktivnijih facial hair opcija u istraživanjima.

---

## Ključne dimenzije i proporcije

* **Dužina stubble-a**: 4–6 mm
  → Optimalno: 5 mm (sweet spot za heavy stubble)
  → <4 mm: Designer stubble territory
  → >6 mm: Prelazi u short beard/boxed beard

* **Coverage**: Full face preferred
  → Jednaka dužina svuda
  → Heavy stubble pokazuje density bolje nego shorter stubble
  → Patchy areas postaju više vidljive

* **Uniformnost**: Važna za groomed look
  → Varijacija: ±1 mm acceptable
  → Natural variation looks intentional at this length

* **Cheek line**: Usually natural ili soft defined
  → Option A: Natural growth (most common)
  → Option B: Soft fade (groomed casual)
  → Hard line manje uobičajena za heavy stubble

* **Neckline**: Typically defined
  → Pozicija: 1–2 prsta iznad Adam's apple
  → Clean shaved ispod
  → Može biti natural za very rugged look

* **Mustache area**: Included, ista dužina
  → At 5 mm, mustache počinje imati presence
  → Lip clearance: May touch upper lip slightly

* **Projekcija volumena**: +5–10%
  → Noticeable shadow and texture
  → Beginning of "beard shadow" effect

* **Tekstura**:
  → Fill factor: 80%+ preferred (patchy more visible than designer stubble)
  → Individual hairs visible at this length
  → Texture differences become apparent

---

## Održavanje – Kontrolne tačke

* **Trim interval**:
  * Full stubble trim: Svakih 3–4 dana
  * Neckline: Svakih 3–4 dana
  * Cheek line (ako defined): Svakih 3–4 dana
  * **Ukupno**: Srednje održavanje (2–3 sessiona sedmično)

* **Alati**:
  * Stubble/beard trimmer sa adjustable guards
  * Guard 4–5 mm – primary setting
  * Precision trimmer (0 mm) – za linije
  * Razor – za neckline cleanup

* **Proizvodi**:
  * Light beard oil: 1–2 kapi za softness (optional)
  * Stubble softener/moisturizer
  * Aftershave balm: Za neckline area

* **Vrijeme po sessiji**: 6–10 minuta
* **Sedmični time investment**: 15–25 minuta

## Razlike vs slični stilovi

| Karakteristika | Heavy Stubble | Designer Stubble | Short Boxed | 10-Day Beard | Beardstache |
|----------------|---------------|------------------|-------------|--------------|-------------|
| **Dužina** | 4–6 mm | 2–4 mm | 30–50 mm | 8–12 mm | 2–5 mm (face) |
| **Visibility** | Strong shadow | Clear stubble | Short beard | Growing beard | Contrast |
| **Trim frequency** | 3–4 dana | 2–3 dana | 5–7 dana | Variable | 3–4 dana |
| **Volume** | Minimal | Trace | Low-Medium | Low | Minimal |
| **Lines** | Optional | Optional | Defined | Forming | Optional |
| **Overall vibe** | Rugged shadow | Groomed stubble | Compact beard | Transitional | Statement |

### Ključne distinkcije:
* **Heavy Stubble vs Designer Stubble**: Heavy Stubble je gušći, daje više "almost beard" effect, manje frequent maintenance.
* **Heavy Stubble vs Short Boxed**: Short Boxed ima definitivnu beard presence (30+ mm); Heavy Stubble ostaje u stubble family.
* **Heavy Stubble vs 10-Day Beard**: 10-Day je "growing in" faza; Heavy Stubble je maintained state.

## Dizajn pravila za autentičan Heavy Stubble

1. **4–6 mm range je defining boundary** – Ispod = designer stubble, iznad = short beard. Stay in range.

2. **Natural look is the goal** – Heavy stubble najbolje izgleda sa minimal sculpting. Over-defined izgleda pretentious.

3. **Neckline optional ali helpful** – Defined neckline adds polish bez losing rugged appeal.

4. **Let texture show** – At this length, natural texture (straight, wavy) becomes visible. Embrace it.

5. **Patchy areas more visible** – Consider if density supports heavy stubble; patchy may look better at shorter length.

6. **Consistent length over perfect lines** – Focus on uniform length više nego perfect edges.

7. **Less frequent is fine** – Heavy stubble tolerates 3–4 day cycles better than designer stubble.

8. **Softening products help** – 5 mm stubble can be prickly. Oils/softeners benefit both you and others.

## Growth Timeline

**Od clean shaven do Heavy Stubble target (5 mm):**

### Phase 1: Initial Growth (Dan 1–5)
* **Dan 1–2**:
  → Expected growth: ~0.5–1 mm
  → 5 o'clock shadow
* **Dan 3–4**:
  → Expected growth: +1–1.5 mm (total: ~2–2.5 mm)
  → Designer stubble territory
* **Dan 5–6**:
  → Expected growth: +1 mm (total: ~3.5–4 mm)
  → Approaching heavy stubble

### Phase 2: Target Achievement (Dan 7–10)
* **Dan 7–10**:
  → Total growth: 4–6 mm
  → Heavy stubble achieved
  → Define neckline
  → First maintenance trim to even out

### Phase 3: Maintenance (Dan 11+)
* Steady state
* Trim to 5 mm svakih 3–4 dana
* Maintain neckline svakih 3–4 dana

**Total time to achieve**: 7–10 dana
**Minimum growth required**: 4 mm

## Transition Paths

### 1. Clean Shaven → Heavy Stubble
* **Duration**: 7–10 dana
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Key steps**:
  1. Dan 1–6: Let it grow
  2. Dan 7–10: First trim to even, define neckline
  3. Ongoing: Maintain every 3–4 days

### 2. Designer Stubble → Heavy Stubble
* **Duration**: 3–5 dana
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Let grow from 3 mm to 5 mm
  2. Maintain neckline during growth
  3. Adjust maintenance schedule

### 3. Short Beard → Heavy Stubble
* **Duration**: 1 session
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Trim from beard length to 5 mm
  2. Significant visual change
  3. More frequent maintenance begins

### 4. Heavy Stubble → Short Beard
* **Duration**: 3–4 sedmice
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Let grow past 6 mm
  2. At 10+ mm: entering beard territory
  3. Transition to beard maintenance

### 5. Heavy Stubble → Designer Stubble
* **Duration**: 1 session
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Trim from 5 mm to 3 mm
  2. More frequent maintenance required
  3. Cleaner look achieved

### 6. Heavy Stubble → Beardstache
* **Duration**: 2–4 sedmice
* **Difficulty**: Medium
* **Steps**:
  1. Keep face at 5 mm (or reduce)
  2. Let mustache grow out
  3. When mustache is 10+ mm: beardstache territory
  4. Different maintenance approach
