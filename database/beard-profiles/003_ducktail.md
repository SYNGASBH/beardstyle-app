# Ducktail – Inženjerski profil i karakteristike

Ducktail je elegantna full beard varijanta karakterizirana pointed, tapered donjom konturom koja podsjeća na rep patke. Za razliku od rounded stilova poput Garibaldija, Ducktail naglašava vertikalnu liniju i šiljati završetak na bradi. Ovaj stil kombinuje volumen full bearda sa sculpted, refined estetikom – zahtijeva više održavanja od natural stilova, ali manje od precision stilova poput Verdija.

---

## Ključne dimenzije i proporcije

* **Dužina brade** (od donje usne do najniže tačke – tip): 8–15 cm
  → Optimalno: 10–12 cm za definisan point bez extremne dužine
  → <8 cm: Nedovoljno za formiranje distinctivnog pointa
  → >15 cm: Prelazi u "long Ducktail" ili Bandholz-with-point teritoriju

* **Širina brade** (na najširoj tački, obično na jaw line): 12–18 cm
  → **Key ratio**: Width-to-length ≈ 1.2:1 do 1.5:1
  → Sides se taperiraju prema bradi, stvarajući V-shape
  → Maksimalna širina je na ili iznad jaw line, ne ispod

* **Donja kontura**: Pointed/tapered (ključna karakteristika)
  → Angle od širine do pointa: 30–45°
  → Point sharpness: Može biti soft point (rounded tip) ili sharp point (defined tip)
  → Centralni point alignment: Mora biti na midline lica
  → Taper počinje: 3–5 cm iznad najniže tačke

* **Cheek line**: Definisana, sa gradualnim fade
  → Fade tip: Gentle-to-medium (ne hard line)
  → Početna tačka: Na razini uha ili 1–2 cm ispod
  → Blend distance: 1.5–3 cm gradualni prelaz
  → Cheeks izgledaju "clean" u poređenju sa Garibaldijem

* **Brkovi (Mustache)**: Integrisani, full ali groomed
  → Stil: Natural full, blago preko gornje usne
  → Dužina: Do 1 cm preko uglova usana
  → Integrisani sa bradom – nema separation
  → Može se blago trimati za neatness

* **Projekcija volumena**: +25–40% donjeg lica
  → Manje dramatic nego Garibaldi zbog tapering
  → Depth na chin point: 3–6 cm
  → Depth na stranama (jaw): 2–4 cm (tapered manje)

* **Tekstura i gustina**:
  → Fill factor: >85% required za clean point definition
  → Stray hairs: Minimalni dozvoljen – narušavaju point definition
  → Preferred texture: Straight-to-wavy; curly zahtijeva više styling

## Održavanje – Kontrolne tačke

* **Trim interval**:
  * Point definition: Svakih 7–10 dana (kritično za stil)
  * Side taper: Svakih 10–14 dana
  * Cheek line: Svakih 7–10 dana
  * Brkovi: Svakih 5–7 dana (lip clearance)
  * Neckline: Svakih 7–10 dana
  * **Ukupno**: Srednje-visoko održavanje (2–3 sessiona sedmično)

* **Alati**:
  * Precision trimmer (bez guard) – za point i cheek line definition
  * Trimmer sa guards (6–12 mm) – za bulk i taper
  * Beard scissors (5 inch) – za fine-tuning i strays
  * Fine-tooth comb – za training i styling
  * Boar bristle brush – za shaping i smoothing

* **Proizvodi**:
  * Beard oil: 5–8 kapi dnevno
  * Beard balm: Za hold i shape definition, dnevno
  * Beard wax: Opcionalno, za extra point hold
  * Beard wash: 2–3× sedmično

* **Vrijeme po sessiji**: 15–20 minuta
* **Sedmični time investment**: 40–60 minuta maintenance + 10 min daily styling

## Razlike vs slični stilovi

| Karakteristika | Ducktail | Garibaldi | Full Beard | Verdi |
|----------------|----------|-----------|------------|-------|
| **Dužina** | 8–15 cm | 12–20 cm | 5–12 cm | 7–10 cm |
| **Donja kontura** | Pointed/V-shape | Wide rounded | Variable | Tight rounded |
| **Cheek line** | Defined fade | Natural/soft | Variable | Hard defined |
| **Side profile** | Tapered V | Wide U | Straight/natural | Sculpted round |
| **Brkovi** | Integrated groomed | Integrated natural | Integrated | Separated styled |
| **Maintenance** | Medium-High | Low | Medium | High |
| **Overall vibe** | Refined masculine | Distinguished wild | Classic | Polished artistic |

### Ključne distinkcije:
* **Ducktail vs Garibaldi**: Ducktail ima pointed bottom (V-shape); Garibaldi ima rounded bottom (U-shape). Ducktail je tapered na stranama; Garibaldi je wide.
* **Ducktail vs Verdi**: Ducktail ima integrisane brkove i pointed chin; Verdi ima separated waxed brkove i rounded chin.
* **Ducktail vs Full Beard**: Ducktail je specifična varijanta sa definisanom pointed konturom. Generic full beard nema point specifikaciju.

## Dizajn pravila za autentičan Ducktail

1. **Point mora biti centriran** – Off-center point je najčešća greška. Koristi midline lica kao guide. Provjeri sa ogledalom direktno ispred.

2. **Taper mora biti gradualan** – Abrupt transition od wide sides do point izgleda neprirodno. Smooth diagonal line (30–45°) od jaw do chin tip.

3. **Širina se smanjuje prema dolje** – Maximum width na jaw line ili iznad. Ako je brada šira ispod jaw-a, to nije Ducktail – to je rounded/Garibaldi shape.

4. **Cheeks moraju biti cleaner** – Ducktail zahtijeva više cheek definition nego natural stilovi. Soft fade OK, ali ne wild natural cheeks.

5. **Point sharpness konzistentna** – Odluči: soft point ili sharp point, i održavaj konzistentno. Mixing izgleda kao "messy trim".

6. **Side length kraća od chin length** – Key ratio: Chin point length ≥ 1.5× side length na jaw line. Ovo stvara V-shape.

7. **Regular maintenance je obavezan** – Za razliku od Bandholza, Ducktail gubi definition brzo. 7–10 dana bez trim-a = lost shape.

## Face Shape Compatibility

| Oblik lica | Score | Obrazloženje |
|------------|-------|--------------|
| **Oval** | 95/100 | Idealno. Point dodaje interest bez narušavanja balansa. Elongacija je controlled. |
| **Square** | 85/100 | Dobro. Point softens angular chin, taper complements strong jaw. |
| **Round** | 90/100 | Odlično. Point i V-shape vizualno elongira i slims okruglo lice. Highly recommended. |
| **Triangle** | 70/100 | Oprez. Već pointed chin + pointed beard može biti "too much point". Consider rounded style. |
| **Diamond** | 80/100 | Funkcioniše. Point balansira narrow chin, taper works sa cheekbones. |
| **Oblong** | 65/100 | Rizično. Point i vertical emphasis mogu dodatno elongirati već dugo lice. Keep length shorter. |

**Best match**: Oval, Round (≥90/100)
**Good**: Square, Diamond (80–85/100)
**Careful**: Triangle, Oblong (<75/100)

## Growth Timeline

**Od clean shaven do Ducktail target (10 cm):**

### Phase 1: Foundation (Sedmica 1–6)
* **Sedmica 1–4**: Growing phase, minimal intervention
  → Očekivani rast: 4–5 cm
  → Neckline: Definisati od sedmice 3
  → Cheeks: Početi soft definition od sedmice 4
* **Sedmica 5–6**: Reaching shapeable length
  → Očekivani rast: +2–3 cm (ukupno 6–8 cm)
  → Start training downward growth sa češljanjem

### Phase 2: Initial Shaping (Sedmica 7–10)
* **Sedmica 7–8**: First point formation
  → Očekivani rast: +2 cm (ukupno 8–10 cm)
  → Početi tapering sides
  → Form initial point – ne mora biti perfect
  → Cheek line: Refine fade
* **Sedmica 9–10**: Refinement
  → Fine-tune point centriranje
  → Establish taper angle
  → Sides vs chin ratio check

### Phase 3: Maintenance (Sedmica 11+)
* Target shape achieved
* Maintenance trims svakih 7–10 dana
* Focus: Keeping point sharp, taper smooth
* Product routine etablished

**Total time to achieve**: 8–12 sedmica (2–3 mjeseca)
**Minimum growth required**: 8 cm na chin (≈ 6–7 mjeseci natural chin growth)

## Transition Paths

### 1. Clean Shaven → Ducktail
* **Duration**: 8–12 sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Medium
* **Key steps**:
  1. Sedmica 1–4: Grow out, establish neckline
  2. Sedmica 5–6: Start cheek definition
  3. Sedmica 7–9: Form point, start tapering sides
  4. Sedmica 10+: Refine and maintain

### 2. Full Beard (8 cm) → Ducktail
* **Duration**: 2–4 sedmice (reshaping)
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Session 1: Establish cheek line definition
  2. Session 2: Begin tapering sides
  3. Session 3: Form point, remove bulk
  4. Session 4: Fine-tune ratios
  5. Maintenance mode

### 3. Garibaldi → Ducktail
* **Duration**: 2–4 sedmice (major reshaping)
* **Difficulty**: Medium
* **Steps**:
  1. Significant bulk removal from sides
  2. Reshape rounded bottom to pointed
  3. Define cheek line more
  4. Reduce overall width
  5. May need to shorten if Garibaldi was very long

### 4. Ducktail → Garibaldi
* **Duration**: 6–12 sedmica (growing out)
* **Difficulty**: Easy-Medium
* **Steps**:
  1. Stop tapering sides
  2. Let cheeks fill in naturally
  3. Reshape pointed bottom to rounded
  4. Allow width expansion
  5. At 12+ cm: shape rounded contour

### 5. Ducktail → Van Dyke
* **Duration**: 1–2 sedmice (dramatic reshaping)
* **Difficulty**: Medium (irreversible short-term)
* **Steps**:
  1. Remove side beard entirely (clean cheeks)
  2. Keep chin point
  3. Separate mustache
  4. Define soul patch if desired
  5. Completely different maintenance routine

## Assumptions

* Prosječna stopa rasta: 1.1–1.3 cm/mjesečno
* Chin hair vs cheek hair: Pretpostavlja relativno uniforman rast; ako chin raste sporije, vrijeme do pointa može biti duže
* Hair density: Medium-to-high preferred; low density može rezultirati wispy point
* Tekstura: Straight-to-wavy ideal; curly može zahtijevati više proizvoda za point control
* Commitment: Kandidat mora biti spreman na redovno održavanje (2–3× sedmično)
* Facial hair distribution: Point formation zahtijeva hair growth na chin midline
