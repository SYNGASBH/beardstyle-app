# Pencil Mustache – Inženjerski profil i karakteristike

Pencil Mustache je thin, precisely trimmed mustache stil koji formira tanku liniju iznad gornje usne. Populariziran u Hollywood Golden Age-u 1930-ih i 40-ih, ovaj stil je sinonim za eleganciju, sofisticiranost i suave charm. Zahtijeva izuzetnu preciznost i redovno održavanje – možda najzahtjevniji mustache stil po pitanju precision trimming-a.

---

## Ključne dimenzije i proporcije

* **Širina linije**: 2–5 mm
  → Ultra-thin: 2–3 mm (classic pencil)
  → Standard: 3–4 mm (most common)
  → Thick pencil: 4–5 mm (upper limit)
  → >5 mm: Prelazi iz pencil kategorije

* **Horizontalna dužina**: Prati lip line
  → Tipično: 6–10 cm
  → Ends: Na ili blago preko uglova usana
  → Symmetry: CRITICAL

* **Pozicija**: Direktno iznad lip line
  → Gap od lip: 2–4 mm
  → Follows natural lip contour
  → Centered precisely

* **Oblik**: Straight line ili subtle arc
  → Straight: Classic, formal
  → Subtle arc: Follows lip curve
  → Sharp, defined edges on all sides

* **Philtrum (centar)**: Options
  → Continuous: Thin line across
  → Split: Small gap in center
  → Both are valid pencil variations

* **Krajevi**: Clean, defined
  → Sharp cut ili tapered point
  → No extension beyond mouth width
  → No curls ili styling

* **Povezanost**: None
  → Clean face mandatory
  → Standalone statement
  → No beard, no soul patch

---

## Održavanje – Kontrolne tačke

* **Trim interval**:
  * Precision trim: Svakih 1–2 dana (CRITICAL)
  * Edge definition: Daily
  * Face shave: Daily ili every other day
  * **Ukupno**: Vrlo visoko održavanje (daily attention)

* **Alati**:
  * Precision trimmer (0.5 mm setting) – ESSENTIAL
  * Detail razor ili straight razor
  * Magnifying mirror – za precision
  * Fine mustache comb
  * Eyebrow/mustache scissors

* **Proizvodi**:
  * Precision shave gel: Za clean edges
  * Aftershave: Za face
  * Light mustache oil: Optional za conditioning

* **Vrijeme po sessiji**: 8–15 minuta
* **Sedmični time investment**: 50–80 minuta

## Razlike vs slični stilovi

| Karakteristika | Pencil | Chevron | Handlebar | English | Toothbrush |
|----------------|--------|---------|-----------|---------|------------|
| **Width** | 2–5 mm | 10–20 mm | Variable | 3–6 mm | 20–30 mm |
| **Shape** | Thin line | Thick block | Styled | Thin, styled | Square block |
| **Ends** | At lip corners | At lip | Extended | Extended | Narrow width |
| **Maintenance** | Very High | Medium | High | High | Medium |
| **Era** | 1930s-40s | 1970s-80s | Victorian | British | 1920s |
| **Styling** | Precision cut | Natural | Wax | Wax | Natural |

### Ključne distinkcije:
* **Pencil vs English**: Pencil krajevi ne prelaze usnu; English ima extended, styled ends.
* **Pencil vs Chevron**: Pencil je thin line; Chevron je thick block.
* **Pencil vs Handlebar**: Pencil nema styling; Handlebar zahtijeva wax.
* **Pencil vs Toothbrush**: Pencil je thin i wide; Toothbrush je thick i narrow.

---

## Dizajn pravila za autentičan Pencil

1. **Thinness is defining** – If wider than 5 mm, it's not pencil. Thin line is the essence.

2. **Precision required daily** – One day without trim = lost definition. Daily attention mandatory.

3. **Symmetry is paramount** – Asymmetric pencil is immediately obvious. Perfect balance required.

4. **Edges must be sharp** – Blurry edges destroy pencil aesthetic. Clean, defined lines all around.

5. **Position is exact** – 2–4 mm above lip, following lip contour. Too high ili too low looks wrong.

6. **Face must be clean** – Any facial hair besides pencil dilutes the refined look.

7. **No styling products needed** – Pencil relies on cutting precision, not product.

8. **Continuous ili split – choose one** – Both valid, but be consistent.

## Growth Timeline

**Od clean shaven do Pencil Mustache:**

### Phase 1: Initial Growth (Sedmica 1–3)
* **Sedmica 1–2**:
  → Expected growth: ~3–4 mm
  → Basic mustache visible
  → Grow full mustache first
* **Sedmica 3**:
  → Expected growth: ~5–6 mm
  → Enough to begin shaping

### Phase 2: Shaping (Sedmica 4)
* **Sedmica 4**:
  → INITIAL PENCIL SHAPING:
  → Trim down to thin line
  → Define position above lip
  → Create sharp edges
  → Establish width (2–5 mm)

### Phase 3: Precision Maintenance (Sedmica 5+)
* Steady state achieved
* Daily ili every-other-day trimming
* Continuous precision required

**Total time to achieve**: 3–4 sedmice
**Note**: Quicker to achieve than thick styles, but hardest to maintain

---

## Transition Paths

### 1. Clean Shaven → Pencil
* **Duration**: 3–4 sedmice
* **Difficulty**: Medium (shaping easy, maintenance hard)
* **Key steps**:
  1. Sedmica 1–3: Grow full mustache
  2. Sedmica 4: Shape to pencil thin line
  3. Ongoing: Daily precision maintenance

### 2. Chevron → Pencil
* **Duration**: 1 session
* **Difficulty**: Easy (reduction)
* **Steps**:
  1. Trim Chevron down to thin line
  2. Define sharp edges
  3. Establish 2–5 mm width
  4. Significantly higher maintenance begins

### 3. Full Mustache → Pencil
* **Duration**: 1 session
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Trim to thin line
  2. Define position i edges
  3. Daily maintenance begins

### 4. Pencil → Chevron
* **Duration**: 4–6 sedmica
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Stop trimming to thin line
  2. Let grow wider
  3. When 10+ mm: Chevron territory
  4. Lower maintenance

### 5. Pencil → Clean Shaven
* **Duration**: 1 session
* **Difficulty**: Easy
* **Steps**:
  1. Shave mustache
  2. Return to clean face

### 6. Pencil → Handlebar
* **Duration**: 3–4 mjeseca
* **Difficulty**: Medium-Hard
* **Steps**:
  1. Let ends grow
  2. Let center thicken slightly
  3. Begin wax training
  4. Transition to handlebar styling
