# Yeard – Inženjerski profil i karakteristike

Yeard je time-based beard milestone koji predstavlja jednu punu godinu (year + beard = yeard) rasta bez trimanja dužine. Za razliku od stilova definisanih oblikom ili dimenzijama, Yeard je definisan metodologijom: 12 mjeseci natural growth, zero length trimming. Rezultirajuća dužina i oblik variraju individualno (tipično 12–16 cm), ali commitment je univerzalan. Yeard je često stepping stone ka Bandholzu ili celebration of patience sam po sebi.

---

## Ključne dimenzije i proporcije

* **Dužina brade** (od donje usne do najniže tačke): 12–16 cm (varies by genetics)
  → Prosječni rezultat: ~13–14 cm nakon 12 mjeseci
  → Spori rast: 10–12 cm
  → Brzi rast: 15–18 cm
  → **Ne trimati dužinu** – whatever length you achieve at 12 months = your yeard

* **Širina brade**: Potpuno natural
  → Nema target širine – diktirano genetikom
  → Tipično 12–20 cm zavisno od facial structure
  → Ne trimati za width control

* **Donja kontura**: Natural/undefined
  → **Nema shaping** – whatever shape grows naturally
  → Može biti rounded, pointed, asymmetric, or irregular
  → Prihvatanje natural outcome je dio philosophije

* **Cheek line**: Natural ili minimalno definisana
  → Opcija 1 (Purist): Potpuno natural, nema trimanja
  → Opcija 2 (Modified): Samo neckline, natural cheeks
  → Opcija 3 (Groomed Yeard): Neckline + soft cheek line, no length trim
  → Sve tri su validne "yeard" varijante

* **Brkovi (Mustache)**: Natural growth
  → Nema trimanja osim za functionality (eating/drinking)
  → Minimal lip line clearing dozvoljeno
  → Tipično dostižu 1–2 cm preko usana
  → Training (combing to sides) OK, trimming nije

* **Projekcija volumena**: +35–55% donjeg lica (varies)
  → Ovisi o density i growth pattern
  → Depth: 4–8 cm tipično

* **Tekstura i gustina**:
  → Fill factor: Whatever genetics provide (60–100% range)
  → Stray hairs: Prihvatljivo – dio natural look
  → Patches: Dužina often covers thin spots over time

## Održavanje – Kontrolne tačke

* **Trim interval (STRICT YEARD RULES)**:
  * Dužina: **NIKAD** kroz 12 mjeseci – ovo je definicija yearda
  * Cheeks: Opciono, ako desired; purists say no
  * Neckline: Opcionalno – most people do minimal neckline
  * Brkovi: SAMO functional trim (lip clearance za jelo)
  * Split ends: Kontroverzno – purists say no; others allow micro-dusting
  * **Ukupno**: Vrlo nisko do nisko (0.5–1 session sedmično)

* **Alati (Minimal set)**:
  * Wide-tooth comb – primary tool
  * Boar bristle brush – for oil distribution
  * Scissors – ONLY for functional mustache trim if needed
  * Trimmer – ONLY for neckline if you choose that option

* **Proizvodi (Health focus)**:
  * Beard oil: Obavezan, 6–10 kapi dnevno (increases as length grows)
  * Beard butter/balm: From month 4+, for moisture
  * Beard wash: 2× sedmično
  * Conditioner: 1× sedmično, leave-in from month 6+

* **Vrijeme po sessiji**: 5–10 minuta (mostly grooming, minimal trimming)
* **Sedmični time investment**: 10–20 minuta + 10 min daily brushing/oiling

## Razlike vs slični stilovi

| Karakteristika | Yeard | Bandholz | Garibaldi | Full Beard |
|----------------|-------|----------|-----------|------------|
| **Definition** | Time-based (12 mo) | Length-based (20+ cm) | Shape-based (rounded) | Generic category |
| **Dužina** | 12–16 cm typical | 20–40+ cm | 12–20 cm | 5–12 cm |
| **Length trimming** | NOT allowed | Minimal | Allowed | Allowed |
| **Shape requirement** | None | None | Wide rounded | Variable |
| **Time to achieve** | Exactly 12 months | 18–36 months | 10–15 months | 4–9 months |
| **Philosophy** | Patience milestone | Epic natural | Distinguished wild | Classic look |

### Ključne distinkcije:
* **Yeard vs Bandholz**: Yeard je 12-mjesečni milestone; Bandholz je ongoing style sa 20+ cm. Yeard often becomes Bandholz ako se nastavi rast.
* **Yeard vs Garibaldi**: Garibaldi ima defined rounded shape; Yeard ima whatever shape grows. Yeard can look like Garibaldi at month 12, but wasn't shaped intentionally.
* **Yeard as methodology**: Yeard nije trajni stil – to je journey. Na kraju 12 mjeseci, odlučuješ: maintain, transition to Bandholz, ili reshape.

## Dizajn pravila za autentičan Yeard

1. **Zero length trimming za 12 mjeseci** – Ovo je jedino apsolutno pravilo. Čim trimiraš dužinu, resetiraš sat. Functional mustache trim za jelo je jedini izuzetak.

2. **Vrijeme je jedini standard** – Ne mjeri se yeard u centimetrima. 12 mjeseci rasta = yeard, bez obzira rezultirajuća dužina.

3. **Neckline je opcionalan** – Purist yeard: nema neckline. Groomed yeard: maintained neckline. Oba su valid, ali be consistent.

4. **Cheek line: tvoj izbor, documentiraj ga** – Ako definišeš cheeks, to je "groomed yeard". Ako ne, "purist yeard". Nema "wrong" answer.

5. **Split end controversy**: Hardcore purists kažu nema. Moderates dozvoljavaju <0.5 cm micro-dusting. Pick your philosophy early.

6. **Dokumentiraj journey** – Yeard je često photo-documented. Monthly progress pics are part of tradition.

7. **End date matters** – Know your start date. Dan 365 je celebration. Prije toga = nije yeard.

8. **Post-yeard decision**: Na dan 365, imaš tri opcije: (A) Continue to Bandholz, (B) Reshape to styled look, (C) Start again. Decide consciously.

## Face Shape Compatibility

| Oblik lica | Score | Obrazloženje |
|------------|-------|--------------|
| **Oval** | 85/100 | Generally works well. Natural growth complements balanced proportions. |
| **Square** | 85/100 | Good foundation for yeard. Strong jaw + natural beard = distinguished. |
| **Round** | 70/100 | Mixed results. Natural growth may add or reduce roundness depending on pattern. |
| **Triangle** | 80/100 | Usually works. Natural length adds balance to wide jaw. |
| **Diamond** | 75/100 | Depends on growth pattern. Results vary. |
| **Oblong** | 65/100 | Risky. Additional length on already long face may be unflattering. Can't control shape. |

**Note**: Yeard compatibility je inherently uncertain jer ne kontrolišeš outcome shape. Scores su based on typical outcomes.

**Best approach**: Commit to yeard knowing you'll reshape at end if needed. It's a journey, not final destination for most.

## Growth Timeline

**Yeard je timeline by definition – 12 exact months:**

### Month 1–3: Early Growth / "Awkward Phase Begins"
* **Month 1**:
  → Expected growth: 1–1.3 cm
  → Just started – barely visible progress
  → Start oil routine immediately
* **Month 2**:
  → Expected growth: +1–1.3 cm (total: 2–2.5 cm)
  → Awkward stubble-to-short beard transition
  → Itchiness common – oil helps
* **Month 3**:
  → Expected growth: +1–1.3 cm (total: 3–4 cm)
  → Peak awkward phase for most
  → Social pressure to trim may be high – resist

### Month 4–6: "The Hump" / Establishing Foundation
* **Month 4**:
  → Expected growth: +1–1.3 cm (total: 4.5–5.5 cm)
  → Starting to look intentional
  → Add beard balm to routine
* **Month 5**:
  → Expected growth: +1–1.3 cm (total: 5.5–7 cm)
  → Past the worst awkward phase
  → Neckline decision: define it or let it grow?
* **Month 6** (Halfway!):
  → Expected growth: +1–1.3 cm (total: 6.5–8 cm)
  → CELEBRATION: You're 50% there
  → Consider first split end assessment

### Month 7–9: Building Momentum
* **Month 7**:
  → Expected growth: +1–1.3 cm (total: 7.5–9.5 cm)
  → Noticeable length now
  → Increase oil quantity
* **Month 8**:
  → Expected growth: +1–1.3 cm (total: 8.5–11 cm)
  → Training beard direction sa brushing
  → Deep conditioning routine
* **Month 9** (Three quarters!):
  → Expected growth: +1–1.3 cm (total: 9.5–12 cm)
  → Approaching respectable length
  → Start thinking about post-yeard plans

### Month 10–12: Final Stretch
* **Month 10**:
  → Expected growth: +1–1.3 cm (total: 10.5–13.5 cm)
  → Home stretch – stay committed
  → Prepare for finale
* **Month 11**:
  → Expected growth: +1–1.3 cm (total: 11.5–15 cm)
  → One month to go
  → Photo documentation ramp-up
* **Month 12** (YEARD ACHIEVED!):
  → Final length: 12–16 cm typical
  → CELEBRATION DAY
  → Decision time: what's next?

**Total time**: Exactly 12 months by definition
**Expected length**: 12–16 cm (varies ±30% based on genetics)

## Transition Paths

### 1. Clean Shaven → Yeard
* **Duration**: Exactly 12 months
* **Difficulty**: Hard (requires extreme patience)
* **Key steps**:
  1. Day 1: Commit. Set calendar reminder for day 365.
  2. Month 1–3: Survive awkward phase. No trimming.
  3. Month 4–6: Establish grooming routine. Neckline decision.
  4. Month 7–9: Build length. Health focus.
  5. Month 10–12: Final push. Document journey.
  6. Day 365: Celebrate. Decide next step.

### 2. Full Beard (6 month growth) → Yeard
* **Duration**: 6 more months
* **Difficulty**: Medium (already have foundation)
* **Steps**:
  1. Stop all length trimming from today
  2. Record current date as "yeard midpoint"
  3. Continue for 6 more months
  4. Note: This is "6-month yeard conversion" – some purists don't count it

### 3. Yeard → Bandholz (Continuation)
* **Duration**: 6–12+ additional months
* **Difficulty**: Easy (just keep going)
* **Steps**:
  1. Day 366: Decide to continue
  2. Maintain health routine
  3. Continue no-trim philosophy
  4. At 20+ cm: Bandholz achieved

### 4. Yeard → Garibaldi (Reshaping)
* **Duration**: 1–2 sessions
* **Difficulty**: Easy (trimming down)
* **Steps**:
  1. Day 365+: Decision to reshape
  2. Shape rounded bottom contour
  3. Define width parameters (wider than current)
  4. Maintain 12–15 cm length
  5. Begin Garibaldi maintenance routine

### 5. Yeard → Ducktail (Reshaping)
* **Duration**: 1–2 sessions
* **Difficulty**: Medium
* **Steps**:
  1. Day 365+: Decision to reshape
  2. Form pointed bottom contour
  3. Taper sides
  4. Define cheek line
  5. May need to reduce length to 8–12 cm

### 6. Yeard → Clean Shaven (Full reset)
* **Duration**: 1 session
* **Difficulty**: Emotionally hard
* **Steps**:
  1. Day 365: Celebrate yeard achievement
  2. Shave completely for dramatic reveal
  3. Popular for charity events or transformations

| Kontekst | Suitable | Napomene |
|----------|----------|----------|
| **Professional/Corporate** | Varies by month | Month 1–3: Awkward. Month 4–6: Questionable. Month 7–12: Depends on industry. |

* **Optimal starting conditions**:
  * Flexible workplace policy
  * Supportive social circle
  * No major formal events in month 2–4
  * Good health and nutrition

* **Personality requirements**:
  * High patience
  * Commitment to long-term goals
  * Ability to handle comments/questions
  * Comfort with "unfinished" look during growth

* **Age note**: Any adult age works, but 25–45 often has best combination of growth rate and social flexibility

## Assumptions

* Prosječna stopa rasta: 1.1–1.3 cm/mjesečno (13–16 cm/year)
* Growth rate variance: ±30% from average is normal
* Terminal length: Not a concern for 12-month yeard; becomes factor in 18+ month grows
* Commitment level: Profil pretpostavlja full commitment; wavering mid-journey is common failure point
* Social support: Significant factor in success; isolated yeard attempts have higher failure rate
* Documentation: Encouraged for motivation; progress photos help maintain commitment
* "Cheating" disclosure: Profil acknowledges neckline/cheek choices – be honest with yourself about your version
